TITLE   :Turning Off Autoswitching on Paradise Plus Card
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0,3.0a
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

To turn off Autoswitching on a Paradise Plus card, try the following settings.

   FOR MULTIFREQUENCY MONITORS       FOR FIXED-FREQUENCY MONITORS
   --------------------------------------------------------------

   Dip Switch      Setting           Dip Switch    Setting
   ----------      -------           ----------    -------

      1            On                   1          Off
      2            Off                  2          On
      3            Off (always)         3          Off (always)
      4            Off                  4          Off
