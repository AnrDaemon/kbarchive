TITLE   :WD97: Additional Word Templates Available in ValuPack Folder
PRODUCT :Word 97 for Windows
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:97
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbualink97kbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Microsoft Office 97 and Microsoft Word 97 compact discs include templates
that are not installed when you run the Setup program. These templates are
located in the following folder on the Office 97 and Word 97 compact discs:

ValuPack\Template\Word

MORE INFORMATION
================

To use the templates, copy them from the compact disc to the Program
Files\Microsoft Office\Templates folder on your hard disk.

The following are the templates located in the ValuPack\Template\Word folder:

   Agenda Wizard
   Brochure.dot
   Calendar Wizard
   Contprel.dot (Contemporary Press Release)
   Elegprel.dot (Elegant Press Release)
   Profprel.dot (Professional Press Release)
   Directory.dot
   Inv_aus.dot (Invoice template for Australia)
   Inv_uk.dot (Invoice template for the United Kingdom)
   Invoice.dot
   Manual.dot
   Pur_aus.dot (Purchase Order for Australia)
   Pur_uk.dot (Purchase Order for the United Kingdom)
   Purchord.dot
   Thesis.dot
   Weektime.dot (weekly time sheet templates for the United States)
   Time_aus.dot (weekly time sheet templates for Austrialia)
   Time_uk.dot (weekly time sheet templates for the United Kingdom)

NOTE: Microsoft Word 97 includes a template, called More Templates and
Wizards.dot, that is installed when you perform a typical installation. This
template provides a summary of where to obtain additional templates.
