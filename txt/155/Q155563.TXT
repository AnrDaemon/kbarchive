TITLE   :Toshiba T4900CT Laptop Computer Hangs at NTDETECT Screen
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:winnt:4.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbhw kbHardwarekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 4.0 
 - Microsoft Windows NT Workstation version 4.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

The Toshiba T4900CT laptop computer may stop responding (hang) at the NTDETECT
screen.

RESOLUTION
==========

Upgrade the computer's BIOS to version 1.50 or later.
