SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book Small Business Solutions for Networking,
ISBN 0-7356-0685-4.

The following topics are covered:

 - Page 257: Delete Reference to Chapter 24

 - Page 438: Step #11 Is Missing

MORE INFORMATION
================

Page 257: Delete Reference to Chapter 24
----------------------------------------

On page 257, the reference to Chapter 24 can be deleted. This chapter does not
exist. Chapter 23 is the last chapter in the book.

Delete:
"24 - Sharing Modems and Internet Accounts"


Page 438: Step #11 Is Missing
-----------------------------

Step #11 is missing from page 438.

The numbered steps on page 438 skip from 10 to 12. Because no user action is
required between these two steps, the steps on this page should be renumbered 11
through 15.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
