SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book E-Commerce Strategies, ISBN 0-7356-0723-0.

The following topics are covered:

 - Table of Contents Corrections

MORE INFORMATION
================

Table of Contents Corrections
-----------------------------

On page vii change the "Introduction" page number from xii to xiii.

On page x, in the Chapter 12 subsection, the following corrections apply:

Change the "Taking Stock" page number from 267 to 266.
Change the "Action Plan" page number from 268 to 267.

Also on page x, change the "Index" page number from 327 to 325.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
