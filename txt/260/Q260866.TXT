DOCUMENT:Q260866  07-MAY-2002  [homegame]
TITLE   :FS2000: Gauges Display Incorrect Data in Instant Replay Mode
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 
 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you view a flight in Instant Replay or Flight Video mode, the gauges on the
instrument panel may not function correctly.

The gauges may display incorrect data, appear to move rapidly, or may not
function at all.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.

Additional query words: 1.00 msgame flightsim fs2k fltsim jerky jumpy
