TITLE   :MCM: Motorcycles and Riders Are Not Displayed Correctly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Motocross Madness on a Microsoft Windows 98-based computer,
motorcycles, riders, and stadium walls may not be displayed correctly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

 - The video adapter installed in your computer is based on the ViRGE/VX
   chipset.

 - The video driver for your video adapter is outdated.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for your
video adapter from the following S3 Web site:

   http://www.s3.com

MORE INFORMATION
================

To determine the brand and model of your video adapter:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click System.

3. Click Device Manager.

4. Double-click Display Adapters.

In the branch under Display Adapters, the brand and model of your video adapter
is displayed.

5. Close Control Panel.
