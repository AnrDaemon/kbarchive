TITLE   :AoE II: You Can Hear Sounds but Cannot See the Opening Movie
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, you may be unable to see the
opening movie. However, you may be able to hear the sounds in the opening movie.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A Hercules Terminator Beast video adapter is installed in your computer.

 - An outdated video driver for your Hercules Terminator Beast video adapter is
   installed on your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest version of the Hercules
Terminator Beast video driver from the following Hercules Web site:

   http://www.hercules.com
