TITLE   :Virtual Globe: Statistics Are Not Listed in Alphabetical Order
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbtool kbui kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta Virtual Globe 99, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The statistics that are available in the World Compare feature of Encarta
Virtual Globe 99 are not listed in alphabetical order.

MORE INFORMATION
================

This behavior is by design.

The statistics that are available in the World Compare feature of Encarta Virtual
Globe 99 are sorted by their internal article ID number instead of by
alphabetical order.
