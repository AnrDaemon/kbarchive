TITLE   :How To Save Or Reset My Place When I Exit Encarta

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta 1994 The Complete Multimedia Encyclopedia 
 - Microsoft Encarta 95 The Complete Interactive Multimedia Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
 - Microsoft Encarta 96 Encyclopedia for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you exit Encarta, it is possible to save or reset your place for the next
time you use the program. To do this, you can select or clear the Save My Place
option in Encarta.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Encarta 1995 or 1996
--------------------

1. Start Encarta, then click Find (in Encarta 1996) or Enter (in Encarta 1995)
   to enter Encarta.

2. Click the circled "i" on the menu bar, then click Options (in Encarta 1996)
   or Settings (in Encarta 1995).

3. Click the "Save my place when I quit Encarta" box, to save your place, or
   clear this option to reset your place each time you start Encarta.

4. Click OK.

Encarta 1994
------------

1. Start Encarta, then click Enter to enter Encarta.

2. Click Menu, then click Options.

3. In the "Save current layout when I exit Encarta" area, click Yes, to Save
   your place, or No, to reset your place each time you start Encarta.

4. Click OK.
