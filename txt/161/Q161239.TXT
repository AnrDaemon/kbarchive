TITLE   :Chips & Technologies 65550 Refresh Rate Changes after Restart
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER::2,2.1
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:osr2 win95

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows 95 OEM Service Release, versions 2, 2.1 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you change the refresh rate settings for your monitor on a computer using a
Chips & Technologies 65550 video chip set, the settings may change back to
the default settings when you restart the computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this problem, follow these steps:

1. In Device Manager, double-click the Display Adapters branch to expand it.

2. Double-click the listed display adapter.

3. Click the Resources tab, and then click the Use Automatic Settings check box
   to clear it.

4. Click OK, and then click OK.

5. When you are prompted to restart your computer, do so.
