TITLE   :Mac Wkst: Shiva Config Init Version 3.4 and 3.5 Not Compatible
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For Appletalk Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks, version 3.1 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Shiva modem Configuration init versions 3.4 and 3.5 are not compatible with
version 3.1 of Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks. Users must upgrade to
Shiva configuration Utility 3.6.6 or higher.

MORE INFORMATION
================

In addition version 3.6.6 and Mail may not be compatible on system version 6.07
or earlier.

Contact Shiva Corporation for information regarding upgrades.
