TITLE   :PC Setup: Mail 3.0 Directory Entries in NetWare
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For PC Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for PC Networks, version 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

An initial installation of Mail for PC Networks on a Novell NetWare server
requires 95 directory entries for the Mail executable files, which in turn
requires 6,492,165 bytes.

The Mail database requires approximately 210 directory entries, which in turn
requires 1,129,644 bytes.

You can use these numbers for planning during a NetWare installation.

Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's
performance or reliability.
