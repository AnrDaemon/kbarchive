TITLE   :PC Win: Renaming Personal Groups
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For PC Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0,3.0b,3.2
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for Windows, versions 3.0, 3.0b, 3.2 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

You cannot rename personal groups in Mail. The following is a simple
workaround:

1. Choose Personal Groups from the Mail menu.

2. Select your personal group and choose Edit.

3. Select all the names under Group Members and press CTRL+INSERT.

4. Choose Cancel.

5. Choose New and enter the new group name.

6. Click the Group Members list and then press SHIFT+INSERT.

7. Choose OK.

You can now delete the old group.
