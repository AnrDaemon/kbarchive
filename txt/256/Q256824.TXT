TITLE   :MapPoint 2001: Driving Directions Are Not Printed Properly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Automap
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbprint kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft MapPoint 2002 
 - Microsoft MapPoint 2001 
 - Microsoft Streets & Trips 2002, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Streets and Trips 2001 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to print driving directions from one of the programs that are
listed at the beginning of this article, the directions may not be printed
properly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

 - Your printer is configured to print in Landscape mode.

 - You attempt to print driving directions on paper that is more than 11 inches
   wide.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, use one of the following methods.

 - Configure your printer to print in Portrait mode.

 - Print the driving directions on standard letter-sized (8 1/2 x 11 inches)
   paper.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed that this is a problem in the Microsoft products that
are listed at the beginning of this article.
