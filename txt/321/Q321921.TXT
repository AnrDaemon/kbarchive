SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book MCSA Readiness Review Managing A Microsoft
Windows 2000 Network Environment Exam 70-218, ISBN 0-7356-1636-1.

The following topics are covered:

 - Page 95: Incorrect Switch For NBTSTAT

 - Pages 95 And 99: -r Should Be -R

 - Page 100: -RR Should Be -R

 - Page 182: Correction To Question 70-218.04.04.002's Answers

MORE INFORMATION
================

Page 95: Incorrect Switch For NBTSTAT
-------------------------------------

On page 95, in the first paragraph,

Change:
"You run NBTSTAT -a on ..."

To:
"You run NBTSTAT -c on ..."


Pages 95 And 99:  -r Should Be -R
---------------------------------

On pages 95 and 99, under question 70-218.02.05.003, in answer D,

Change:
"NBTSTAT -r"

To:
"NBTSTAT -R"


Page 100: -RR Should Be -R
--------------------------

On page 100, in question 70-218.02.05.005, in answer D,

Change:
"NBTSTAT -RR"

To:
"NBTSTAT -R"


Page 182: Correction To Question 70-218.04.04.002's Answers
-----------------------------------------------------------

On page 182, under question 70-218.04.04.002, the correct answer should be A, E,
and G, not A, C, E, and G.

Change:
"Correct Answers: A, C, E, and G
...
C. Correct: Outlook is published to computers at the OU=Engineering,
OU=Workstations OU and appears in Add/Remove Programs in Control Panel."

To:
"Correct Answers: A, E, and G
...
C. Incorrect: An application can only be published to users.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
