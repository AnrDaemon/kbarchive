DOCUMENT:Q248761  09-NOV-2001  [homegame]
TITLE   :Fighter Ace II: Engine Is Not Numbered Correctly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you select an engine in Microsoft Fighter Ace II, the engine may not be
numbered correctly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur because Fighter Ace II does not number engines in the
following (correct) sequence.

+-------------------------+
| Engine | Location       | 
+-------------------------+
| #1     | Outboard-left  | 
+-------------------------+
| #2     | Inboard-left   | 
+-------------------------+
| #3     | Inboard-right  | 
+-------------------------+
| #4     | Outboard-right | 
+-------------------------+

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, use the following sequence when you select engines in
Fighter Ace II.

+-------------------------+
| Engine | Location       | 
+-------------------------+
| #1     | Inboard-left   | 
+-------------------------+
| #2     | Inboard-right  | 
+-------------------------+
| #3     | Outboard-left  | 
+-------------------------+
| #4     | Outboard-right | 
+-------------------------+

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Fighter Ace 2.0 for
MSN Gaming Zone.
