TITLE   :Fighter Ace II: Direct 3D and Glide Options Are Unavailable
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay igz kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Fighter Ace II, the Direct 3D and Glide display options
may be unavailable.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A video adapter based on the 3Dfx Voodoo chip set is installed in your
   computer.

 - The video driver installed on your computer is outdated.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the 3Dfx
Voodoo Graphics chip set from the following 3Dfx Web site:

   http://www.3dfxgamers.com/view.asp?PAGE=VGDrivers
