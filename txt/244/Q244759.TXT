SUMMARY
=======

When making the transition from WordPerfect, it helps to understand the
different functionality in Word that will help get your job done without "reveal
codes."

This Word 97 document reviews the formatting tools used in Microsoft Word and
discusses features that help WordPerfect users do what they used to do with
"reveal codes."

The following file is available for download from the Microsoft Download Center:

   Revcodes.exe
   (http://download.microsoft.com/download/word97win/white1/1/WIN98/EN-US/Revcodes.exe)

Release Date: Feb-13-1997
