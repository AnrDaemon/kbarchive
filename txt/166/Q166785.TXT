TITLE   :WD97: Cannot Undo Resizing of ActiveX Controls
PRODUCT :Word 97 for Windows
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbtool word8 kbwordvba winword word97

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you resize an ActiveX control (for example, a command button) added to a
document using the Control Toolbox toolbar, and you then choose to undo the
action of resizing the control, nothing happens.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products listed at
the beginning of this article.
