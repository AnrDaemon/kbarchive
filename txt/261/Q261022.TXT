TITLE   :Motocross Madness 2: Mouse Pointer Is Not Redrawn Properly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, the mouse pointer may not be
redrawn properly on certain screens in the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a Creative Labs 3D Blaster RIVA TNT2 Ultra video
adapter is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest version of the video
driver for your video adapter from the following Creative Labs Web site:

   http://www.creative.com

MORE INFORMATION
================

To determine the brand and model of your video adapter:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click System.

3. Click Device Manager.

4. Double-click Display Adapters.

In the branch under Display Adapters, the brand and model of your video adapter
is displayed.

5. Close Control Panel.
