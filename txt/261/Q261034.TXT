TITLE   :MCM2: Distorted Textures When You Start Race or Encounter Snow
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbhw kbimu kbHardwarekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start a race in Microsoft Motocross Madness 2 or you encounter snow in
the Enduro races, textures in the game may be distorted.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a video adapter that is based on the 3Dfx Voodoo2 SLI
chip set is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest reference driver for the
3Dfx Voodoo2 SLI chip set from the following 3Dfx Web site:

   http://www.3dfx.com

MORE INFORMATION
================

To determine the brand and model of your video adapter:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click System.

3. Click Device Manager.

4. Double-click Display Adapters.

In the branch under Display Adapters, the brand and model of your video adapter
is displayed.

5. Close Control Panel.
