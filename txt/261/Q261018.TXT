DOCUMENT:Q261018  12-JUN-2001  [homegame]
TITLE   :MCM2: Sounds Are Played If You Turn Off Sound Effects in Game
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbsound kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, some sound effects may be played if
you click to clear the "In-game Sound Effects" check box on the Sound tab of the
Options screen.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A Creative Labs Sound Blaster AWE64 sound card is installed in your computer.

 - The sound driver for your sound card is outdated.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest sound driver for your
sound card from the following Creative Technology Web site:

   http://www.soundblaster.com
