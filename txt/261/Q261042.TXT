TITLE   :MCM2: Textures and Colors on the Ground Are Distorted
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbgraphic kbhw kbimu kbHardware kbgraphxlinkcritical

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, textures and colors on the ground
may be distorted.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

 - A Creative Labs 3D Blaster Savage 4 video adapter is installed in your
   computer.

 - The video adapter is configured to use a screen resolution higher than 1280 X
   1024 in the game.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, configure your video adapter to use a lower display
resolution in the game or download and install the latest 3D Blaster Savage 4
video driver from the following Creative Labs Web site:

   http://www.creativelabs.com

To configure your video adapter to use a lower screen resolution in the game:

1. Start Motocross Madness 2, and then click Options ([GRAPHIC:the three
   gears]).

2. Click the Graphics tab.

3. In the Display Resolution box, click a resolution that is lower than 1600 X
   1200.

4. Click Back ([GRAPHIC:the pointing hand]) until you return to the startup
   screen.
