TITLE   :MCM2: Game Stops Responding When You Select Motorcycle or Rider
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you click the left or right arrow to select a motorcycle or rider on the
Bike & Riders screen in Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, you may experience
either of the following symptoms:

 - Motocross Madness 2 may stop responding (hang).

 - Motocross Madness 2 may quit unexpectedly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a video adapter that is based on the Intel I740
graphics accelerator chip set is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this issue, click the text description of the motorcycle or rider
to display a list of the available motorcycles and riders, and then click the
motorcycle or rider that you want to use.
