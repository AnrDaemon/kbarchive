TITLE   :Motocross Madness 2: Text in Game Flickers or Does Not Appear
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Microsoft Motocross Madness 2, the text in the game may flicker
or may not appear.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - You have already started Motocross Madness 2 at least once without restarting
   your computer.

 - A 3Dfx Voodoo 3 2000 video adapter is installed in your computer.

 - The video driver for the video adapter is outdated.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the 3Dfx
Voodoo 3 2000 video adapter from the following 3Dfx Web site:

   http://www.3dfx.com
