TITLE   :Flight Simulator Coordinates for Fuel Bases in WWI Mode
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
SUMMARY
=======

The coordinates for the fuel bases in World War I mode for Flight Simulator
Versions 3.00 and 4.00 are as follows:

Friendly Base 1
---------------

   North:    17417
   East:     7445
   Altitude: 413

Friendly Base 2
---------------

   North:    17404
   East:     7432
   Altitude: 416

Additional query words: WWI
