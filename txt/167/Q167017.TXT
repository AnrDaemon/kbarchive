DOCUMENT:Q167017  07-SEP-2001  [winnt]
TITLE   :Lexmark Optra L and L Plus Printers Incorrectly Show Middle Tray
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:4.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbprintkbbuglist kbfixlist

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Workstation version 4.0 
 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 4.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

When you set up the tray options for the Lexmark Optra L and L Plus Series
printers under Windows NT version 4.0, you are presented with a Middle Tray
option. A middle tray does not exist on these two models of the Optra. A middle
tray does exist on the R Series Optra printer.

STATUS
======

Support for middle tray has been removed for the L and L Plus series Optra
printers that use Optra.dll driver is included in the latest Windows NT version
4.0 U.S. Service Pack. For information on obtaining the Service Pack, query on
the following word in the Microsoft Knowledge Base (without the spaces):

   S E R V P A C K

Additional query words: 4.00 prodnt nt4sp3fea
