OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdta winword word97

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SUMMARY
=======

This article describes how to remove the separator from a footnote or endnote.

MORE INFORMATION
================

1. On the View menu, click Normal.

2. On the View menu, click Footnotes.

3. In the Footnotes pane, click the down arrow next to Footnotes or Endnotes,
   and select Footnote Separator or Endnote Separator. The separator appears.

4. Select the separator and delete it.
