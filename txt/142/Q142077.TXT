SUMMARY
=======

The following article tells how to read specific movie reviews in Cinemania for
the Macintosh.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To read specific movie reviews do the following:

Cinemania 97 and Cinemania 96
-----------------------------

1. Open Cinemania, and then click Start.

2. Click Find at the top of the screen

3. Select a movie from the list (i.e., for example, click 12 Angry Men).

4. Click any reviews listed at top of page (i.e. Maltin Review or Kael Review).

Cinemania 95
------------

1. Open Cinemania, and then click Dive Right In!

2. Select a movie from the list (i.e., 12 Angry Men).

3. Click the "Reviewed By" option.

4. On the critic list select Kael, and then Click OK.

   Note: The critic name, Kael, will appear next to the "Reviewed By" option.

5. Click the "Go To Subject" option, located at the bottom of the movie list; an
   image appears to the right.

6. Click the small box on the top left corner of the Find window.

   NOTE: The window titled "Microsoft" will now be visible.

7. Click the Kael button. Kael's review of the "12 Angry Men" appears.

8. To return to the Find window, click Find.
