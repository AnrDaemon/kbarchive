TITLE   :Encarta Encyclopedia 99: Suite Menu Disabled on Home Screen
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbui kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia 99 
 - Microsoft Encarta Reference Suite 99 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you view a Home screen in Encarta Encyclopedia 99 or Encarta Reference
Suite 99, the commands on the Suite menu are unavailable.

NOTE: This does not occur in Encarta Reference Suite 99 DVD-ROM.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this issue, disable the Home screen. To do this, follow these
steps:

1. Start Encarta Encyclopedia 99.

2. On the Options menu, click Settings.

3. Click to clear the "Display Home screen when I start Encarta Encyclopedia"
   check box, and then click OK.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design.
