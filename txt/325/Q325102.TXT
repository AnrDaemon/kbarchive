TITLE   :Train Simulator: Train Bell Produces No Sound
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Train Simulator and click on the bell, the bell produces no sound.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior occurs if the QUANTITY OF SOUNDS option is set to LOW.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, follow these steps:

1. Click OPTIONS.

2. Click SOUND.

3. Under VOLUMES, move the QUANTITY OF SOUNDS slider to MEDIUM or HIGH.

4. Click SAVE.
