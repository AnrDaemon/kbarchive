TITLE   :Baseball 2001: Background Scenery in the Game Flickers
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Baseball 2001 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Baseball 2001, you may experience one or more of the
following symptoms:

 - The background scenery in the game may flicker.

 - Horizontal lines may appear and disappear on the screen at random intervals.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a video adapter based on the nVIDIA GeForce 256 chip
set is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this issue, configure Baseball 2001 to run in Software mode:

1. Start Baseball 2001.

2. On the Home Plate screen, click Options.

3. Click the Graphics tab.

4. Under Mode, click Software, and then click OK.
