TITLE   :FS98: How to Register Online After Program Is Installed
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbsetup kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 98 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you install Microsoft Flight Simulator 98, you receive the following
message when Setup is complete:

   If you have a modem and web browser configured you can register your new
   software electronically. Do you want to register online now?

If you click No, but decide you want to register Flight Simulator online at a
later time, you are unable to do so.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To register Flight Simulator online after you have installed the program, you
need to remove and reinstall Flight Simulator. To do so, follow these steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.

3. On the Install/Uninstall tab, click Microsoft Flight Simulator 98, and then
   click Add/Remove.

4. When you are prompted to confirm the removal of Flight Simulator 98, click
   OK.

5. Click OK until you return to Control Panel.

6. Close Control Panel.

7. Delete the folder where Flight Simulator 98 is installed. By default, Flight
   Simulator 98 is installed in the following folder:

   C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Flight Simulator

   For information about how to delete folders, click Start, and then click Help.
   On the Index tab, type "deleting" (without the quotation marks), and then
   double-click the "Deleting files or folders" topic.

8. Insert the Flight Simulator 98 compact disc into the CD-ROM drive. If Auto
   Insert Notification is enabled, Setup begins automatically. Follow the
   instructions on the screen.

   NOTE: If Setup does not begin automatically, click Start, click Run, type the
   following line in the Open box, and then click OK

   <drive>:\setup.exe

   where <drive> is the letter of the CD-ROM drive.

9. When you are prompted to register online now, click Yes.
