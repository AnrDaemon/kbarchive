TITLE   :FS98: Cannot Resize Radio Stack in Boeing 737-400
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbgraphic fsim fs98 kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 98 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you resize the radio stack panel for the Boeing 737-400 in Flight Simulator
98 to increase its width, you may be unable to further resize the radio stack
panel.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if you stretch the width of the the radio stack panel to
478 pixels or more.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, follow these steps to reset the size of the radio stack
panel:

1. On the Aircraft menu, click Select Aircraft.

2. In the Aircraft box, click a different aircraft, and then click OK.

3. On the Aircraft menu, click Select Aircraft.

4. In the Aircraft box, click Boeing 737-400, and then click OK.

5. On the aircraft control panel, click the Master Avionics Switch to display
   the radio stack panel in its original size.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Flight Simulator 98.
