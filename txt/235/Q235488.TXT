DOCUMENT:Q235488  11-JUL-1999  [exchange]
TITLE   :XFOR: Vcards Not Received Correctly by the GroupWise Connector
PRODUCT :Microsoft Exchange
PROD/VER:winnt:5.5
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:exc55

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Exchange Server, version 5.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

When you send Vcard attachments to a GroupWise user through the Microsoft
Connector for Novell GroupWise, they are not handled correctly by most GroupWise
systems. The only GroupWise system that supports Vcards is GroupWise 5.5 with
Service Pack 1.
