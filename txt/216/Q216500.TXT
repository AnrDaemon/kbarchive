TITLE   :Encarta 99: Blank Box Displayed When You Play A Video Clip
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbenv kberrmsg kbtool kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia 99 
 - Microsoft Encarta Reference Suite 99 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to play a video clip in Microsoft Encarta 99, you may see a
blank box that contains an error message similar to the following:

   Video not available.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the Microsoft Windows Media Player is missing or
damaged.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, remove and reinstall Windows Media Player. To do this,
follow these steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Add/Remove Programs, and then click the Windows Setup tab.

3. Under Components, click to clear the Multimedia check box, and then click
   Apply.

4. Click to select the Multimedia check box, and then click OK.

5. Close Control Panel.

If this issue continues to occur, download and install the latest Indeo codecs
from the following Intel Web site:

   http://www.intel.com
