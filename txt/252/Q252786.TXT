TITLE   :Allegiance: Unable to Cancel Technology Research
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbimu msgame kbFAQ

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you invest in technology research in Microsoft Allegiance, you cannot
cancel the research and receive a refund. Why?

CAUSE
=====

You cannot cancel technology research in Allegiance.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To prevent this issue from occurring, only invest in technologies that you
absolutely want or need.
