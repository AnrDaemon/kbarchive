TITLE   :Okidata 24-Pin Driver Lacks Accelerators
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0,3.0a
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

The Okidata 24-pin driver included with Microsoft Windows 3.0 lacks accelerator
keys for the graphics resolution options.

WORKAROUND
==========

To work around this problem, press TAB to move to the Graphics Resolution group.

STATUS
======

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
becomes available.

Additional query words: 3.00 3.00a win30
