 - Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows 1995 edition

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows, 1995 Intro edition is a special version of
Bookshelf available on MSN, The Microsoft Network. The standalone version of
Bookshelf 1995 Intro Edition on Compact Disc has additional features which
require the high-bandwidth of a multimedia computer and CD-ROM drive.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The standalone and Intro versions of Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows go beyond
printed reference books, such as the thesaurus in your word processor, and other
online reference products. Both products provide access to:

 - Seven best-selling reference books

 - Nearly 3,000 images

 - More than 80,000 pronunciations

 - 180 National Anthems

The standalone edition of Microsoft Bookshelf provides these extra features:

 - QuickShelf toolbar: One-click access to eight best selling reference books
   from within any program. Use the QuickShelf Toolbar from within your word
   processor, electronic mail, presentation package, or database.

 - United States ZIP Codes

 - 45 Video Clips

 - 50 Animation

 - Five Hours of Sound

 - Year In Review

 - Automatic Footnotes
