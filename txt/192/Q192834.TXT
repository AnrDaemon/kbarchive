-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft My Personal Tutor: Sam's Hide and Seek Adventure, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft My Personal Tutor: Sky's Space Station Voyage, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft My Personal Tutor: Turru's Daring Sea Quest, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the minimum system requirements needed to run the following
Microsoft My Personal Tutor programs:

 - Sky's Space Station Voyage

 - Turru's Daring Sea Quest

 - Sam's Hide and Seek Adventure

MORE INFORMATION
================

To run the Microsoft programs listed in the Applies To section of this article,
you need:

 - A Multimedia PC with a Pentium or faster microprocessor.

 - Microsoft Windows 95 or later, or Microsoft Windows NT, version 4.0 or later.

 - 16 megabytes (MB) of random access memory (RAM).

 - 17 MB of available hard-disk space for the first My Personal Tutor 1st &
   2nd Grade program, and an additional 10 MB of available hard-disk space for
   each additional program.

 - An additional 10 to 15 MB of available hard-disk space to use as a swap file
   when you run the program.

 - A quad-speed (4X) or faster CD-ROM drive.

 - A PCI display adapter and Super VGA monitor capable of supporting 256 colors
   at 640 X 480 resolution.

 - A Microsoft mouse or compatible pointing device.

 - A 16-bit Windows-compatible sound card with speakers or headphones.
