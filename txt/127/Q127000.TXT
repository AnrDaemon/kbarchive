The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
 - Microsoft Windows for Workgroups versions 3.1, 3.11

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the settings for the Hewlett-Packard (HP) DeskJet series
printer driver (DESKJETC.DRV) versions 3.0 and 3.1, and the equivalent settings
for versions 4.x and 5.0.

MORE INFORMATION
================

-------------------------------------------------------------------

mode

                                   Halftoning

Black or Minimize Black            the following Print Quality
and Color Bleed                    settings:
                                   Presentation mode, media-
                                   dependent (v4.0/v4.1/v5.00)
