======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

This article lists the plotters supported by the CalComp Plotter Driver for
Microsoft Windows.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Plotter family name            Model number
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   Artisan                        1023, 1025, 1026
   Desktop ColorMaster +          6603VRC, 6613VRC
   ColorView                      5912, 5913
   Drawing Master                 52224, 52236, 52424, 52436
   Electrostatic Plotters         5725, 5735, 5745,
                                  5825, 5835, 5845,
                                  57424, 57436, 57444,
                                  58424, 58434, 58444,
                                  67436, 68436, 68444
   PaceSetter                     2036, 2074
   Color Printer - PlotMaster     5902A, 5902G
   Pen Plotters                   1040, 1041, 1042,
                                  1043, 1044, 1075,
                                  1076, 1077
   DesignMate                     3024

You can download this driver from the CalComp bulletin board service (BBS). For
more information, call CalComp Technical Support.
