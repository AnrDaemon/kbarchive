TITLE   :Baseball '95: Houston Astros Invalid Opposing Team Reference
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Baseball Guide for Windows, 1995 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Houston Astros July 9, 1995 opponent is the San Diego Padres. The Baseball
Guide schedule incorrectly abbreviates the team name as PAD. The correct
abbreviation is SD.

The abbreviation is correct on all other schedules.
