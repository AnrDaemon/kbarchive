TITLE   :Baseball 95:  How to Change Daily Service Phone Access Numbers
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Baseball Guide for Windows, 1995 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you try to connect with Baseball Daily service, you may receive a message
stating the access phone number has been changed. To continue to use Baseball
Daily, you must change your access phone number.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Access numbers are listed when you register for Baseball Daily service. From the
contents screen of Complete Baseball, click the Baseball Daily button to access
the service.

Registration for Baseball Daily is a two part process. The first part records
general registration data and billing information, such as name, address, and
credit card number. The second part assigns modem configurations and access
numbers.

To change your access number, use one of the following methods.

 - From the Phone Prefix dialog box in step 2 of the registration setup process,
   choose Custom. Choose the Set Custom Prefix button. Type the prefix including
   area code and phone number.

   -or-

 - Add the following line to the MSSPORTS.INI file located in the Windows
   directory:

        [BS95OL]
        PhoneNumber=##########

   For more information about how to perform this task in Windows, see your
   Windows printed documentation or online Help.
