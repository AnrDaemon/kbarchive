TITLE   :Matrox Millennium PCI Video Card Causes STOP 0X0000000A
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:winnt:4.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbhw kbHardware

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Workstation version 4.0 
 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 4.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Computers using the Matrox Millennium PCI video card may experience an
intermittent STOP 0X0000000A error.


RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this problem, do either of the following:

 - Change the driver to VGA Compatible Display Adapter at 640 x 480 resolution.

   -or-

 - Obtain new Windows NT driver files and card BIOS from Matrox. The Matrox Web
   site is:

   http://www.matrox.com/
