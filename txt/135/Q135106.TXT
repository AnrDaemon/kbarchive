TITLE   :Changing Billing Address Does Not Change Address Book
PRODUCT :The Microsoft Network
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.2,1.3,2.0,2.5,95
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbui kbmsn

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - The Microsoft Network versions 1.2, 1.3, 2.0, 2.5 
 - the operating system: Microsoft Windows 95 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you change your billing name or address, the change is not automatically
applied to the address book for MSN, The Microsoft Network e-mail. You need to
manually change your information in the address book.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For information about how to change your billing information, click the Index
tab in The Microsoft Network online Help, type the following text

   "billing" (without the quotation marks)

and then double-click the "changing your billing information" topic.

In MSN version 2.0 or higher, click the Member Services button (?), and then
click "Check or Change Account."

For information about changing your information in the address book, click the
Index tab in The Microsoft Network online Help, type the following text

   "address book" (without the quotation marks)

and then double-click the "changing your member information" topic.

In MSN version 2.0 or higher, click the Member Services button, click "Get
Answers, Support and Help," click the Search tab, type the following text

   "address book" (without the quotation marks)

and then double-click the "changing MSN address book information" topic.
