TITLE   :Cinemania 96: Unable to Export Contents of April Update
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1996 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1996 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you create a list in the List Maker that contains movies from the April 96
Cinemania update, you are unable to export the complete list. The exported list
is truncated at the first entry for a movie from the April 96 update.

WORKAROUND
==========

Remove from your list any movies from the April 96 Cinemania update. To remove
the April movies, do the following:

1. In the List Maker, select the movie(s) from the April 96 update, and then
   click Delete From List.

2. Create a new list that includes the movies from the April 96 update.

You are now able to export the original list from the List Maker.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The newly created list containing only movies from the April 96 update can be
printed from the List Maker but cannot be exported.

For a complete list of movies included in the April 96 update, please see the
following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q150561 Cinemania 96 Update Contents: April 96
