TITLE   :Bookshelf '96-'97: No Online Registration in Windows 3.1x
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf 1996-97 for Windows 
 - the operating system: Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you click the Subscription and Registration button in Microsoft Bookshelf
'96-'97, the Subscription and Registration screen states:

   To register:

   by modem, click Subscribe and Register Online and follow the instructions for
   registration only.

However, the Subscribe and Register Online button does not exist.

CAUSE
=====

The Subscribe and Register Online feature is not available in the 16-bit version
of Bookshelf. This is the version installed on computers running Windows 3.x.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Bookshelf '96-'97 for
Windows. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in
the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The Subscribe and Register Online button is available when Bookshelf is
installed on computers running Microsoft Windows 95.
