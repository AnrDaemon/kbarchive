SUMMARY
=======

This article describes how to obtain information on participating in the
Microsoft Component Builders Program.

If you create Microsoft FoxPro add-ons, OLE Controls, toolkits, or any other
product that works with Microsoft tools, be a part of the Microsoft Component
Builders Program. Our program offers marketing opportunities and Information to
vendors of add-on products for Microsoft development tools.

Participating in the Microsoft Component Builders Program means having access to
registered developers and receiving important information on new products and
technologies before it becomes news, giving you a jump on developing your
products. Most important, you will have the support of Microsoft's expertise
behind your products.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To learn more about the Component Builder Program, please send your company
name, contact information, address, and product information to:
cbisv@microsoft.com
