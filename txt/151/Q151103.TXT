TITLE   :Cinemania 96: April Update Does Not Print Descriptions
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1996 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1996 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

In Cinemania List Maker, when movies discussed in the April 96 Cinemania update,
only the movie title and year print.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Cinemania for Windows,
1996 edition. We are researching this problem and will post new information here
in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

There is currently no resolution to this problem.

MORE INFORMATION
================

This behavior occurs only in the April 96 update. The behavior has been
corrected and has been verified as fixed in the May 96 update. The problem still
occurs in the April update.
