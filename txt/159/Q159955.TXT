SUMMARY
=======

The following article summarizes the differences between the Deluxe and Standard
versions of the 1997 edition of Encarta Encyclopedia.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Encarta 97 Encyclopedia comes in two versions, Standard and Deluxe. The Deluxe
Edition is so filled with content that it requires two disks. It contains twice
as many images, sounds, and videos as the Standard edition. The Deluxe edition
also comes with a full-size User's Manual.

The Deluxe Edition also includes 20 Collages. The Media Gallery contains
360-degree views with Surround Video, a new multimedia feature for exploring
topics. The Atlas in the Deluxe edition includes a Sights and Sounds option.

Owners of the Deluxe edition receive their Yearbook Updates and Web links for
free. The Deluxe edition also includes a Browsing For Fun feature and an
additional Interactivity: Ellis Island.
