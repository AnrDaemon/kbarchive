TITLE   :Windows 3.0 Drivers for Xerox Panorama-IME 2-Page Display

KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

A Microsoft Windows version 3.0 compatible display driver is available for the
Xerox Panorama-IME 2-page display from IME Computers. This driver ONLY works in
real and standard modes.

The display driver's disk from IME, labeled Xerox Panorama Windows 3.0 Driver
Disk, contains two Xerox Panorama drivers. Select the appropriate driver for
your hardware configuration.

MORE INFORMATION
================

These drivers allow you to use the Xerox Panorama display in the high-resolution
modes, dual-page at 1216 x 925, and MDA or HGA at 1080 x 696. They also uses
extra system memory.

To obtain these drivers, please contact IME Computers.

The IME products included here are manufactured by a vendor independent of
Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding these products'
performance or reliability.
