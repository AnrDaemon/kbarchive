TITLE   :Using CorelDRAW! with a Plotter Prints One Copy Only
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0,3.0a
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When printing from CorelDRAW! version 2.00, the print option "Number of Copies"
is dimmed and unavailable when the active printer is the Hewlett-Packard (HP)
Plotter driver. This does not affect other Windows applications, such as Word
for Windows or Excel.

For further information, please contact Corel Corporation.

or reliability.
