TITLE   :Office 97 Uninstall Program Does Not Clean Registry
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:winnt:4.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 4.0, Terminal Server Edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you uninstall Microsoft Office 97 for Windows, the uninstall program may
not remove all Office-related keys from the registry.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For additional information about completely uninstalling Office 97, see the
following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q158658 OFF97: How to Completely Remove Microsoft Office 97

Additional query words: setup
