TITLE   :ADT/ODE: Final Help Workshop Training Card Does Not Close
PRODUCT :Microsoft Access Distribution Kit
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:7.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbusage

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Office 97 Developer Edition 
 - Microsoft Access Developer's Toolkit, version 7.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Moderate: Requires basic macro, coding, and interoperability skills.

SYMPTOMS
========

When you use the Training Cards on the Help Menu for assistance in creating a
project in the Help Workshop, when you get to the last of these cards, the Close
button does not dismiss the final Training Card.

RESOLUTION
==========

The only way to dismiss the final Training Card for Creating A Project is to
close it manually by clicking the Close button in the upper-right corner of the
window or by pressing ALT+F4 on the keyboard.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Office Developer
Edition 97 and Microsoft Access Developer's Toolkit, version 7.0.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Steps to Reproduce Problem
--------------------------

1. Open the Microsoft Help Workshop from ODE Tools (or ADT for version 7.0).

2. Click Training Cards on the Help Menu, and then select Creating A Project.

3. Follow the instructions on the Training Cards, until you get to the last one
   that begins with "Done!..." and contains a Close button.

4. Click the Close button. Note that the Training Card does not close as
   expected.
