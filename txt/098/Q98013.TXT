TITLE   :PC WRmt: Unsendable Messages Counted Incorrectly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For PC Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.2
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail Remote for Windows, version 3.2 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

If there are unsendable messages waiting in the Outbox for version 3.2 of
Microsoft Mail Remote for Windows, when you choose Connect Now from the View
menu, the messages are counted as messages waiting to be sent, even though they
are not waiting to be sent.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in version 3.2 of Microsoft Mail
Remote for Windows. We are researching this problem and will post new
information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
