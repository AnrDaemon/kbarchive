DOCUMENT:Q234383  08-MAR-2000  [exchange]
TITLE   :XCON: GroupWise Router Doesn't Stop w. Connectivity Controller
PRODUCT :Microsoft Exchange
PROD/VER:winnt:5.5
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:exc55

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Exchange Server, version 5.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

When you stop the Exchange Server Connectivity Controller service, the router
for the Novell GroupWise service does not stop running, even though a dialog box
is displayed that says that the Exchange Connector for Novell GroupWise will be
shut down.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Exchange Server
version 5.5.
