SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft Access 2000 At a Glance, ISBN
1-57231-946-1.

The following topics are covered:

 - Pages 8, 30 and 40: Need to show new dialog box tab for Office 97 Templates

 - Page 174: Tip refers to wrong Office program

 - Page 218: Step 2, additional instructions on "exclusive mode"

MORE INFORMATION
================

Pages 8, 30 and 40: Need to show new dialog box tab for Office 97 Templates
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Need to replace screen shots to show the new tab, Office 97 Templates.


Page 174: Tip refers to wrong Office program
--------------------------------------------

In the marginal tip replace "Excel" in 4th line of tip with "Access"

Add the following "See Also" marginal note:
See Also
See "Installing Additional Features" on page 36 for information about installing
mapping features.


Page 218: Step 2, additional instructions on "exclusive mode"
-------------------------------------------------------------

In step 2, add instructions for opening the database in exclusive mode.
Add the following to step 2:
In the Open dialog box, click the Open button drop-down arrow, and then click
Open Exclusive.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
