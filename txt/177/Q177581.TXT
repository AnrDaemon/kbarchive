TITLE   :Bookshelf: Taskbar Icon Replaces QuickShelf Toolbar
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbtool kbui kbimukbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf 98 for Windows 
 - Microsoft Bookshelf 99 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The version of QuickShelf included in Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows no longer
appears as a toolbar on the desktop. QuickShelf now appears as an icon on the
taskbar.

MORE INFORMATION
================

You can use the QuickShelf icon on the taskbar to start Bookshelf, or to access
specific QuickShelf features. To start Bookshelf, click the QuickShelf icon on
the taskbar. To access QuickShelf features, right-click the QuickShelf icon on
the taskbar, and then click one of the following menu commands:

Bookshelf 98
------------

 - QuickDefine
 - QuickQuote
 - Quick Zip Code
 - Find In All Books

Bookshelf 99
------------

 - QuickDefine
 - QuickQuote
 - QuickSynonym
 - Find In All Books

You can use QuickShelf with many Windows programs to define a word or to find
relevant articles in Bookshelf. To define a word, select the word you want to
define, and then click the QuickShelf icon on the taskbar. To find relevant
articles in Bookshelf, select a word or phrase, right-click the QuickShelf icon
on the taskbar, and then click QuickQuote or Find In All Books.
