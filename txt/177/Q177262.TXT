TITLE   :FS98: List of Video Codecs Installed by the Indeo4 Setup Program
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbsetup

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 98 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains a list of the video codes that are installed by the Indeo4
Setup program.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The video codecs installed by Indeo4 are:

 - Indeo video interactive R4.1 by Intel
 - Indeo video R3.2 by Intel (this video codec is listed twice)
 - Indeo video Raw YVU9 by Intel or iyvu9_32[YVU9]

The Indeo4 Setup program is located in the Indeo4 folder on the Flight Simulator
98 CD-ROM. To run Indeo4 Setup, double-click Install.exe, and then choose the
appropriate version of Setup for your operating system.

A video codec is a software compressor and decompressor for compressing and
decompressing video data.
