TITLE   :Bookshelf 98: Horizontal Scroll Bar Is Not Provided
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf 98 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

In Bookshelf 98, the article pane may be too narrow to display the full width of
a table included in an article. In some cases, a horizontal scroll bar may not
be provided at the bottom of the article pane to allow you to view the complete
table.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, use one of the following methods:

Method 1
--------

If you are using the Largest text size, use either the Normal or Larger text
size. To change the text size in Bookshelf 98, click Options on the Tools menu,
and then click the text size option you want.

Method 2
--------

Copy the entire article from Bookshelf 98, and then paste it into a word
processor document to view the complete table.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Bookshelf 98. We are
researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft
Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
