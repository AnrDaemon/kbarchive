TITLE   :How to Display Quick View Icon Only When Connected to MSN
PRODUCT :The Microsoft Network
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2.5
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbenv

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - The Microsoft Network version 2.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

MSN, The Microsoft Network, normally displays the Quick View icon on the Windows
taskbar at all times. This article describes how to configure MSN to display the
Quick View icon only when you are connected to MSN.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To configure MSN to display the Quick View icon only when you are connected to
MSN, use the following steps:

1. Click the MSN Quick View icon, point to MSN Options, and then click Customize
   MSN.

2. Under Settings, click Quickview Icon.

3. Click Show Only When Connected, and then click Apply.

For more information about the MSN Quick View icon, see the following articles in
the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q177319 How to Set the MSN Quick View Notification Delay

   Q172787 How to Prevent MSN Quick View from Being Loaded Automatically

   Q172788 MSN Quick View Icon Does Not Appear on Taskbar
