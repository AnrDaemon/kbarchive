SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book HTML in Action, ISBN 1-55615-948-X.

The following topics are covered:

 - Chapter 13: Volcano Coffee Co. Web Site Not Available

MORE INFORMATION
================

Chapter 13: Volcano Coffee Co. Web Site Not Available
-----------------------------------------------------

Chapter 13:
References to the Volcano Coffee Company web site, starting on page 238, are out
of date. This web site is no longer available at
www.microsoft.com/ie/showcase/howto_3/volcano3.htm.

You can browse http://www.microsoft.com for the latest information about VB
Script and ActiveX controls.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
