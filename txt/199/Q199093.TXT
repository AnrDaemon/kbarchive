DOCUMENT:Q199093  22-APR-1999  [exchange]
TITLE   :XIMS: Performing WHOIS on Invisible User Returns No Information
PRODUCT :Microsoft Exchange
PROD/VER:winnt:5.5
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Exchange Server, version 5.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

When performing a WHOIS on a user who is invisible and connected to a Microsoft
Exchange Chat Server, no information on that user is returned.

STATUS
======

This is by product design.
