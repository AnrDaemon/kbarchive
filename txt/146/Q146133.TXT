TITLE   :Sending Attachments When Responding to Meeting Request
PRODUCT :Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:7.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows, version 7.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you respond to a meeting request and click Accept, the Insert and Attach
buttons on the tool bar will not be available.

WORKAROUND
==========

When you respond to a meeting request and want to send an attachment, click
Reply. The Insert and Attach buttons will be available, and you can send an
attachment.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Schedule+ version 7.0.
We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the
Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.


Additional query words: 7.00 grayed out greyed schedule plus
