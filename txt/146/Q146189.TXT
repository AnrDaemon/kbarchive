SUMMARY
=======

The following are the valid characters that can be downloaded to the US version
of the Timex DataLink Watch:

A-Z

!
"
#
$
%
&
'
(
)
*
+
,
-
.
/ 
:
<division-sign>
=
<reminder-bell>
?
_
<check-mark>
<Arrow Left>
<Arrow Right>
<Inverted Space>
<Bullet>
