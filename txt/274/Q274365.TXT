SUMMARY
=======

MSN Companion can be used in any US time zone and changing your time zone is
easy to do.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Time Zone Set-up
----------------

When you first connect to MSN, The Microsoft Network, using the MSN Companion,
MSN Companion asks for your time zone in order to configure the settings
properly. Select the proper time zone by clicking on the corresponding link.

Time Zone Change
----------------

To change the time zone on your MSN Companion

1. On the Start page click settings.

2. Click time zone.

3. Select your state or time zone.

4. Click done.
