TITLE   :Crimson Skies Quits to End Menu with ATI Rage 128 Installed
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbhw kbsound kbimu kbHardware

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Crimson Skies 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you fly an Instant Action mission in Microsoft Crimson Skies, you may
unexpectedly exit the mission after a few seconds of flight.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if an ATI Rage 128 video adapter is installed in your
computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, reduce the sound quality in the game:

1. On the Main Menu screen in Crimson Skies, click Preferences.

2. Click Audio.

3. In the Sound Quality box, click Low.

4. Click Accept Changes.

5. Click Return To Main Menu.
