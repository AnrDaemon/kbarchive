TITLE   :MGS: Inventory Continues to Scroll After You Release Arrow Keys
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbenv kbhw kbimu kbHardware

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Metal Gear Solid 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

In Microsoft Metal Gear Solid, when you use the arrow keys on a Microsoft
IntelliType Pro keyboard to scroll through your Inventory in first-person mode,
the inventory may continue to scroll for a few seconds after you release the
arrow keys.

CAUSE
=====

This issue can occur if the IntelliType Pro keyboard is connected to a USB port
on your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, connect the IntelliType Pro keyboard to a PS/2 keyboard
port on your computer.

Additional query words: msgame metalgearsolid mgs stick down arrow
