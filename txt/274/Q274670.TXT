DOCUMENT:Q274670  07-MAY-2002  [homegame]
TITLE   :CFS2: Cannot Configure the Amount of Fuel and Ammunition
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbenv kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you move the "Initial fuel level" or the "Initial ammo level" slider in the
Player Aircraft dialog box, you may not change the actual amount of fuel or
ammunition that is loaded on your aircraft when you fly the mission.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the Unlimited Fuel or Unlimited Ammo check boxes are
selected in the game settings.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, turn off the Unlimited Fuel and Unlimited Ammo features
in the game:

1. Start Combat Flight Simulator 2.

2. Click Settings.

3. Click Realism Settings.

4. Click to clear the Unlimited Fuel and the Unlimited Ammo check boxes.

5. Click OK, and then OK again.

MORE INFORMATION
================

In addition, you cannot adjust the "Initial fuel level" or the "Initial ammo
level" slider when you play one of the following game types:

 - Free Flight
 - Quick Combat
 - Single Missions
 - Multiplayer
