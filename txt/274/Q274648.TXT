TITLE   :Combat Flight Simulator 2: Aircraft Fly with Landing Gear Down
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games

OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbui kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you play the Japanese campaigns in Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2 or
when you encounter Japanese aircraft in the U.S. campaigns, you may notice that
some Japanese aircraft do not raise their landing gear.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The Aichi D3A aircraft does not have a landing gear that can be raised.

The Aichi D3A (Allied reporting name "Val") was the standard Japanese
carrier-based dive-bomber during the opening stages of World War II in the
Pacific theater.
