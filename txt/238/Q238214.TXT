The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Asheron's Call, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Asheron's Call: Dark Majesty, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article describes how to enable or disable 3D Acceleration in Microsoft
Asheron's Call. Enabling 3D Acceleration improves both the quality and the
performance of graphics in Asheron's Call.

If you experience display problems when you play Asheron's Call, you may be able
to work around these problems by disabling 3D Acceleration.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To enable or disable 3D Acceleration in Asheron's Call:

1. Click Start, point to Programs, point to Microsoft Games, point to Asheron's
   Call, and then click Asheron's Call.

2. Click Settings, and then click 3D Settings.

3. Click to clear or select the Enable 3D Acceleration check box, and then click
   OK.

4. Click OK, and then click Exit.
