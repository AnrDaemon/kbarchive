TITLE   :Where to Obtain Multimedia Information
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1,3.11; :1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
 - Microsoft Windows with Multimedia Extensions, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The following items are available by calling the Multimedia Development Council
at (202) 331-0494:

 - MPC specifications

 - List of current Multimedia titles (including hardware and software)

 - Information on upgrading an existing system

 - Information on how to gain approval for the Multimedia PC (MPC) logo

Note: The Multimedia Development Council is the owner of the MPC logo.

Additional query words: 1.00 3.10 3.11
