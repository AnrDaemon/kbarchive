TITLE   :GP Faults when Printing over Banyan Vines 4.11
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When using Banyan vines 4.11, with Windows 3.1, you may receive general
protection (GP) faults when printing to any port other than FILE:.

To correct this problem, obtain Patch 5 from Banyan Vines.


Additional query words: 3.10 3.11 4.10 fault print
