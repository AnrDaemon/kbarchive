SUMMARY
=======

This article describes how to type international characters and symbols on an
English keyboard.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To type international characters with an English keyboard, you need to change
the keyboard layout. To change the Windows keyboard layout, follow these steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Keyboard.

3. Click the Language tab, and then select your current keyboard layout (which
   you want to change).

   Note: If your keyboard is not listed, click the Add button to install a
   different language.

4. Click the Properties tab, and then click United States-International for the
   keyboard layout.

5. Click OK, and then click "Set as Default".

6. Click Apply, and when prompted, insert your Windows CD-ROM.

7. Click OK to close Keyboard Properties, and then close Control Panel.

When you want to type an international character, hold down the ALT key, and then
press the character you would like to accent.

If you want to disable this feature, change the keyboard layout back to its
original setting.
