TITLE   :Combat Flight Simulator: Computer Hangs When Aircraft Crashes
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kburl kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator: WWII Europe Series, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When your aircraft crashes in Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator, your computer
may stop responding (hang).

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a Diamond Viper V330 display adapter is installed in
your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the
Diamond Viper V330 display adapter. To do this, visit the following Diamond
Multimedia Web site:

   http://www.diamondmm.com
