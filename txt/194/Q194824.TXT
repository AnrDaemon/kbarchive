DOCUMENT:Q194824  07-MAY-2002  [homegame]
TITLE   :Combat Flight Simulator: Movie Unavailable for Training Mission
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdisplay kbsound kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator: WWII Europe Series, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to start a Training Mission in Combat Flight Simulator, you may
be unable to play the introductory movie for the mission, and you may not hear
any verbal instructions while you fly the mission.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

 - You selected the Typical option when you installed Combat Flight Simulator,
   or you selected the Custom option, and then clicked to clear the Training
   Mission Media check box.

 - The Combat Flight Simulator CD-ROM is not in the CD-ROM drive.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, follow these steps:

1. Insert the Combat Flight Simulator CD-ROM into the CD-ROM drive.

2. Press X to end the Training Mission.

3. Restart the Training Mission.
