TITLE   :Mac Bookshelf 96-97: Bookshelf Guide Disabled with System 7.0
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:MACINTOSH:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf 1996-97 for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you open Bookshelf '96-'97, you receive the following error message:

   Bookshelf Guide will not work with this version of the system software. To
   use this feature, please upgrade to System 7.1 or later.

CAUSE
=====

Because of an incompatibility between Bookshelf, Apple Guide, and System 7.0,
access to the Bookshelf Guide has been disabled.

RESOLUTION
==========

To use the Bookshelf Guide, you need to upgrade your system software to System
7.1 or later.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Microsoft Bookshelf '96-'97 can be installed on System 7.0, however, you will
not have access to the Bookshelf Guide. The Bookshelf Guide requires at least
System 7.1. Bookshelf Guide is a help feature used to show you how to perform
tasks within the program.

For information on upgrading your system software, please contact the Apple
Corporation at (800) 767-2775 or on the World Wide Web (WWW) at

   http://www.apple.com.
