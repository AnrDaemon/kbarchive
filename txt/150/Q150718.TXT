TITLE   :Mac Bookshelf 96-97: Pronunciation Does Not Copy
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1996-1997
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf version 1996-1997 for the Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you try to copy a pronunciation from Bookshelf and then paste the
pronunciation into another program, the pronunciation does not play.

CAUSE
=====

If the article where you copied the pronunciation also contains an anthem, then
only a picture of the sound may be pasted. The actual sound of the pronunciation
was not copied, and nothing is heard when you try to play the sound.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Bookshelf 96-97 for the
Macintosh. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in
the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

MORE INFORMATION
================

If the article contains only a pronunciation and not an anthem, then the
pronunciation is be copied and can be played in another application.
