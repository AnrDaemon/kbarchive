TITLE   :Mac Bookshelf 96-97: Bookshelf Guide Quits on a PowerBook
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:MACINTOSH:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf 1996-97 for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

If the screen dims when you are in the Bookshelf Guide, Bookshelf stops
responding (hangs) or unexpectedly quits.

This occurs if you are using a PowerBook 520 or 540 running System 7.1.1.

CAUSE
=====

Bookshelf Guide requires Apple Guide to function. Apple Guide has a known
problem running on System 7.1.1 on the PowerBook 520 and 540 models. This
problem causes Bookshelf Guide quit or hang if the screen dims. This problem
does not occur if you put the PowerBook to sleep.

RESOLUTION
==========

To prevent this problem from occurring, do one of the following.

 - Upgrade your system software to System 7.5 or higher.

   -or-

 - Prevent the screen from dimming.

   To prevent the screen from dimming, so the following:
   1. On the Apple menu, click Control Panels.

   2. Open the PowerBook control panel.

   3. Choose Custom in the PowerBook control panel.

   4. On the Screen Dims slider, select Never.

   -or-

 - Instead of letting the screen dim, put the screen to sleep.

   To put the screen to sleep do the following:
    - on the Special menu, click Sleep.
