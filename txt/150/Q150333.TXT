TITLE   :Will Not Print Projects Without Assigned Tasks
PRODUCT :Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:7.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows, version 7.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you try to print a list of your current projects that do not have tasks
currently assigned to them, they will not print out.

CAUSE
=====

This is by design. The only projects printed are the ones that have at least one
task currently assigned to them.
