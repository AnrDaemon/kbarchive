SUMMARY
=======

The following article lists the movie titles included in the September-November
95 update of Cinemania 1996:

Movie Titles
------------

The Addiction - Against the Wall - Angus - Arabian Knight - Assassins - Babe -
The Baby-Sitter's Club - Bandit Queen - Beyond Rangoon - The Big Green - Blue in
the Face - The Brothers McMullen - Clockers - Clueless - Country Life -
Dangerous Minds - Dead Presidents - Desperado - Devil in a Blue Dress - Double
Happiness - Feast of July - Free Willy 2: The Adventure Home - Get Shorty -
Hackers - How to Make an American Quilt - Indian in the Cupboard - Jade -
Jeffrey - A Kid in King Arthur's Court - Living in Oblivion - Lord of Illusions
- Magic in the Water - A Month by the Lake - Moonlight and Valentino - Moving
the Mountain - Moving the Mountain - Mr. Write - Nadja - Nine Months - Operation
Dumbo Drop - Persuasion - Pure Formality - Run of the Country - The Scarlet
Letter - Seven - Show Girls - Steal Big, Steal Little - Strange Days - Three
Wishes - To Die For - To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything, Julie Newmar - Troll
in Central Park - Under Siege 2: Under Dark Territory - Unstrung Heroes -
Unzipped - Usual Suspects - Virtuosity - A Walk in the Clouds

MORE INFORMATION
================

This first set of updates includes many films from the late summer and fall of
1995, hence the unusually large number of titles.
