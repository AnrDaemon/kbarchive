TITLE   :Bookshelf '96-'97: Adjusting QuickShelf Toolbar to Fit Screen
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf 1996-97 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

If you customize QuickShelf to show all books as buttons, and then use the large
buttons, the QuickShelf toolbar may extend beyond the edge of the display area.
Some toolbar buttons may be clipped or invisible.

This occurs only if your display resolution is set to 640 X 480.

RESOLUTION
==========

To make all of the QuickShelf toolbar buttons visible, use one of the following
resolutions :

 - Customize QuickShelf to use regular toolbar buttons.

   -or-

 - Use a display resolution higher than 640 x 480.

To change the toolbar button size, use the steps below for your version of
Windows:

Windows 3.x
-----------

1. Click the QuickShelf options button (downward-pointing arrow) on the far
   right of the QuickShelf toolbar.

2. Click Customize, then click the View tab.

3. Click Regular Buttons, then click OK.

Windows 95/98
-------------

1. Click the QuickShelf icon on the QuickShelf toolbar

2. Click Customize, then click the View tab.

3. Clear the check mark from the Large Buttons box, then click OK.

Changing Display Setting
------------------------

For more information about how to change the display settings in Windows,
including resolution settings, see your Windows printed documentation or online
Help.
