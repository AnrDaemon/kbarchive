TITLE   :FP: Reselecting Reports Shows Previous Report Not Site Summary
PRODUCT :Word Front Page
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdta

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft FrontPage 2000 
 - Microsoft FrontPage 2002 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

In Microsoft FrontPage, when you change views after selecting a report in
Reports view, and then reselect Reports on the Views bar, the previously
selected report appears rather than the default Site Summary. This behavior
occurs even if you close and reopen your web.

RESOLUTION
==========

To change to a different report view, use either of the following methods:

 - On the View menu, point to Reports, and click the report you want. For
   example, to show the list of available reports, click Site Summary.

   -or-

 - On the Reporting toolbar, in the Reports list, click the report you want. For
   example, to show the list of available reports, click Site Summary.

   NOTE: If the Reporting toolbar does not appear, point to Toolbars on the View
   menu, and then click Reporting.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For more information about about using reports in FrontPage, click Microsoft
FrontPage Help on the Help menu, type "Web site reports" (without the quotation
marks) in the Office Assistant or the Answer Wizard, and then click Search to
view the topic.
