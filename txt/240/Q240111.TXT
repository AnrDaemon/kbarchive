DOCUMENT:Q240111  22-NOV-1999  [homegame]
TITLE   :NBA Inside Drive 2000: Mouse Pointer Hangs or Is Duplicated
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdisplay kbenv kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, your mouse pointer may stop
responding (hang), or duplicate pointers may be displayed on the screen.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior occurs if the following conditions are true:

 - Online Help is displayed on the screen.

 - You press ALT+TAB to switch to another program, and then switch back to NBA
   Inside Drive 2000.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, close online Help, press ALT+TAB to minimize the game and
switch to another program, and then press ALT+TAB again to restore NBA Inside
Drive 2000.

NOTE: To prevent this issue from occurring, close online Help before you press
ALT+TAB to switch to another program.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.
