DOCUMENT:Q173893  16-DEC-1999  [exchange]
TITLE   :XCLN: Pronto 97 IMAP Client can't use Mailbox Name Over 23 Chars
PRODUCT :Microsoft Exchange
PROD/VER:winnt:5.5
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Exchange Server, version 5.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SUMMARY
=======

Users of the Pronto 97 IMAP/POP mail client will not be able to download mail
from the Microsoft Exchange Server if their account name is longer than 23
characters. This is specified in the Online/Mailbox settings/Host Login field of
the Pronto 97 client.
