 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 98 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Flight Simulator 98 is the most significant upgrade in the history of the
product. With aircraft flight models validated by Cessna, Learjet, and
aerobatics champion Patty Wagstaff, plus detailed information developed in
conjunction with Flight Safety International, a worldwide database of airports
and navigational aids, and the latest graphical technology, Flight Simulator 98
is "As real as it gets!"

MORE INFORMATION
================

What's New
----------

 - Detailed scenery with support for 3-D graphics acceleration.

 - Highly detailed objects in more than 45 cities around the world.

 - Support for force feedback devices.

 - Digitally sampled sounds.

 - Multiplayer capability over the Internet, a network, or via a Modem,
   including support for Zone.com at http:\\www.zone.com.

 - Photo Realistic Panels.

 - More than 3,000 airports worldwide.

 - Online Airport and Facilities Directory that displays airport, ILS, VOR, and
   NDB data directly from the scenery files.

 - More lessons and Adventures.

 - Customize Controls dialog box makes it easy to assign functions to joystick
   buttons and key combinations.

 - Three new aircraft. A Bell 206B JetRanger III helicopter, a Cessna Skylane
   182S, ad the Learjet 45.
