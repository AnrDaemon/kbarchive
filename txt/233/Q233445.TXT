TITLE   :Business Planner: 20-Second Delay Before Program Starts
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbenv kbtool kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Business Planner 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to start Microsoft Business Planner, you may experience a
20-second delay before the program starts.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if you are viewing a wizard interview page when you quit
Business Planner, and one or more of the following conditions is true:

 - You run Business Planner on a Microsoft Windows 95 or Microsoft Windows NT
   4.0-based computer.

 - You attempt to start Business Planner immediately after you quit the program.

RESOLUTION
==========

To prevent this issue from occurring, click Exit on the Wizard toolbar before
you quit Business Planner.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design.

Additional query words: msbp delay prolonged slow
