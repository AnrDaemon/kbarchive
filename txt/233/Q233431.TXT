TITLE   :Business Planner: Unexpected Text Is Displayed in ToolTip
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbinterop kbui kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Business Planner 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you open a Microsoft Excel or Microsoft Word template created for Microsoft
Business Planner and move the pointer over the "Go to Article" link in the
template, you may see a ToolTip that contains text similar to the following:

   Msbp://-r:1A50C19A/

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Business Planner.

Additional query words: msbp linked url tips
