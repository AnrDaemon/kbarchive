TITLE   :CD Sampler: Mouse Pointer Invisible During Demo
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::Fall 1993 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Home CD Sampler Fall 1993 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The mouse cursor will disappear when you run the Microsoft Works, Microsoft
Publisher, or Microsoft Flight Simulator full demonstrations from the Microsoft
Home CD Sampler if an MS-DOS level mouse driver is not loaded.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The Microsoft Works or Microsoft Publisher full demonstrations were originally
designed as stand-alone demonstrations for MS-DOS. To properly display the mouse
cursor, the mouse driver must be loaded at the operating system level.
