TITLE   :Mac Wkst: Can Print and Read in Outbox
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For Appletalk Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks, version 3.1 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

While in the offline mode of version 3.1 of Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk
Networks, sent messages in the Outbox will change from bold to plain text if
read or printed. This does not affect the status of the messages in the
recipient's mailbox or the return receipt, if used. The message appears to the
recipient as an unread message.

Additional query words: 3.10
