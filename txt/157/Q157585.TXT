The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Golf for Windows, version 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

To play Microsoft Golf 3.0, you need:

 - A 486/66 MHz computer (Pentium 90MHz computer or better recommended).

 - Microsoft Windows 95/98 or Windows NT 3.51 or later operating system (will
   not run on earlier versions of Windows).

 - A 2X CD-ROM drive or faster to play from the compact disc.

 - 8 MB of memory (RAM) (16 MB or more recommended).

 - 20 MB of available hard disk space.

 - An audio board plus speakers or headphones to hear audio.

 - 14,400 baud modem for modem play (28,800 baud modem recommended).

 - A Microsoft Mouse or compatible pointing device.

 - Super VGA 256-color monitor.

NOTE: Golf 3.0 requires Microsoft Windows 95 or Windows NT 3.51 or higher. To
purchase a Windows upgrade, see your local retailer.
