TITLE   :Do-It-Yourself Guide: Ceramic Tile Estimator Limitation
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Reader's Digest Complete Do-It-Yourself Guide for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you use the Ceramic Tile Estimator in the Microsoft Reader's Digest
Complete Do-It-Yourself Guide, the largest sized tile you can enter is a 12.99
inches by 12.99 inches. You cannot enter any whole number greater than 12
inches.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Reader's Digest
Complete Do-It-Yourself Guide for Windows, version 1.0. We are researching this
problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it
becomes available.
