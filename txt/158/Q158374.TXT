TITLE   :Hellbender: Viewing Options Available in Different Pilot Views

OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Hellbender for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article discusses the pilot viewing options available during game play in
Hellbender.

MORE INFORMATION
================

View with No Instruments        Cockpit     Chase     Outside

Left                              Yes         No        Yes
Right                             Yes         No        Yes
Up                                Yes         No        Yes
Down                              Yes         No        Yes

View with Full Instruments      Cockpit     Chase     Outside

Left                              Yes         No        Yes
Right                             Yes         No        Yes
Up                                No          No        Yes
Down                            RearView      No        Yes

View with Partial Instruments   Cockpit     Chase     Outside

Left                              Yes         No        Yes
Right                             Yes         No        Yes
Up                                No          No        Yes
Down                            RearView      No        Yes

If the individual views are available, the following table describes what keys
are used to access them.

VIEW CONTROLS
-------------

View               Keys

Left               INSERT or NUMERIC KEYPAD 0
Right              DELETE or NUMERIC KEYPAD period (.)
Up                 NUMERIC KEYPAD MINUS SIGN (-)
Down               NUMERIC KEYPAD PLUS SIGN (+)

Additional query words: 1.00 see side top bottom backwards behind under belly view
