TITLE   :GEX: Choppy Sound or Gameplay
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft GEX, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

You may experience choppy gameplay or sound in GEX.

RESOLUTION
==========

Verify that your CD-ROM drive is optimized for Windows 95, or modify the display
options in GEX.

To verify that your CD-ROM drive is optimized for Windows 95:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click the System icon.

3. Click the Performance tab, and then click File System.

4. Click the CD-ROM tab.

5. Drag the Supplemental Cache Size slider to Large, and then select the speed
   of your CD-ROM drive.

To modify display options in GEX:

1. Click Options on the GEX menu, and then click Display.

2. In the Window Size box, click 320 x 224 or click Full Screen.

For more information about troubleshooting sound issues, see the following
article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q158031 GEX: Troubleshooting Sound Problems

Additional query words: 1.00 distortion noise crack skip chop game play slow pause figures characters
