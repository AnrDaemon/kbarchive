TITLE   :Mac Encarta 97: About Encarta Not Available From Some Screens
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1997 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The About Encarta... menu option (used to obtain product ID, system information,
and program credits) located on the Apple menu is not available (grayed out)
from several areas in the program.

However, you can always access About Encarta by restarting Encarta and selecting
About Encarta... from the Apple menu.
