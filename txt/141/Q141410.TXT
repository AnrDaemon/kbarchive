TITLE   :World of Flight: Color Palette Problem Occurs with QuicRes
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:1.00
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft World of Flight for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

If you change the video card settings with QuickRes while World of Flight is
running, the screen image colors become incorrect and the display becomes
distorted.

RESOLUTION
==========

To change your video color palette settings, do the following:

1. Exit World of Flight.

2. Change the video display settings.

3. Restart World of Flight.

NOTE: Do not switch colors while World of Flight is running.

MORE INFORMATION
================

QuickRes allows you to switch the color palette settings for your video card
dynamically, without restarting Windows.

QuickRes is a program included with the PowerToys package. PowerToys includes a
notice that its programs do not have product support, and are to be used only at
your own risk.
