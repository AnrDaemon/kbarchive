TITLE   :Baseball 1995: Team Filter Button Resets to All
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Baseball Guide for Windows, 1995 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you click Back to return to the Team History category, the Choose Team
"Show In List:" resets to All.

This occurs if you click Choose Team in Team History, select Current Teams under
Show In List:, choose a team and click any hot text. When you click Back, and
click Choose Team the category resets to All.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design.
