TITLE   :MAC Art Gallery: Screen Flickers with Animations
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:MACINTOSH:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbbuglist

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Art Gallery for the Macintosh, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you run an Art Gallery animation on a Power Macintosh (PowerPC), a portion
of the animation may flicker with patterns of thin, horizontal white lines.

RESOLUTION
==========

There is no resolution to this problem. The problem affects only Power Macintosh
computers and does not occur on 680x0 series Macintosh computers.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Art Gallery for the
Macintosh, version 1.0. We are researching this problem and will post new
information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
