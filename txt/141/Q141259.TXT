TITLE   :Correct Answer Feedback Absent in Multimedia Matching Game
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Dangerous Creatures for Windows, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Oceans for Windows, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft World of Flight for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to play one of the Matching Quiz games, such as "Aces and Their
Airplanes" or "Beach Walk", no feedback is provided by the game to tell you if
your match is correct.

CAUSE
=====

The game sound effects are turned off.

Feedback for matching games is provided by sound effects. Sound effects can be
turned off and on using the Options button.

RESOLUTION
==========

Turn on the sound effects using the following steps:

1. In the program listed at the top of this article, click Options, then click
   Sounds or Audio/Video.

2. In the Sound Effects area, click On.

3. Click OK, then close Options.
