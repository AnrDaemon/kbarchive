TITLE   :Encarta 1996: Selected Text Prints Garbled
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbbuglist

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta 96 Encyclopedia for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you print selected text containing both italics and regular text, the
printed document may contain garbled or overlapped data.

RESOLUTION
==========

Print the entire article or copy the selected text and paste the text into
another program. Print the document from the program.


STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem with Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia
for Windows, 1996 edition. We are researching this problem and will post new
information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

Additional query words: multi media multimedia multi-media mmtitles kbmm distorted distort scrambled overlapped
