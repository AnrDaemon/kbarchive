TITLE   :Baseball 2001: Graphics Are Redrawn Multiple Times During a Game
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbhw kbimu msgame kbHardware

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Baseball 2001 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Baseball 2001, certain graphic elements on the screen
may be redrawn several times during the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a Matrox Millennium G200 video adapter is installed
in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this issue, configure Baseball 2001 to run in a lower display
resolution:

1. Start Baseball 2001.

2. On the Home Plate screen, click Options.

3. Click the Graphics tab.

4. Under Available Resolutions, click a lower display resolution, and then click
   OK.
