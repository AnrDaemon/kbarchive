TITLE   :MS-DOS 5.0 & Later and the AST 3G Plus Graphics Card

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft MS-DOS operating system versions 5.0, 5.0a, 6.0, 6.2, 6.21, 6.22 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

If you are running MS-DOS with the AST 3G Plus graphics card and experience
problems with video output, contact AST and upgrade from the 3G Plus to the 3G
Plus II graphics card. The AST 3G Plus card is a full-length video card that
used to be distributed with AST machines but is no longer in production because
of various problems with the card. AST has replaced the AST 3G Plus with the 3G
Plus II graphics card.

3G Plus graphics card is manufactured by AST, a vendor independent of Microsoft;
we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's performance
or reliability.
