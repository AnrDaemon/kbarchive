TITLE   :WD97: Tabs Are Removed Running Spelling Check
PRODUCT :Word 97 for Windows
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:97
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

When you change the spelling of a word in the Spelling And Grammar dialog box
(under Not In Dictionary) and then click Change, white space (tabs or spaces) is
removed from the beginning of the sentence.

WORKAROUND
==========

To prevent the white space from being removed from your document, do one of the
following when the incorrect word is selected in the Spelling And Grammar dialog
box.

Method 1: Make the Correction in the Document
---------------------------------------------

To make the correction in the document, follow these steps:

1. Click in your document and make the correction directly in the document.

2. Click Resume to resume the spelling check.

Method 2: Use a Suggested Word
------------------------------

To use a suggested word, follow these steps:

1. Under Suggestions, select the correct spelling of the incorrectly spelled
   word.

2. Click Change.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.

This problem has been corrected in Word 2000.
