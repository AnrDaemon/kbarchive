TITLE   :Drawing 45-Degree Lines in Paintbrush

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

To draw a line in Paintbrush that snaps to 45-degree increments, do the
following:

1. Select the Line tool.

2. Hold down the SHIFT key and the left mouse button.

3. Move the mouse cursor in a circular motion around the starting point.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For example, imagine your drawing placed over a clock face. You can only draw
lines between:

 - 12 and 6

 - 1.5 and 7.5

 - 3 and 9

 - 4.5 and 10.5

This technique also works with other drawing tools.
