TITLE   :Instruction: VOR Tracking with Glideslope On
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
SUMMARY
=======

In Flight Simulator Versions 3.00 and 4.00, during the VOR Tracking with Wind
lesson (under Option W, "See more lessons") in Flight Instruction mode, the
glideslope vertical indicator should be out of sight when not in use.

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
becomes available.
