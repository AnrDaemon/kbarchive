SYMPTOMS
========

It been reported that some Macintosh accelerator cards will cause FoxPro for
Macintosh to give a "Unimplemented trap error" message when starting, and then
abnormally terminate the program.

STATUS
======

Microsoft is researching this issue and will post new information here in the
Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

MORE INFORMATION
================

This has been reported on an accelerated Macintosh SE/30 with a Mobius
accelerator card and a Daystar-accelerated Macintosh IIfx.

FoxPro for Macintosh will fail to run if this error is encountered.

The Apple, Mobius, and Daystar products included here are manufactured by vendors
independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding
these products' performance or reliability.
