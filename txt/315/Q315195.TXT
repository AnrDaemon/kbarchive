TITLE   :Flight Simulator 2002: Cannot Access Air Traffic Controller
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2002 Professional Edition 
 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2002 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you press the ACCENT key (`) to gain access to the Air Traffic Controller
(ATC) feature while you play Microsoft Flight Simulator 2002, the Air Traffic
Controller window may not displayed as expected.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if you play Flight Simulator 2002 in multiplayer mode.
The ATC feature is available only in a single-player Flight Simulator game.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For additional information about the ATC feature in Flight Simulator 2002, refer
to the Air Traffic Control Handbook. To do this, follow these steps:

1. Start Flight Simulator 2002.

2. In the left pane, click LIBRARY/HELP.

3. Under Handbooks, click "Air Traffic Control Handbook", and then click Open
   Handbook.

   NOTE: To view the Air Traffic Control Handbook, Adobe Acrobat Reader must be
   installed on your computer.
