TITLE   :Works 2000 Icons Change After Upgrade to Windows 2000
PRODUCT :Windows for Workgroups and Windows NT Networking Issues
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2000
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbgraphic kbinterop kbtool kbui kbOSWin2000 kbGrpDSW2KISVkbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows 2000 Professional 
 - Microsoft Works Suite 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

After you upgrade your computer to Windows 2000, the Works Suite 2000 menu icons
may be changed to Works word processor icons. Also, the Works Suite 2000 address
book icon may not be available.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.
