DOCUMENT:Q250684  12-NOV-2001  [homegame]
TITLE   :Err Msg: <Program> Executed Invalid Instruction in <Program>.icd
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0,2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kberrmsg kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
 - Microsoft Pandora's Box, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to start one of the programs listed at the beginning of this
article, you may receive one of the following error messages:

    - Empires2 has executed an invalid instruction in Empires2.icd.

    - Empires2 has executed an invalid instruction in Empires2.exe.

    - Pandora has executed an invalid instruction in Pandora.icd.

    - Pandora has executed an invalid instruction in Pandora.exe.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if one of the following Cyrix microprocessors is
installed in your computer:

 - Cyrix 486DX

 - Cyrix 6x86

 - Cyrix MediaGX

MORE INFORMATION
================

The Cyrix processors mentioned in this article are not compatible with the
programs listed at the beginning of this article.

independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding
these products' performance or reliability.
