TITLE   :Mac Form: Versions of HyperCard Required by the Form Designer
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For Appletalk Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2.0,2.0a,2.0b,3.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks, versions 2.0, 2.0a, 2.0b, 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Microsoft Mail Form Designer utility requires Apple's HyperCard program.

With Mail version 2.0, use HyperCard version 1.2.2 or 1.2.5. The 2.0 Form
Designer is not compatible with HyperCard version 2.x.

With Mail version 3.0, use HyperCard versions 1.2.2, 1.2.5, 2.0, or 2.1. With
HyperCard version 2.0 or 2.1, Form Designer 3.0a is recommended. For a
description of the differences in Form Designer 3.0a, query on the following
words:

   " resize and form " (without the quotation marks)


Additional query words: 2.00 2.00a 2.00b 3.00 2.0a 2.0b
