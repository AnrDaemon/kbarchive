TITLE   :Combat FS: Master List of Aircraft in Combat Flight Simulator

OPER/SYS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator: WWII Europe Series, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the aircraft that are included in Microsoft Combat Flight
Simulator: WWII Europe Series and Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII
Pacific Theater.

NOTE: You cannot view the cockpit of certain aircraft that are included in Combat
Flight Simulator. These aircraft are designed to be controlled by the computer
and cannot be flown by a human player.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The following aircraft are included with Combat Flight Simulator: WWII Europe
Series:

 - F109E
 - F109G
 - FW 190a
 - Hurricane Mark I
 - P47D
 - P51D
 - Sopwith Camel
 - Spitfire Mk I
 - Spitfire Mk IX

The following aircraft are included with Combat Flight Simulator: WWII Europe
Series but can only be controlled by the computer:

 - B17G
 - B24J
 - B25J
 - C47
 - Do17Z2
 - Ju87A
 - Mosquito
 - P38
 - Tempest
 - V1 aircraft

The following aircraft are included with Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific
Theater:

 - Grumman F4F-4 Wildcat
 - Grumman F6F-3 Hellcat
 - Kawanishi N1K2-J Shiden
 - Lockheed P-38F Lightning
 - Mitsubishi A6M2 Reisen
 - Mitsubishi A6M5 Reisen
 - Vought F4u-1A Corsair

The following aircraft are included with Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific
Theater but can only be controlled by the computer:

 - Aichi D3A1
 - Bell P-39D Airacobra
 - Consolidated B-24D Liberator
 - Douglas C-47 Skytrain
 - Douglas SBD-2 Dauntless
 - Douglas TBD-1 Devastator
 - Grumman TBF Avenger
 - Mitsubishi G4M2
 - Nakijima B5N2
 - Nakijima Ki-43-lib
 - North American B-25D Mitchell

NOTE: You may be able to download third-party versions of these aircraft from the
Internet.

Microsoft does not support the use of third-party aircraft in Combat Flight
Simulator. If you experience a problem when you fly third-party aircraft in
Combat Flight Simulator, contact the manufacturer of the third-party aircraft
for technical support.
