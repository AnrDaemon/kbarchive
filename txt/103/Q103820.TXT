TITLE   :PC Adm: Generating RNETWORK.GLB, Writing Records to .XTN Files
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For PC Networks
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0b
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for PC Networks, version 3.0b 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

The following two problems can occur:

 - When you generate an RNETWORK.GLB file for remote users, only FFAPI
   postoffices of service type equal to 100 are processed.

 - When you write records to .XTN files, a corrupt record is sometimes appended
   to the .XTN files.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in version 3.0b of Microsoft Mail
for PC Networks. This problem was corrected in version 3.2.
