TITLE   :List of Color Pictures in Cinemania
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1992 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1992 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Cinemania contains 31 color images. These color images require a non-pallatized
color video driver installed in Windows. A 256-color video driver is
recommended.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To display a color image in Cinemania, select one of the titles listed below
from either Movie Listings or Gallery.

   Amadeus
   Annie Hall
   Becket
   Ben Hur (1959)
   Cinema Paradiso
   Edward Scissorhands
   Empire Strikes Back
   Godfather III
   Handmaids Tale, The
   House Party
   Kiss of the Spider Woman
   Krays
   Marriage of Maria Brown
   Matewan
   Miller's Crossing
   Mountains of the Moon
   Mr. and Mrs. Bridge
   My Left Foot
   Narrow Margin
   Network
   Pacific Heights
   Pump Up the Volume
   Russia House
   Sheik
   Stanley and Iris
   Ten Commandments
   Terms of Endearment
   Total Recall
   Two Jakes, The
   Young Guns II
   Zorba the Greek
