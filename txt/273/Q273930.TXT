DOCUMENT:Q273930  09-NOV-2001  [homegame]
TITLE   :Crimson Skies: Quits Unexpectedly After Finishing Game on Zone
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Crimson Skies 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you finish playing a game of Microsoft Crimson Skies with more than five
other players on Zone.com, Crimson Skies may quit unexpectedly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the video driver or the sound driver that is
installed on your computer is outdated.


RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, contact your hardware manufacturer to inquire about how
to obtain and install the latest version of the video driver for your video
adapter and the latest version of the sound driver for your sound card.

For information about how to contact your hardware manufacturer, click the
appropriate article number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge
Base:

   Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K

   Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P

   Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
