TITLE   :Midtown Madness 2: Large Patches of the Road Are Missing
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Midtown Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Midtown Madness 2, large patches of the road may be
missing until you drive over them.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if your video adapter is based on the nVIDIA GeForce 256
or the nVIDIA TNT chip set.

================
