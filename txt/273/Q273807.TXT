TITLE   :Midtown Madness 2: Correct Audio Device Is Not Displayed
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbui kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Midtown Madness 2, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Midtown Madness 2 on a Microsoft Windows 98-based
computer, the default audio device that is displayed in the Device box on the
Audio Options screen may be incorrect.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a Creative Sound Blaster PCI sound card is installed
in the computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, click the correct audio device in the Device box on the
Audio Options screen in Midtown Madness 2.
