TITLE   :Pregnancy Err Msg: "No Articles Were Found Containing..."
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you use the Find By Word feature, you may receive the following error
message:

   No articles were found containing "<your word>".

   Make sure you have spelled the word correctly. Or try turning on or off the
   "Search article text instead of article titles".

   If you want to find a different word, click Start Over.

As you type the word, the error message repeats as you delete or insert each
character.

CAUSE
=====

The Keyboard Repeat Delay option is set to Long.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this problem, do the following:

1. Click on Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click the Keyboard icon.

3. On the Speed tab, set the Repeat Delay option to Short.

   NOTE: By default, the Speed tab is in the foreground.

4. Click Apply and then OK.
