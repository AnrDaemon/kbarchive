TITLE   :FS2000: Black Screen/Distorted Scenery Using #9 FX Video Adapter
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition 
 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000, the screen may turn black, or the
scenery may flicker or be distorted.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

 - A Number Nine FX Reality 334 video adapter is installed in your computer.

 - The screen resolution in the game is set to 1024 x 768 pixels.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest version of the video
driver for the Number Nine FX Reality 334 video adapter from the following
Number Nine Web site:

   http://www.nine.com

To work around this issue, reduce the screen resolution in the game. To do this:

1. On the Options menu, point to Settings, and then click Display.

2. In the Resolution box, click a lower resolution setting, and then click the
   CHECK MARK.
