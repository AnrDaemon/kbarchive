TITLE   :Midtown/Motocross Madness: Game Hangs or Pointer Flickers
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Midtown Madness, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Motocross Madness, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Microsoft Midtown Madness or Microsoft Motocross Madness, you may
experience either of the following symptoms:

 - The game stops responding (hangs).

 - The pointing device pointer flickers.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if an ATI Rage 128 video adapter is installed in your
computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the ATI
RAGE 128 video adapter from the following ATI web site:

   http://www.atitech.com
