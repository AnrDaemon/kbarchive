TITLE   :AoE II: Cannot Record a Multiplayer Game on Zone.com
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
 - Microsoft Zone.com 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to record a multiplayer game on Zone.com, you cannot locate the
recorded game after you complete the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior occurs because you cannot record a game that is played on
Zone.com.

NOTE: You can only record a multiplayer game that is played in a local area
network (LAN).

Additional query words: msgame aok aoe2 aoeii
