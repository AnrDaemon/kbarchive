TITLE   :Baseball: Must Re-Enter Info if Registration Is Unsuccessful
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1994 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Baseball for Windows, version 1994 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Unless you make a connection and are successfully registered, you must re-enter
your registration and modem setting information every time you try to dial out
to the online Baseball Daily service. After you have successfully made the
connection once, you will be registered and this information will be permanently
recorded.
