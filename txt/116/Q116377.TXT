TITLE   :WFWG 3.11: Fax Machines That Are Incompatible with AWFAX
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 3.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SUMMARY
=======

The following fax machines are incompatible with Microsoft At Work PC Fax:

 - Hitachi HiFax 17

 - Omnifax 9s Series

 - Omnifax G90 Series

MORE INFORMATION
================

Hitachi HiFax 17
----------------

The Hitachi HiFax 17 fails during the negotiation stage and generates an
"Undeliverable Mail" error message.


Omnifax 9s Series and G90 Series
--------------------------------

Both of the above Omnifax machines fail during the negotiation stage when
receiving faxes from Microsoft At Work PC Fax.
