SUMMARY
=======

The following is the complete text of the subscription information that is
included in Encarta 96 Encyclopedia.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Encarta Subscription Information

Even as you read this sentence, our staff is already hard at work
compiling material for next year's Encarta(r) Encyclopedia. Now there's a
convenient, no-risk way to keep current and get the latest, up-to-date
information delivered to your home every year. Automatically. Just sign
up for an Encarta Encyclopedia annual subscription.

HOW TO SUBSCRIBE

Click the Print button and fill out the subscription form. Send it to us
via mail or fax indicating that you want the Encarta Encyclopedia annual
subscription for just US $29.95/CDN $39.95 per year.* If you would
prefer, you can send us an e-mail message and we'll send you back a form
to complete. Just follow the instructions below.

We'll automatically send you each annual version of Encarta Encyclopedia
as soon as it's available. You'll have 30 days to look it over and
decide whether you want to keep it. There will be no more order forms,
and no more gaps in your encyclopedia's information - you'll always have
the latest facts at your fingertips.

TO RESPOND BY ELECTRONIC MAIL

Address your message to enc_sub@microsoft.com (you may have to use a
   special prefix to send this message over the Internet; check with
   your e-mail provider).

In the "Subject" area, type: subscribe.

In the "Message" area, type: subscribe.

We will send you an electronic mail message reply with a form for you
   to fill out.

Simply return the form by electronic mail to the return address
   you'll find in the reply.

TO RESPOND BY PHONE

If you would prefer to order your subscription by phone, please call
800-531-6758.

TO RESPOND BY MAIL OR FAX

Print this form using Encarta Encyclopedia's "Print" function.

Complete the Subscription form (don't forget to indicate whether
   you'd like the Windows or Macintosh version).

Mail to:   Encarta Encyclopedia  Subscription Offer
              P.O. Box 660
              Buffalo, NY 14207-0660

       - or

   Fax to: 716-873-0906  (please do NOT mail your order form after
                          faxing it)
