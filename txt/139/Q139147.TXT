TITLE   :Right Click On Title Does Not Activate Control Menu
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0,95
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Julia Child: Home Cooking with Master Chefs for Windows, version 1.0 
 - Microsoft Encarta 96 Encyclopedia for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you click the Julia Child or Encarta title bar with your right mouse
button, the control menu is not activated.


RESOLUTION
==========

To open the control menu, follow these steps:

1. For Julia Child, move your mouse cursor over the down arrow located on the
   left side of the title bar.

   For Encarta, move your mouse over the Encarta symbol on the left side of the
   title bar.

2. The control menu automatically slides open.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Microsoft Julia Child uses special popup menus instead of the standard Windows
menus.
