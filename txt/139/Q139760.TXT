TITLE   :Baseball 1995: Ballpark Scrolling in Alphabetical Order Only
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Baseball Guide for Windows, 1995 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you scroll through the list of Ballparks, Complete Baseball only scrolls in
alphabetical order.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design and not a problem with Complete Baseball.
