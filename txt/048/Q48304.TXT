TITLE   :Throttle Indicator Displayed Flying Schweizer 2-32

SUMMARY
=======

The Schweizer 2-32 Sailplane mode incorrectly displays a throttle indicator on
the control panel.

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information as it becomes
available.
