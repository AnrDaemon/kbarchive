The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks, versions 2.0, 2.0a, 2.0b, 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

In Microsoft Mail, the keyboard shortcut in the Address window for Cc (carbon
copy) is COMMAND+OPTION+C. The shortcut for Bcc (blind carbon copy) is
COMMAND+OPTION+B.

The COMMAND+C and COMMAND+B key combinations do not perform any commands in the
Address window.

For a complete listing of the Mail keyboard shortcuts, see Appendix A in the
"Microsoft Mail User's Guide."
