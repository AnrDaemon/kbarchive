TITLE   :Close Combat 3: Err Msg: Unable to Get Format on Stream
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Close Combat III: The Russian Front 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to play Microsoft Close Combat III: The Russian Front, you may
receive an error message similar to the following:

   Unable to get format on stream.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur when you computer is unable to show the introduction
video.

RESOLUTION
==========

To turn off the introduction video, follow these steps:

1. At the desktop, press and hold down the CTRL key, and then double-click the
   Close Combat icon.

2. On the General tab, click to clear the Enable Videos check box, and then
   click OK.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For additional information, click the article number below to view the article
in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q176365 Close Combat: Changing Settings Before Starting the Game
