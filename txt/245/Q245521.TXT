DOCUMENT:Q245521  08-AUG-2001  [winnt]
TITLE   :French Accented Characters Are Not Displayed in Uppercase
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:winnt:4.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbhw kbui kbHardware

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 4.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you add the French Multilingual or French Canadian keyboard and you enable
the CAPS LOCK key, some accented characters are not displayed in uppercase.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this problem, press SHIFT to create uppercase accented
characters.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For additional information, click the article number below to view the article
in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q142089 French Grammar Checker Doesn't Recognize Accented Uppercase
