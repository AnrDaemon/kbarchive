TITLE   :Streets and Trips 2000: Errors When You Open Saved Trips or Maps
PRODUCT :Microsoft Automap
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kberrmsg kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to open a saved trip or map in Microsoft Expedia Streets and
Trips 2000, you may receive either of the following error messages:

   Failed to open document.

   Unexpected file format.

After you click OK, the selected trip or map may not be displayed properly on the
screen.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if you attempt to open trips or maps saved in Microsoft
Expedia Streets 98 or Microsoft Expedia Trip Planner 98.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Although you are unable to open trips or maps saved in Expedia Streets 98 or
Expedia Trip Planner 98, you can convert your Expedia Streets 98 and Expedia
Trip Planner 98 pushpin sets for use in Expedia Streets and Trips 2000.

For additional information, click the article number below to view the article in
the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q241101 How to Convert Pushpin Sets from 98 to 2000 Versions
