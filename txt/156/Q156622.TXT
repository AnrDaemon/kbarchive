TITLE   :Replacing NBA Players on Team Removes Them from Database
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbfaq

======================================================================
1.0
WINDOWS
kbusage kbwebcontent

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NBA Full Court Press for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Microsoft NBA Full Court Press allows you to edit team rosters and replace
existing players with other players listed in the NBA database. However, if a
player is replaced by another player, the original team player is permanently
removed.

RESOLUTION
==========

To restore NBA Full Court Press to include its original team rosters, re-install
the game.

Additional query words: 1.00 NBA full court press player team roster database missing
