TITLE   :Error When Printing from Excel or Word with HP Printer Driver
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:97; winnt:4.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 4.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you try to print from Microsoft Excel or Microsoft Word in landscape mode
from a Microsoft Windows NT 4.0-based computer, using the Hewlett-Packard (HP)
LaserJet 8100 PCL6 printer driver, you may receive a STOP 0x1e error message.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, obtain the latest updated printer driver from the
Hewlett-Packard Web site.

For information about how to contact Hewlett-Packard, click the appropriate
article number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K

   Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P

   Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
