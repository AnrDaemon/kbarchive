The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Asheron's Call: Dark Majesty, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the minimum system requirements needed to play Microsoft
Asheron's Call Dark Majesty.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To play Asheron's Call Dark Majesty, you need:

 - Internet connectivity and an active Zone.com membership are required to set
   your Asheron's Call account. A monthly service charge is applicable after
   your trial period ends. Zone membership is subject to the terms of the Zone
   license agreement.

 - Internet access (you are responsible for all charges related to your Internet
   access. Connect time charges may apply).

 - Multimedia PC with Pentium 333 MHz or higher processor.

 - Microsoft Windows 98, Microsoft Windows 2000, Microsoft Windows Millennium
   Edition (Me), or Microsoft Windows XP operating system.

 - DirectX 6.1 API.

 - 64 megabytes (MB) RAM.

 - Microsoft Internet Explorer version 4.0 or later browser software, or
   Netscape 4.0x.

 - 300 MB available hard disk space for typical install, 100 MB of hard disk
   space for swap file.

 - Quad-speed CD-ROM drive or faster.

 - Super VGA (800 x 600) monitor.

 - Super VGA (800 x 600) video card supporting 16-bit color.

 - Microsoft Mouse or compatible pointing device.

 - 28.8 Kbps modem.

 - Sound card.

Optional

 - 3D accelerator card (8 MB or more memory recommended).
