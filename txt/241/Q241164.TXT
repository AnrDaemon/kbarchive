TITLE   :NBA Inside Drive 2000: Lights Flash Behind Spectatators
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, multi-colored lights may flash in
the black area behind the spectators.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a Creative Labs Graphics Blaster Exxtreme video
adapter is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, contact your hardware manufacturer for information about
how to obtain and install the most recent video driver for your video adapter.

For information about how to contact your hardware manufacturer, click the
appropriate article number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge
Base:

   Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K

   Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P

   Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
