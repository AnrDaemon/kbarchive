TITLE   :NBA Inside Drive 2000: Game Is Not Displayed Correctly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv kbhw kbimu msgame kbHardware

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, you may experience any of the
following symptoms:

 - Introductory screens are superimposed over one another.

 - The basket flashes when you play the game.

 - Textures are not applied to objects in the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a video adapter based on the ATI Rage Pro chipset is
installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for your
video adapter from the following ATI Web site:

   http://www.ati.com
