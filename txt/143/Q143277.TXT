TITLE   :No Notification If Monthly Charge Exceeds Specified Amount
PRODUCT :The Microsoft Network
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0,1.05,1.2,1.3,2.0,2.5
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - The Microsoft Network versions 1.0, 1.05, 1.2, 1.3, 2.0, 2.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Although you click the "Notify me when any one charge would exceed" option in
the Online Statement for MSN, The Microsoft Network, and enter an amount, you
may not receive a notification that your monthly charge exceeds that amount.

CAUSE
=====

The option refers to the charge that you incur for any one event, such as
downloading a file, and does not refer to your total monthly charge.
