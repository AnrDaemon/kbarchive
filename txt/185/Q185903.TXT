TITLE   :Kids Plus!: Documentation Error in Paint It! Help
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Kids Products

OPER/SYS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Plus! for Kids, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Paint It! "Adding ClipArt from Outside Paint It!" Help topic states that you
can add a clip art file to Picture Picker. This is incorrect.

To add a picture from another program to a Paint It! file, copy the picture from
the other program, open a Paint It! file, and then click Paste on the Edit
menu.

For information about how to copy a picture, consult the printed documentation or
online Help for the program from which you are copying the picture.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Picture Picker is a utility you can use to add clip art images to your Paint It!
files. To start Picture Picker, click the icon with the yellow circle located on
the toolbar.

For additional information about using clip art in your Paint It! files, see the
"Working with ClipArt" Help topic in Paint It! online Help. To display this Help
topic, click Help Topics on the Help menu, double-click Working With ClipArt,
click the topic you want to see, and then click Display.
