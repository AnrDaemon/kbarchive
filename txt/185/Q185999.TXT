TITLE   :FS98: Location of the Learjet 45 Spoiler Indicator
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbui kbdocerr fs98 kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator 98 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

In Flight Simulator 98, the Aircraft Handbook for the Learjet 45: Cockpit
Orientation incorrectly states that the spoiler indicator is located in the
lower-right corner of the instrument panel.

To display the Learjet 45 spoiler indicator, click Instrument Panel on the Views
menu, and then click Thrust Levers. The spoiler indicator is located on the
Thrust Levers instrument panel displayed in the upper-left corner of the screen.
To close the instrument panel, click Instrument Panel on the Views menu, and
then click Thrust Levers.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The Thrust Levers instrument panel contains the flap, spoiler, and thrust
indicator levers. To extend or retract the spoilers, press the FORWARD SLASH (/)
key.
