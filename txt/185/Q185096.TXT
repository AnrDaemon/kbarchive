SUMMARY
=======

This article describes how to configure your ZoneFriends privacy settings for
Zone.com.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To configure your ZoneFriends privacy settings, follow these steps:

1. On the Options menu in the ZoneFriends screen, click Privacy.

2. Click the privacy setting you want to use. If you click Custom, follow these
   steps:

   a. If you want only specific players to be able to see you, click "People who
      can see me."

      If you want only specific players to not be able to see you, click "People
      who can not see me."

   b. In the Add Person box, type the Zone name for a player you want to be able
      or unable to see you, and then click Add Person. Repeat this step for each
      player you want to add to the list.

3. Click OK.
