TITLE   :OmniPage Pro 2.0 Does Not Display Scanned Image at High Res
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0,3.0a,3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

OmniPage Pro 2.0 may not display a scanned image when using a display driver
with a resolution higher than 800 x 600 x 16.

RESOLUTION
==========

OmniPage Pro version 2.1 corrects this problem.

MORE INFORMATION
================

You may be able to view the scanned image at high resolutions by resizing the
image window. To do this, move the Size Bar at the lower right up. This will
allow you to reduce the image window to one-fourth or one-third of its full
size.

Contact OmniPage Technical Support for information about obtaining OmniPage Pro
version 2.1.

The product included here, OmniPage Pro, is manufactured by a vendor independent
of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this
product's performance or reliability.
