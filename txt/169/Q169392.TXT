DOCUMENT:Q169392  23-DEC-1999  [exchange]
TITLE   :XFOR: LinkAge Connector Does Not Support Sharing Calendars
PRODUCT :Microsoft Exchange
PROD/VER:winnt:5.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbusage

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Exchange Server, version 5.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The LinkAge Notes Connector does not support sharing of calendars and schedules
between Notes and Exchange users.
