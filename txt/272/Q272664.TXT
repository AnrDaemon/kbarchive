TITLE   :Golf 2001 & Links LS: Cannot Start or Game Quits Unexpectedly
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:; :
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdisplay kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Links LS 1999 Edition 
 - Microsoft Golf 2001 Edition 
 - Microsoft Links LS 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to start one of the games listed at the beginning of this
article, the game may not start, or the game may quit unexpectedly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A video adapter based on one of the following chip sets is installed in your
   computer:

    - nVIDIA TNT2 Ultra
    - 3Dfx Voodoo 3

 - The video driver for your video adapter is outdated.

 - Your video adapter is configured to use a True Color (32-bit) color palette
   with a screen resolution of 1600 by 1200 pixels or higher.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, contact the manufacturer of your video adapter to inquire
about how to obtain and install the latest version of the video driver for your
video adapter.

For information about how to contact the manufacturer of your video adapter,
click the appropriate article number below to view the article in the Microsoft
Knowledge Base:

   Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K

   Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P

   Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z

If the issue continues to occur, download and install the latest version of the
reference driver for your video adapter from the appropriate Web site for your
video chip set manufacturer.

nVIDIA TNT2 Ultra:

   http://www.nvidia.com

3Dfx Voodoo3:

   http://www.3dfx.com

NOTE: When you install the reference driver for your video adapter, some of the
features of your video adapter may not work as expected.
