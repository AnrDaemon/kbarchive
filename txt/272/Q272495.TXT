SUMMARY
=======

How to change account information on the MSN Companion system.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To change MSN Companion account information

1. From the MSN Companion start page, click settings in the upper-right corner.

2. On the change my MSN Companion settings page click all settings.

3. On the right side of the page select MSN Internet Access support services and
   billing under the my personal account information category.

4. To change your information, click change information at the top of the page.

5. When you have made the changes:
    - Click the done button to save any changes you made on this page.

    - Click the cancel button to discard any changes you made on this page.

NOTE: When you navigate to the change my account information page, all of your
current information will be filled in, except for your credit card number. For
security reasons, only the last six (6) digits of your credit card will be
displayed.
