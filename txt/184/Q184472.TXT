TITLE   :Zone: ZoneFriends Icon Remains on Taskbar When You Close Browser
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbtool igz kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Zone.com 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you close Zone.com window in your Web browser, the ZoneFriends icon remains
on the taskbar.

RESOLUTION
==========

To quit ZoneFriends, close the ZoneFriends window, right-click the ZoneFriends
icon on the taskbar, and then click Exit.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design. ZoneFriends maintains your connection to the Zone
Messeging Server when you close your Web browser to play a retail or premium
game on the Zone.
