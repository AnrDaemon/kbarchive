SUMMARY
=======

Information about the configuration of a Cisco router can be obtained using the
"show tech-support" command at the privilege EXEC command:

show tech-support

A customer can copy the information that is collected and paste it into e- mail
to aid Microsoft Product Support Services in diagnosing a problem. In no form or
fashion does this command alter the configuration of the router.
