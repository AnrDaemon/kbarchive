TITLE   :NFL Fever 2000: Graphics Are Blurry or Distorted
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame kbFAQ

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, the yardage markers, the logo in the
center of the field, or the players may appear blurred or distorted.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - The video adapter installed in your computer is based on the ATI 3D Rage Pro
   chipset or the NVidia Riva 128/128ZX chipset.

 - Mipmapping is turned on.

 - Filtering is turned off.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this issue, turn Mipmapping off and filtering on.

To turn Mipmapping off:

1. Start NFL Fever 2000.

2. Click Options, and then click Display.

3. Under Visuals, click to select Off to the right of Mipmapping.

4. Click OK.

To turn filtering on:

1. Start NFL Fever 2000.

2. Click Options, and then click Display.

3. Under Visuals, click to select On to the right of Filtering.

4. Click OK.
