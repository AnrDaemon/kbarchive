TITLE   :NFL Fever 2000: Video Adapter Does Not Appear in List of Drivers
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv kbimu msgamekbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, you may experience the following
symptoms:

 - The video adapter installed in your computer does not appear in the list of
   available drivers in the NFL Fever 2000 Display options window.

 - You are only able to play NFL Fever 2000 in software mode.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A Diamond Viper V770 or Diamond Viper V770 Ultra video adapter is installed
   in your computer.

 - You play NFL Fever 2000 on a Microsoft Windows 95-based computer.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For additional information about this issue, please click the article number
below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   Q239872 NBA/NFL 2000: Can Only Play Game in Software Mode
