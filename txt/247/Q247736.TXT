DOCUMENT:Q247736  09-NOV-2001  [homegame]
TITLE   :Fighter Ace II: Plane and Ground Textures Are Not Displayed
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kbdisplay igz kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Fighter Ace II, plane textures and ground textures may
not be displayed.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A Diamond Viper V770 or Diamond Viper V300 video adapter is installed in your
   computer.

 - The video driver installed on your computer is outdated.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for a video
adapter based on the nVIDIA Riva TNT2 or nVIDIA Riva 128 chip set from the
following nVIDIA Web site:

   http://www.nvidia.com/Products.nsf/htmlmedia/software_drivers.html

NOTE: The Diamond Viper V770 video adapter is based on the nVIDIA Riva TNT2 chip
set, and the Diamond Viper V330 video adapter is based on the nVIDIA Riva 128
chip set.
