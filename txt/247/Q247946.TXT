TITLE   :AoE II: Quits Unexpectedly After Playing Opening Movie
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:2.0; :
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kb3rdparty kberrmsg aoe kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
 - Microsoft Age of Empires II Expansion: The Conquerors 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, the program may
quit unexpectedly after playing the opening movie.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if Artisoft i.Share is installed on your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, disable the Artisoft i.Share client:

1. Right-click the i.Share icon on the taskbar.

2. Click Disable i.Share Client

If the issue continues to occur, remove Artisoft i.Share from your computer:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.

3. On the Install/Uninstall tab, click i.Share, and then click Add/Remove.

4. When you are prompted to remove i.Share, click Yes.

5. When you are prompted to restart your computer, do so.
