TITLE   :Fighter Ace II: Black Lines Appear Around Ground Textures
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdisplay igz kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Fighter Ace II, black lines may appear around ground
textures.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - A Hercules Stingray 128/3D video adapter is installed in your computer.

 - Direct 3D hardware acceleration is enabled in Fighter Ace II.

RESOLUTION
==========

To work around this issue:

1. Start Fighter Ace II.

2. In Fighter Ace II, click the Settings tab.

3. Under Display, click Software.

4. Click Fly Now.
