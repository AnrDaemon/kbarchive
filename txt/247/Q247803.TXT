TITLE   :EST2000: Problems Printing Driving Directions with Strip Maps
PRODUCT :Microsoft Automap
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbenv kbprint kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to print driving directions with strip maps in Microsoft
Expedia Streets and Trips 2000, only the first page of output may be printed
properly.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:

 - You are running Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 on a Microsoft Windows
   95-based computer.

 - You print to a Hewlett-Packard 720-series printer.

NOTE: This issue does not occur when you run Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 on a
Microsoft Windows 98-based computer.
