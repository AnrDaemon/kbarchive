DOCUMENT:Q247606  12-NOV-2001  [homegame]
TITLE   :NFL Fever 2000: Unable to Delete a Created Player
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbui kbimu msgame

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, you may be unable to delete a player
that you created in the Create Free Agent window.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the player is not a free agent.

RESOLUTION
==========

To delete a player you created, change the player's status to Free Agent.

To change the player's status to Free Agent and then delete the player:

1. On the Main screen, click Manager.

2. Click the left or right arrow until the player's team appears.

3. Click Trades.

4. Under Position, click the left or right arrow until the player's position
   appears.

5. Under the name of the player's team, click the name of the player you want to
   delete, and then click Trade. The player's name is now listed under Free
   Agents.

6. Under Free Agents, click the name of the player you want to delete, and then
   click Delete Player.

7. When you are prompted to confirm that you want to permanently delete this
   player, click Yes.

8. Click OK.

9. Click Main to return to the Main screen.
