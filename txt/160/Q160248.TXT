TITLE   :Clicking Close Button During MSN Setup Restarts Computer
PRODUCT :The Microsoft Network
PROD/VER:2.00
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - The Microsoft Network version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

If you click the Close button in the upper right corner of the Restart Your
Computer dialog box after installing MSN, The Microsoft Network, your computer
restarts.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design. You must restart your computer to continue.
