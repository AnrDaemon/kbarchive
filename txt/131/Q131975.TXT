TITLE   :SETUP.TXT Missing Information Regarding Diamond Viper PCI
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows NT
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Workstation version 3.51 
 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 3.51 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The technical note for the Diamond Viper PCI video card in the Windows
NT 3.51 Hardware Compatibility List (HCL) states:

   Refer to SETUP.TXT on the Windows NT 3.51 CD-ROM for information on
   configuring this machine/device before installation.

The SETUP.TXT does not include any information regarding the Diamond
Viper PCI video card. It should have included the following
information:

   If you are missing chips in the U44 and U45 sockets of the Diamond Viper PCI
   video card, contact Diamond Computer Systems Technical Support at (408)
   325-7100 for a replacement.


The Diamond Viper PCI video card discussed here is manufactured by
Diamond Computer Systems, a vendor independent of Microsoft; we make no
warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's performance or
reliability.
