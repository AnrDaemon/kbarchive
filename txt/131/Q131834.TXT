TITLE   :Baseball Daily 1995 Downloads Have Ended
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Baseball Guide for Windows, 1995 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Baseball Daily 1995 download program ended with the end of the 1995 Baseball
season. Downloads are not possible after 10/31/95.

From 11/1/95 to 11/15/95, any attempt to download editions can result in a an
error message similar to the following:

   There was a problem downloading the Baseball Daily file to your HD.
   Check available disk space.

From 11/16/95 on, if you try to download Baseball Daily, you receive the
following error message:

   Baseball Daily is not available in the off-season.
   As a result, we cannot complete your call.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Baseball Daily uses your computer's system date to determine if the Baseball
Daily program has ended for the season. After 11/16/95, a pre- programmed
message occurs. Before this system date, Baseball Daily attempt to dial out, and
may give an unexpected error message.
