TITLE   :Mac Encarta 95: Computer Hangs While Playing Video
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1995 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta 95 The Complete Interactive Multimedia Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you open an Apple menu submenu while a video is playing on a Macintosh the
computer stops responding (hangs) and needs to be restarted.


RESOLUTION
==========

Wait for videos to stop playing before accessing Apple menu items.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Encarta 1995 for the Macintosh.
We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the
Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
