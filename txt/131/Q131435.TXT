TITLE   :Mac Encarta 95: Can't Print from Atlas
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::1995 edition
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Encarta 95 The Complete Interactive Multimedia Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Print commands are unavailable (dimmed) when you view the Encarta Atlas.

CAUSE
=====

Encarta does not offer a printing feature while in the Atlas.

WORKAROUND
==========

Atlas information can be copied from Encarta, pasted into another application,
and printed using the following steps:

1. From the Atlas menu, click Copy.

2. Open another program, such as Microsoft Word, and paste in the Encarta
   picture.

3. Print the picture from the new program.

MORE INFORMATION
================

This feature is under review and will be considered for inclusion in a future
release. Printing Atlas information is supported in Encarta 96 and later
versions.
