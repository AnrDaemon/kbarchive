TITLE   :BizWiz Context-Sensitive Help Doesn't Work with Windows
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 3.x Retail Product
PROD/VER::3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The context-sensitive help feature of BizWiz does not work with Microsoft
Windows.
