TITLE   :Relay Gold 5.0 and Gateway Computers
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Microcom's Relay Gold version 5.0 does not connect to a Gateway
computer over the Novell Network, using the newest Novell drivers (IPX
and NETX version 3.1).

Contact Novell for an upgrade.


Additional query words: 3.10 ipx netx 3.11
