DOCUMENT:Q82621  13-DEC-1999  [win95x]
TITLE   :CoolSwitch Window Loses Document Title Switching to Excel
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

When you press ALT+TAB to switch between applications running under Microsoft
Windows operating system version 3.1, and you switch to Excel 3.0, the current
open document is not displayed in the CoolSwitch window.

The CoolSwitch window says, "Microsoft Excel" and should say "Microsoft Excel
C:\DATA\FILENAME.EXT."


Additional query words: 3.10 3.11 3.1
