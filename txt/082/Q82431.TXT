TITLE   :GP Fault in Charisma After Insufficient Memory
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0,3.0a,3.1,3.11
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows versions 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

A general protection (GP) fault or unrecoverable application error (UAE) occurs
with Micrografx Charisma version 2.0 running under Microsoft Windows. The error
occurs immediately after an insufficient memory message under low memory
conditions.
