TITLE   :PRB: Corrupted .vcx or .vct File Causes Visual FoxPro to Hang
PRODUCT :Microsoft FoxPro
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

A corrupted .vcx file causes Visual FoxPro to hang (stop responding).

CAUSE
=====

This usually happens because the associated .vct file, not the .vcx file, is
corrupted.

STATUS
======

Microsoft is researching this behavior and will post new information here in the
Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

Additional query words: VFoxWin
