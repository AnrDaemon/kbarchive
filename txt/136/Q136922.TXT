TITLE   :PRB: Cannot Print Grid Lines
PRODUCT :Microsoft FoxPro
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

There is currently no support for printing the grid or the lines in the grid on
a report or label.

STATUS
======

This behavior is by design.

Additional query words: VFoxWin print
