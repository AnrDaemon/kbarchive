The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Flight Simulator for MS-DOS, version 5.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The following is a summary of functional maximums for the Weather Generation
option in Flight Simulator 5.0:

+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Areas                                       | Maximum              | 
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Maximum Weather areas                       | 3 (including Global) | 
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Maximum Cloud layers per area               | 3                    | 
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Maximum Wind layers per area                | 4                    | 
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Maximum Temperature layers per area         | 4                    | 
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Maximum Barometric pressure layers per area | 1                    | 
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
