TITLE   :Plane Still Flies When Out of Fuel in World War I Ace
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
SUMMARY
=======

When you run out of fuel in the World War I Ace mode, your plane continues to
fly normally.

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
becomes available.

Additional query words: WWI
