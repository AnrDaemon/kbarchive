TITLE   :HOWTO: Obtain Macintosh System Software Updates

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft FoxPro for Macintosh, version 2.6a 
 - Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Macintosh, version 3.0b 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

System software updates for Apple Macintosh systems may be obtained in the
following ways:

 - Through you local dealer. To locate dealers in the US, call 1-800-538-9696.
   To locate dealers in Canada, call 1-800-665-2775.

 - Apple Order Center: 1-800-919-2775.

 - Sun Remarketing: 1-800-821-3221.

 - The Internet.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Information on updates may be obtained from the Apple web site on the Internet
at the following location:

http://www.apple.com

and look for the support link.

Once at this site, please follow the download instructions provided by Apple.
