TITLE   :Gardening: Unable to Add a Plant Name to the Shopping List
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Complete Gardening for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you select a plant in the Plant Selector and click Features to open the
List Maker and select the Shopping List, the selected plant does not appear in
the Shopping List Add text box.

RESOLUTION
==========

Before you open the Shopping List, click the Plant Description tab after you
select a plant

MORE INFORMATION
================

The instructions to the right of the Shopping List screen state:

   To add plant names directly to your list from Plant Selector or Plant
   Description, open Help (press F1) for instructions.

The Shopping List Help topic states the plant variety selected appears in the Add
text box only if the Shopping List is started from Plant Description.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Complete Gardening
version 1.0 for Windows. We are researching this problem and will post new
information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
