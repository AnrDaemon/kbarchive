SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft Encyclopedia of Networking, ISBN
0-7356-0573-4.

The following topics are covered:

 - Page 1303: EMA.ORG URL Is No Longer Valid

MORE INFORMATION
================

Page 1303: EMA.ORG URL Is No Longer Valid
-----------------------------------------

On page 1303, in the definition for Voice Profile for Internet Mail, in the
section "VPIM Information" found under the sub-heading "On the Web", the URL
http://www.ema.org/vpimdir is no longer valid. This should be changed to
http://www.ietf.org/html.charters/vpim-charter.html
(http://www.ietf.org/html.charters/vpim-charter.html).


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
