TITLE   :Train Simulator: Can't Add Coal/Water to Goelsdorf 380 Tender
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER::1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbimu

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

In Microsoft Train Simulator, when you use the Goelsdorf 380 train to refuel the
tender wagon with water or coal, the refueling operation does not take place as
expected.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.

MORE INFORMATION
================

You can successfully refuel if you use the Flying Scotsman train.

Additional query words: msgame trainsim
