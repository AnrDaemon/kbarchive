TITLE   :BBS Status Bar Sometimes Shows Inaccurate Number of Messages
PRODUCT :The Microsoft Network
PROD/VER:1.00 1.05 1.2 1.3
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - The Microsoft Network versions 1.0, 1.05, 1.2, 1.3, 2.0, 2.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you open a bulletin board (BBS), the number of messages listed in the
status bar may be incorrect. The number shown may even be a negative number.

MORE INFORMATION
================

This problem occurs erratically. The next time you enter the BBS, the number of
messages may be correct or may be a different incorrect number.

Additional query words: msn
