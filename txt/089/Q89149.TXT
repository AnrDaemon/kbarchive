TITLE   :ErrMsg: Audio Failed: MIDI File Does Not Exist or Is Invalid
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER::
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kberrmsg kbmm kbsound kbprbkbfaq

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows 
 - Microsoft Bookshelf '95 for Windows 95 
 - Microsoft Bookshelf 1996-97 for Windows 
 - Microsoft Encarta 96 Encyclopedia for Windows 
 - Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Windows 
 - Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia 97 Deluxe for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to play a MIDI music clip in one of the programs listed at the
top of this article, you may experience one or more of the following symptoms:

 - You receive one of the following error messages:

   Audio Failed: MIDI file does not exist or is invalid

   No MIDI device available

 - The cursor flashes for a moment and the program does not play the MIDI music
   clip.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the [MCI] MIDI Sequencer driver is missing, disabled,
or damaged.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To install the [MCI] MIDI Sequencer driver, use the appropriate method for your
version of Microsoft Windows:

Installing MIDI Sequencer in Windows 98
---------------------------------------

To install the MIDI Sequencer device in Microsoft Windows 98, follow these
steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Add New Hardware, and then click Next.

3. When you are prompted to search for plug and play devices, click Next.

4. When you are prompted to search for new hardware devices, click No, and then
   click Next.

5. Under Hardware Types, click Sound, Video And Game Controllers, and then click
   Next.

6. Under Manufacturers, click Microsoft MCI.

7. Under Models, click MIDI Sequencer Device (Media Control), and then click
   Next.

8. Click Finish.

9. Follow the directions on the screen to finish installing the MIDI Sequencer
   device.

Enabling MIDI Sequencer in Windows 98
-------------------------------------

To enable the MIDI Sequencer Device in Windows 98, follow these steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Multimedia.

3. On the Devices tab, double-click Media Control Devices.

4. Click MIDI Sequencer Device, and then click Properties.

5. Click Use This Media Control Device, and then click OK.

6. Click OK.

Installing MIDI Sequencer in Windows 95
---------------------------------------

To install the MIDI Sequencer device in Microsoft Windows 95, follow these
steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Add New Hardware, and then click Next.

3. When you are prompted to search for new devices, click No, and then click
   Next.

4. Under Hardware Types, click Sound, Video And Game Controllers, and then click
   Next.

5. Under Manufacturers, click Microsoft MCI.

6. Under Models, click MIDI Sequencer Device (Media Control), and then click
   Next.

7. Click Finish.

8. Follow the directions on the screen to finish installing the MIDI Sequencer
   device.

Enabling MIDI Sequencer in Windows 95
-------------------------------------

To enable the MIDI Sequencer Device in Windows 95, follow these steps:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Multimedia.

3. On the Advanced tab, double-click Media Control Devices.

4. Click MIDI Sequencer Device, and then click Properties.

5. Click Use This Media Control Device, and then click OK.

6. Click OK.

Installing MIDI Sequencer in Windows 3.x
----------------------------------------

To install [MCI] MIDI Sequencer in Microsoft Windows 3.x or Microsoft Windows for
Workgroups 3.x, follow these steps:

1. In Program Manager, double-click the Main group, and then double-click
   Control Panel.

2. Double-click Drivers.

3. Click Add.

4. Click [MCI] MIDI Sequencer, and then click OK.

5. Follow the instructions on the screen to finish installing [MCI] MIDI
   Sequencer.
