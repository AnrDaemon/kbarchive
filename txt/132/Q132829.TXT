TITLE   :Creative Labs Mixer Controls Not Working in Windows 95
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows 95 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Many of the controls, such as volume, treble, and base, on Creative Mixer
version 4.3 (an audio mixer program that ships with the Creative Labs Sound
Blaster AWE32) work incorrectly in Windows 95, even though the controls work
correctly in Windows version 3.1 or Windows for Workgroups version 3.11.

CAUSE
=====

Creative Labs Windows 3.1 Creative Mixer is not compatible with Windows 95.

RESOLUTION
==========

Contact Creative Labs for an updated version of Creative Mixer.

Creative Mixer is manufactured by Creative Labs Inc., a vendor independent of
Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's
performance or reliability.
