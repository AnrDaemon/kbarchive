======================================================================
SYMPTOMS
========

The "my billing information" page and the "change my billing information" page
do not display the top banner or the buttons at the bottom of the screen.

CAUSE
=====

This is a known issue that the product team is working to resolve.

RESOLUTION
==========

Access "my billing information" page and the "change my billing information"
page though the settings link.

Additional query words: kbimu; MSN Companion
