TITLE   :Adding A Web Page To Favorites

OPER/SYS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - MSN Companion, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

You would like to add a Web page to your Favorites list.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To add a Web page to Favorites

1. When you have the page you want to add to Favorites displayed on MSN
   Companion, click options.

2. Click add this page to my favorites.
