TITLE   :Flt Sim: Enemy Aircraft Fly Very Fast on 386 Computer
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
SUMMARY
=======

The World War I Ace enemy aircraft fly very fast on some 80386 computers. This
can make shooting or chasing the enemy quite difficult.

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
becomes available.
