TITLE   :ASD: Scenery Library Menu Is Blank or Corrupted
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
SUMMARY
=======

A damaged static scenery file (.SC1 file) may corrupt the Scenery Library menu
or cause the menu options to not appear.

Removing the damaged file from the Flight Simulator directory restores all menu
options to normal.
