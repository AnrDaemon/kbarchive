TITLE   :IBM Quietwriter III Shows Incorrect Fonts
PRODUCT :Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:3.1
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows 3.1 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SYMPTOMS
========

The Microsoft Windows operating system version 3.1 IBM Quietwriter III driver
version 1.0 does not show the correct printer-resident device fonts.

Device Fonts the Driver Shows
-----------------------------

 - Courier 12 CPI

 - Courier 17 CPI

 - Prestige Elite 12 CPI

 - Boldface PS

Device Fonts the Printer Actually Has Available
-----------------------------------------------

 - Courier 10 CPI

 - Courier 12 CPI

 - Courier 17 CPI

 - Boldface PS

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in QWIII.DRV version 1.0. We are
researching this problem and will post new information here as it becomes
available.


Additional query words: 3.10 quitwriter printer resident
