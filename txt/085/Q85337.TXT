TITLE   :FAX: Hardware Required of the Fax Gateway PC
PRODUCT :Microsoft Mail For PC Networks
PROD/VER:MS-DOS:3.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Mail Gateway to Fax, version 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Microsoft Mail Gateway to Fax program requires this hardware:

 - Its own workstation on the network

 - A network connection to the MS Mail database

 - A fax board installed

For more information on compatible fax boards, query on these words in the
Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   "fax" (without the quotation marks) and "boards" (without the quotation
   marks) and "Fax Gateway" (without the quotation marks)

If you are using one of the Intel fax boards, the gateway needs a dedicated IBM
PC/XT/AT or 100-percent compatible with at least 512K of memory. If you are
using the GammaLink GammaFax board, the gateway needs an IBM PC or compatible
386 with a minimum 2 MB of memory.

The gateway also needs an outside telephone line.

REFERENCES
==========

The "System Requirements" chapter of the "Microsoft Mail Gateway to Fax
Administrator's Guide"
