SUMMARY
=======

The Fahrenheit 1280 is a video card that is up to 25 times faster than standard
super VGA while running the Microsoft Windows.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The 1280 card supports a maximum interlaced display resolution of 1280 x 1024,
with both interlaced and noninterlaced support at 1024 x 768 and lower. The
Orchid 1280 card also supports up to 32,768 colors in 640 x 480, and memory
options of 512K and 1 MB of VRAM.

The driver supplied with this card has been tested to support the following
programs:

   Windows 3.0, AutoCAD 4.01 and 386, Generic CAD, Microstation, GEM, VersaCAD,
   PCAD, Ventura Publisher, Lotus 1-2-3, Microsoft Word, Word Perfect, Cadvance,
   Cadkey

NOTE: Orchid developed a new driver for Windows 3.1. Contact Orchid Technology
for more information.

The Orchid card is manufactured by vendors independent of Microsoft; we make no
