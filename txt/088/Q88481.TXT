TITLE   :Customizing Sounds in Golf for Windows
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:
OPER/SYS:

======================================================================
SUMMARY
=======

Microsoft does not recommend changing or substituting the .WAV files in
Microsoft Golf. If you choose to add your own sounds, any problems incurred are
your responsibility, and must not be longer than the original sounds and must
meet the 8-bit mono 11.025KHz specifications.
