SUMMARY
=======

This article tells you where to get in touch with user groups and authorized
Microsoft training and education centers around the United States and Canada.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Affinity Publishing, located in Seattle, Washington, publishes the "FoxPro
Resource Guide," which lists third-party products, services, and user group
information. To order a copy of this reference, call Affinity Publishing at
206-281-0089. The cost is $7 per copy.

To find a Solution Provider or Authorized Training Center in your area, please
contact Microsoft at 1-800-765-7768.
