TITLE   :Julia Child: Pressing Return After Find does Not Go to Topic
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:1.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Julia Child: Home Cooking with Master Chefs for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you use the Find command in Julia Child to search for a topic, a list
appears showing the items found. If an exact match is found and you press
RETURN, the program does not jump to the selection.

RESOLUTION
==========

To jump to a topic, do one of the following:

 - Double-click the topic.

   -or-

 - Click the topic one time. When the topic is selected, Click GoTo.
