TITLE   :SMS: Reports on Alpha Remote Admin Console Displays Wrong Time
PRODUCT :Microsoft Systems Management Server
PROD/VER:winnt:2.0
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbsms kbsms200 kbsms200bug

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Systems Management Server version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Crystal Reports that are displayed on a remote SMS Administrator console that is
installed on an Alpha workstation incorrectly report the execution time and
date.

STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
listed at the beginning of this article.
