TITLE   :WD97: Some Words and Synonyms Missing After Installing SR-2
PRODUCT :Word 97 for Windows
PROD/VER::Service Release 2
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kbdta word97

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Word 97 for Windows, version Service Release 2 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

After you install Microsoft Office 97 Service Release 2 (SR-2), some words and
synonyms may not be found, or Word may show different synonyms than you would
expect, when you use the Thesaurus (on the Tools menu, point to Language and
then click Thesaurus).

CAUSE
=====

Microsoft Office 97 Service Release 2 (SR-2) installs and uses new files that
have potentially offensive words and phrases removed from the thesaurus.


Additional query words: 8.0 8.00 dumb oxymoron information utopian
