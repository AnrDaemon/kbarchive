The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Windows NT Server version 3.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

After you configure Windows NT Server version 3.5 with a COPS LTI Appletalk
network card in a single subnet, the following error message may appear:

   Unable to find Seed Router.

RESOLUTION
==========

To correct this problem, make sure that the routing option on the COPS LTI
Appletalk network card is disabled. For more information, call COPS at (404)
840-0810.

The COPS and Appletalk products discussed here are manufactured by vendors
independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding
these products' performance or reliability.
