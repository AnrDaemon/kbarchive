TITLE   :Puzzle Collection: Error Message Received When Hard Disk is Full
PRODUCT :Microsoft Home Games
PROD/VER:WINDOWS:
OPER/SYS:
KEYWORDS:kberrmsg kbsetup

======================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

 - Microsoft Entertainment Pack: The Puzzle Collection 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

If your hard disk is full when you attempt set up The Puzzle Collection, you may
receive one of the following error messages:

   SETUP caused an invalid page fault in module SETUP.EXE at 0137:0041463d

   -or-

   Unable to load language specific dll. Unable to continue.

When you click Close, Setup stops responding and you may have to reboot the
computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

You will need to free up enough space on your hard disk for Setup to run
correctly.

It is recommend that you have at least 30 MB free of hard disk space for a
Typical installation, 5 MB free of hard disk space for a Minimum installation,
and between 5 MB and 30 MB of free of hard disk space for a Custom installation
of The Puzzle Collection.

Additional query words: 1.00 wep w_wep entertainment puzzle collection pac hang general protection fault crash invalid page gpf
