## Q196006: Virtual Globe 99: How to Disable Sound in Virtual Flights

	Article: Q196006
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbsound kbui kbimu
	Last Modified: 19-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta Virtual Globe 99, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes how you can disable sound in the Virtual Flights feature
	of Microsoft Encarta Virtual Globe 99.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To disable sound in Virtual Flights, follow these steps:
	
	1. On the Options menu, click Settings.
	
	2. Click the Play Background Music And Sounds check box to clear it, and then
	  click OK.
	
	NOTE: To re-enable sound in Virtual Flights, click Defaults in the Settings
	dialog box, and then click OK.
