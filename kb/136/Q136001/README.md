## Q136001: Explorapedia: Nature: Tad's Mouth Doesn't Move

	Article: Q136001
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 29-NOV-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Explorapedia series: World of Nature for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	In Microsoft Explorapedia, World of Nature, Tad (the frog who serves as the
	guide) does not move his mouth when he speaks.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	In the CD-ROM title, Microsoft Explorapedia, World of People, Tad does move his
	mouth when he speaks; this is a feature new to World of People.
