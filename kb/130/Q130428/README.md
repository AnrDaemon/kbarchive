## Q130428: Day Starts At and Day Ends At Settings Round Down

	Article: Q130428
	Product(s): Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows
	Version(s): WINDOWS:7.0; Win95:7.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 21-MAY-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows, version 7.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	Changes to the Day Starts At and Day Ends At settings are rounded down to
	the nearest half hour.
	
	You can choose Schedule Settings on the Tools menu to set the Day Starts
	At and Day Ends At options to times that are not on the half hour.
	Microsoft Schedule+ rounds down the selection to the nearest half hour. For
	example, if you select Day Starts At 8:20, the Appointment Book will show
	the start of the day at 8:00. If you choose Schedule Settings again, the
	Day Starts At setting will now be 8:00.
	
	Additional query words: schedule plus 7.00 half-hour
