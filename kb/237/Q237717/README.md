## Q237717: NFL Fever 2000: Slow Frame Rate and Poor Graphics Performance

	Article: Q237717
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-SEP-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, you may experience a slow video frame
	rate and poor graphics performance in the game.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Diamond Monster 3D or a 3Dfx Voodoo II SLI-based video adapter is installed
	  in your computer.
	
	- The video driver for the video adapter installed in your computer is
	  outdated.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for your
	video adapter from the appropriate Web site.
	
	Diamond Monster 3D Video Adapter:
	
	  http://www.diamondmm.com/products/drivers/driver-index.html
	
	3Dfx Voodoo II SLI-based Video Adapter:
	
	  http://www.3dfx.com/view.asp?PAGE=nusV2drivers
