## Q237506: Encarta 2000: Previous Version of Research Organizer Starts

	Article: Q237506
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kberrmsg kbtool kbimu
	Last Modified: 25-JUN-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia 2000 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	If you attempt to open a Sample Project included with Microsoft Encarta Research
	Organizer 2000 from Windows Explorer, you may experience one or more of the
	following symptoms:
	
	- A previous version of Encarta Research Organizer may start and open the
	  Sample Project.
	
	- You may receive the following error message:
	
	  You need a new version of Research Organizer to open this file.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- You install Encarta Research Organizer 2000 without removing a previous
	  version of Encarta Research Organizer.
	
	- The most recent instance of Encarta Research Organizer you started is the
	  previous version of Encarta Research Organizer that is still installed on
	  your computer.
	
	- You attempt to open the Sample Project from Windows Explorer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue:
	
	1. Start Encarta Research Organizer 2000.
	
	2. Under Get Started, click View Sample Projects.
	
	3. Click a sample project, and then click Open.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia
	2000.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, remove any previous versions of Encarta
	Research Organizer installed on your computer after you install Encarta
	Encyclopedia 2000.
