## Q117934: Mac Encarta 94: Small White Spots Appear on Grayscale Printout

	Article: Q117934
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1994 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 24-MAY-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta 1994 The Complete Multimedia Encyclopedia 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you print a 256-color graphic to a grayscale laser printer, small white
	spots may appear on the printout. This occurs if your monitor is set to 256
	colors.
	
	Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the
	Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
	
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To avoid this problem, do one of the following:
	
	- Change your monitor's color bit depth setting to thousands or millions of
	  colors and then print the graphic or article.
	
	  -or-
	
	- Copy the graphic or article to the word processor you use with Encarta, and
	  print it from there.
