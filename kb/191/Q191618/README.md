## Q191618: Urban Assault: Computer Hangs at the End of Movies

{% raw %}

	Article: Q191618
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgamekbfaq
	Last Modified: 21-MAR-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Urban Assault, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play an Urban Assault movie, the movie may play successfully but your
	computer may stop responding (hang) at the end of the movie.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This issue can occur if you have installed the DirectMovie component, available
	for 3Dfx-based video cards.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, disable any third-party software you are using to play
	Urban Assault movies.

	================

	independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding
	these products' performance or reliability.
