## Q156502: Cinemania/Music Central 96: Availability of Monthly Updates

	Article: Q156502
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1996 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 19-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1996 edition 
	- Microsoft Cinemania for the Macintosh 1996 edition 
	- Microsoft Music Central for Windows 1996 edition 
	- Microsoft Music Central for the Macintosh 1996 edition 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	August 1996 is the last monthly update available for Microsoft Cinemania 96 and
	Microsoft Music Central 96. As stated on the product boxes, monthly updates were
	available at no charge through August 31, 1996.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The total of ten Cinemania updates available for downloading were dated November
	1995 to August 1996. The November 1995 update contained reviews for 3 months:
	September, October and November.
	
	The total of eleven Music Central updates available for downloading were dated
	October 1995 to August 1996. The October 1995 update also contained information
	for September.
