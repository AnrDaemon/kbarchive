## Q156775: MSB Dinos: Errors Installing with 8-bit Sound Card

	Article: Q156775
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbtlckbfaq
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores in the Age of Dinosaurs for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you start installing Magic School Bus Explores in the Age of Dinosaurs (MSB
	Dinosaurs), the following message may appear:
	
	  Microsoft Kids Setup
	
	  Program has detected that:
	  your sound card does not support Microsoft Kids Setup
	  11.025 kHz mono, 8-bit
	  11.025 kHz Stereo, 8-bit
	  22.05 kHz mono, 8-bit
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	This problem has been reported with the original Creative Labs Sound Blaster
	(8-bit), ESS ES488 (8-bit), and Media Vision Thunder Board (8-bit) sound cards,
	as well as other older, 8-bit sound cards.
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Magic School Bus Explores in the
	Age of Dinosaurs, version 1.0. Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in
	.
	
	Contact Customer Service at 1-800-426-9400 between the hours of 6:00am and 6:00pm
	Pacific time to obtain a refund or contact your local computer retailer for
	information regarding upgrading your sound card to 16-bit or better.
