## Q84875: Remote-Boot PROM Information

	Article: Q84875
	Product(s): Microsoft LAN Manager
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	
	This article provides information on what remote-boot PROMs (programmable
	read-only memory) are available and where to purchase them.
	
	Lanworks Corporation of Ontario, Canada, manufactures remote-boot PROMs for the
	following cards:
	
	  3Com ELNKII (3C503)
	  3Com ELNK16 (3C507)
	  3Com ELNKMC (3C523)
	  IBM -- Many cards available
	  Novell NE1000
	  Novell NE2000
	  Racal Interlan NI5210
	  Racal Interlan NI6510 (16-bit)
	  Racal Interlan NI9210 (MC)
	  Western Digital -- Most cards available
	
	Customers can phone Lanworks directly for additional information or to purchase a
	remote-boot PROM:
	
	  Lanworks Phone: (416) 238-5528
	  Lanworks Fax: (416) 238-9407
	
	As of approximately March, 1994 the (416) area code will change to (905). At that
	time use the following for contacting Lanworks:
	
	  Lanworks Phone: (905) 238-5528 Lanworks Fax: (905) 238-9407 LANWORKS 2425
	  Skymark Ave Mississagua, ON L4W 4Y6 CANADA
	
	Additional query words: 2.00 2.00c 2.10
