## Q252655: Computer Hangs When You Click Allegiance Zone or Free Zone Game

	Article: Q252655
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbnetwork kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you click Allegiance Zone or Free Zone Game after you start Microsoft
	Allegiance, your computer may stop responding (hang).
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior occurs if your computer is not connected to the Internet.
	
	Your computer connects to the Internet when you start Allegiance. However, the
	Internet connection may take a short amount of time to be established.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, wait for your computer to connect to the Internet before
	you start Allegiance.
	
	To confirm that you are connected to the Internet, attempt to connect to the
	following Zone.com Web site:
	
	  http://www.zone.com/allegiance
