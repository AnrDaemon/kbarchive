## Q146225: Pregnancy and Child Care: Content Specific to US Market

	Article: Q146225
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:95
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 01-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview release on MSN, The Microsoft
	Network (MSN) was created for the United States (US) marketplace. If you log
	into MSN from another part of the world, please be aware that much of the
	information in this service is specific to the US.
