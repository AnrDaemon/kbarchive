## Q161756: WD97: Comment Inserted at Unexpected Location

{% raw %}

	Article: Q161756
	Product(s): Word 97 for Windows
	Version(s): WINDOWS:97
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbualink97 kbdta
	Last Modified: 14-NOV-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	You experience one of the following symptoms:
	
	Case 1
	------
	
	When you attempt to insert a comment at the beginning of a paragraph, the comment
	is inserted after the first word of the paragraph.
	
	Case 2
	------
	
	When you attempt to insert a comment at the beginning of a sentence within a
	paragraph, the comment is inserted after the first word of the sentence.
	
	Case 3
	------
	
	When you attempt to insert a comment on a blank line,
	
	- the comment is inserted after the first word of the following sentence or
	  paragraph.
	
	  -or-
	
	- the comment is inserted at the end of the preceding paragraph that contains
	  text.
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	Place the insertion point within or at the end of a paragraph that contains
	text, and then insert the comment.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the products listed at the
	beginning of this article.
