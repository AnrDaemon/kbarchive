## Q281329: MechWarrior 4: Auto Torso Centering Feature May Not Engage

	Article: Q281329
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 4.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui kbimu
	Last Modified: 13-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft MechWarrior 4: Vengeance, version 4.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft MechWarrior 4: Vengeance, you may not be able to engage
	the Auto-Torso Centering feature.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur when your mech moves at 40 kilometers per hour (kph) or
	faster.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To use the Auto-Torso Centering feature, you need to slow down your mech to less
	than 40 kph in the game.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To toggle the Auto-Torso Centering feature on or off, press the SEMICOLON (;)
	key on your keyboard.
	
	To force the mech to center automatically, press the SPACEBAR, or to center the
	mech manually press SHIFT+SPACEBAR.
