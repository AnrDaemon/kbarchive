## Q281432: MSN Companion: International Questions

	Article: Q281432
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.0,2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Companion, versions 1.0, 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Some customers may inquire whether MSN Companion is available outside of the
	United States.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	- Explain to the customer that (1) MSN Companion is currently offered only
	  within the United States and (2) we may exploring international support
	  sometime in the future.
