## Q281526: Browsing and Downloading Questions: MSN Companion

{% raw %}

	Article: Q281526
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.0,2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Companion, versions 1.0, 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	- The customer wants to download files such as games, music, images, or videos.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	- The device has no local storage for items downloaded from the Internet.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	- At this time, MSN Companion does not support download files or the storage of
	  files.

	================
