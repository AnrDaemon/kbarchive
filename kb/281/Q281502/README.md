## Q281502: IA-2 MSN Companion Starting Screen

	Article: Q281502
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.0,2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Companion, versions 1.0, 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	- During Start up, the system passes by the "COMPAQ" logo and halts at the MSN
	  Starting screen or at a white screen. Unplugging and plugging the device back
	  in does not clear the problem. The customer can reproduce this issue 100
	  percent of the time.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	- The device is permanently damaged and must be returned.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	- Instruct the customer to return the device to the point of purchase and
	  exchange it for a new device.

	================
