## Q66842: ASD: 747 Panel Displays Wrong Checklist

	Article: Q66842
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	When flying with the 747 enhanced instrument panel, an incorrect checklist or
	procedure may display at times. The checklists operate by trying to determine if
	you are currently attempting to land, take off, taxi, etc.
	
	If an unwanted checklist displays, press SHIFT+Y repeatedly until the correct
	checklist displays.
	
	If the highlighted action appears incorrect, press SHIFT+Z to reset the checklist
	based on current conditions.
