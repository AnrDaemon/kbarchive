## Q66834: ASD: Using 747 Panel Distance Measuring Equipment (DME)

	Article: Q66834
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	To use the distance measuring equipment (DME) on the 747 panel, press SHIFT+TAB
	until the NAV1-NAV2-DME display appears. The DME panel shows your distance from
	the VOR and your speed toward or away from the VOR. Below the DME gauge is an
	indicator of which navigation radio is being used to measure for distance. Press
	B to toggle between NAV1 and NAV2.
