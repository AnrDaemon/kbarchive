## Q219731: Golf 99: Swing Animation Does Not Match the Shot You Play

	Article: Q219731
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 09-MAY-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Golf 1999 Edition, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Golf 1999 Edition, the animation for a swing may not
	match the shot you play.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Golf 1999 Edition,
	version 1.0.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	When you play a full-swing shot from the edge of a green, Golf 99 may play the
	animation for a partial swing. However, the ball travels the full distance for
	the shot.
