## Q306292: Microsoft PowerPoint Version 2002 Step by Step Comments and Corr

{% raw %}

	Article: Q306292
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 28-AUG-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Microsoft PowerPoint Version 2002 Step by Step ISBN 0-7356-1297-8 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft PowerPoint Version 2002 Step by
	Step, ISBN 0-7356-1297-8.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- Page 222: Change Text Of Objective
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Page 222: Change Text Of Objective
	----------------------------------
	
	On page 222, under "Playing Sounds and Movies in a Slide Show," the objective
	should be changed from:
	
	"In this exercise, you add sound to a slide transition, insert a sound into a
	slide, insert and play a movie, and change the movie effect options and actions
	settings."
	
	To:
	
	"In this exercise, you modify the animation or action settings, adjust the play
	options, and change the animation order."
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
