## Q157834: WD97: Applying Bullets and Numbers Removes Indents

{% raw %}

	Article: Q157834
	Product(s): Word 97 for Windows
	Version(s): WINDOWS:97
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 14-NOV-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you do one of the following, any indentation applied to the paragraph is
	removed:
	
	- Apply bullets to an indented and numbered paragraph.
	
	  or
	
	- Apply numbering to an indented and bulleted paragraph.
	
	  or
	
	- Remove bullets of an indented and bulleted paragraph.
	
	  or
	
	- Remove numbering of an indented and bulleted paragraph.
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	To work around this problem, reapply the indent after you apply the bullets or
	numbering format.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products listed at
	the beginning of this article.
