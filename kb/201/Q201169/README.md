## Q201169: WD97: Tabs May Be Removed Running Spelling and Grammar Check

{% raw %}

	Article: Q201169
	Product(s): Word 97 for Windows
	Version(s): WINDOWS:97
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdta word8 word97
	Last Modified: 14-NOV-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you change the spelling of a word within the Spelling and Grammar dialog
	box (in the Not in Dictionary box) and then click Change, tabs at the beginning
	of the sentence that contains the misspelling are removed.
	
	NOTE: To check the spelling of your document, click Spelling and Grammar on the
	Tools menu.
	
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	Make the correction to the misspelling using one of the following methods:
	
	Method 1: Use Suggestions
	-------------------------
	
	1. On the Tools menu, click Spelling and Grammar.
	
	2. In the Spelling and Grammar dialog box, click to select the correct spelling
	  of the misspelled word in the Suggestions box.
	
	3. Click Change.
	
	Method 2: Use the Shortcut Menu
	-------------------------------
	
	After Word flags a misspelled word with the wavy red underline, right-click the
	misspelled word. On the shortcut menu that appears, click to select the correct
	spelling of your word.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
	listed at the beginning of this article.<A0>This problem was corrected in Microsoft
	Word 2000.
