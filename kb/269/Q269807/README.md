## Q269807: MSB: Computer Hangs When You Eject The Magic School Bus CD-ROM

	Article: Q269807
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): mskids kbimu
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Scholastic's The Magic School Bus in Concert 
	- Scholastic's The Magic School Bus Lands on Mars 
	- Scholastic's The Magic School Bus Discovers Flight, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's The Magic School Bus Whales and Dolphins, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus Series: Volcanoes, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	If you eject the Microsoft Scholastic's The Magic School Bus in Concert CD-ROM
	or the Microsoft Scholastic's The Magic School Bus Lands on Mars CD-ROM while
	the program is running, you may experience any of the following symptoms:
	
	- You may receive an "Invalid Page Fault" error message.
	
	- Your computer may stop responding (hang).
	
	- Your computer may become unstable.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, do not eject the program CD-ROM while the
	program is running.
	
	If you need to eject The Magic School Bus CD-ROM, quit the program, and then
	eject the CD-ROM.
