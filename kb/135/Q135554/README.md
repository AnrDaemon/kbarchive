## Q135554: U.S Robotics Courier V.34 Fax/Modem Not Detected

	Article: Q135554
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 17-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows 95 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	Windows 95 may not detect the U.S. Robotics Courier V.34 fax/modem installed in
	your computer.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	Windows 95 does not detect the modem because of the modem's outdated onboard
	Intel flash BIOS.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Obtain an updated flash BIOS dated later than 7/5/95 from U.S. Robotics.
