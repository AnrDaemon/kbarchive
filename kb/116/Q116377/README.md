## Q116377: WFWG 3.11: Fax Machines That Are Incompatible with AWFAX

	Article: Q116377
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 3.x Retail Product
	Version(s): WINDOWS:3.11
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 06-NOV-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The following fax machines are incompatible with Microsoft At Work PC Fax:
	
	- Hitachi HiFax 17
	
	- Omnifax 9s Series
	
	- Omnifax G90 Series
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Hitachi HiFax 17
	----------------
	
	The Hitachi HiFax 17 fails during the negotiation stage and generates an
	"Undeliverable Mail" error message.
	
	
	Omnifax 9s Series and G90 Series
	--------------------------------
	
	Both of the above Omnifax machines fail during the negotiation stage when
	receiving faxes from Microsoft At Work PC Fax.
