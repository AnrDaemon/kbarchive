## Q301513: Ads Do Not Appear When Going Online From Offline Mode With &quot;My S

	Article: Q301513
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 6.1
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 13-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network version 6.1 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	A customer views e-mail in offline mode in MSN Explorer 6.1.
	The customer minimizes the "My Stuff" bar, and then goes online.
	The ads that normally appear in the "My Stuff" bar do not appear, even if the
	customer maximizes the bar after going online.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	Known issue.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	This issue will be resolved in a later release of MSN Explorer.
