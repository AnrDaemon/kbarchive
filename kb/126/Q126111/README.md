## Q126111: Baseball 1994: Boston Team Franchise Dates Not Consistent

{% raw %}

	Article: Q126111
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1994 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbbuglist
	Last Modified: 11-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Complete Baseball for Windows, version 1994 edition 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The franchise dates for the Boston Red Sox are different in the Team History and
	the Team Roster screens.
	
	The Team History screen lists the franchise dates as:
	
	  Boston Red Sox 1908-
	  Boston Americans 1901-1907
	
	The Team Rosters screen lists the franchise dates as:
	
	  Boston Americans 1901-1906
	  Boston Red Sox 1907-
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Steps to Reproduce Problem
	--------------------------
	
	1. Open Microsoft Complete Baseball and from the Main screen, choose Teams.
	
	2. Choose Boston Red Sox as the team to view. Either choose Change Team and type
	  or select the appropriate team and choose OK, or choose the up or down arrow
	  next to Change Team and select Boston Red Sox.
	
	3. Select the Team History option and note the caption:
	
	  Boston Red Sox 1908-
	  Boston Americans 1901-1907
	
	4. Change to the Team Rosters screen and note the date information in the
	  Franchise History section:
	
	  Boston Americans 1901-1906
	  Boston Red Sox 1907-
	
	Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the
	Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
