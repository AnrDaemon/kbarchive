## Q323938: Train Simulator: Windshield Wipers Not Visible from Inside Cab

	Article: Q323938
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 27-JUN-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	While using Train Simulator, you may not be able to see the windshield wipers
	from inside the cab.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	The windshield wipers are a feature on the GP38 and KIHA 31 locomotives only,
	and the windshield wipers are only visible from outside the locomotives. The
	windshield wipers are not visible from inside the cab on any locomotives.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To activate the windshield wipers on the GP38 and KIHA 31 locomotives only,
	press V.
