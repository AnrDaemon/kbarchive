## Q159255: Spike's World: How Do I Send Spike E-Mail?

{% raw %}

	Article: Q159255
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbfaq
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Spike's World, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Spike's World has several links so that you can send e-mail (suggestions and
	comments) to the Spike's World product team. Some comments and interesting facts
	will be posted on the site for everyone to see.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To send mail, just find a place somewhere on a page where the e-mail link is
	located, and click on it. You'll get an e-mail form to send your thoughts and
	comments.
