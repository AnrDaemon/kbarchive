## Q159258: Spike's World: How Do I Get My High Score Uploaded?

	Article: Q159258
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbfaq
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Spike's World, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Some of the featured games in Spike's World have a high score upload ability
	built into the game. When you play the game, even offline, the game keeps track
	of your highest score.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The next time you come back to Spike's World, we can tell if you have a high
	score and we'll ask if you want to upload it to the space. If you say yes, the
	score is uploaded to the Spike's World site. You can view it at the High Scores
	Page.
