## Q247724: Fighter Ace: Matrox Millennium G-400 Video Adapter Not Available

	Article: Q247724
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay igz kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 09-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you select the Direct 3D display setting in Microsoft Fighter Ace II, the
	Matrox Millennium G400 video adapter may not be available, even though the
	Matrox Millennium G400 video adapter is installed in your computer.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the video driver installed on your computer is
	outdated.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the
	Matrox Millennium G400 video adapter from the following Matrox Web site:
	
	  http://www.matrox.com/mga/drivers/home.htm
