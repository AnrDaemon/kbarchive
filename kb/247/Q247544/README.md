## Q247544: Understanding Electronic Commerce Comments and Corrections

	Article: Q247544
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 12-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Understanding Electronic Commerce ISBN 1-57231-560-1 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Understanding Electronic Commerce, ISBN
	1-57231-560-1.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- Author Biography: Author's E-mail Address Has Changed
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Author Biography: Author's E-mail Address Has Changed
	-----------------------------------------------------
	
	The e-mail address listed in the author biography in the back of the book is out
	of date. The last sentence of the author biography should read:
	
	"He can be contacted at dkosiur@tbg.com."
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
	
	Additional query words: EXECBK 1-57231-560-1
