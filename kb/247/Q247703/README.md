## Q247703: Fighter Ace II: Hangs When You Click &quot;Fly Now&quot;

{% raw %}

	Article: Q247703
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbui igz kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 09-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you click Fly Now in Microsoft Fighter Ace II, the program may stop
	responding (hang).
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Diamond Viper V330 video adapter is installed in your computer.
	
	- The video driver installed on your computer is outdated.
	
	- Direct 3D hardware acceleration is enabled in Fighter Ace II.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the
	Diamond Viper V330 video adapter from the following Diamond Web site:
	
	  http://www.diamondlegacy.com/products/drivers/driver-index.htm
	
	To work around this issue, disable 3D hardware acceleration in Fighter Ace II:
	
	1. Start Fighter Ace II.
	
	2. Click the Settings tab.
	
	3. Under Display, click Software.
	
	4. Click Fly Now.
