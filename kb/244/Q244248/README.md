## Q244248: AOE: The Rise of Rome: List of Campaigns and Scenarios

{% raw %}

	Article: Q244248
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): aoe kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires Expansion: The Rise of Rome, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Microsoft Age of Empires: The Rise of Rome includes scenarios for four new
	campaigns you can play in single-player mode. You can choose to play these
	scenarios in five increasing levels of difficulty, ranging from Easiest to
	Hardest.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The following list contains the campaigns and associated scenarios that are
	included in Age of Empires: The Rise of Rome.
	
	Expansion 1: Rise of Rome
	-------------------------
	
	- Birth of Rome
	- Metaurus
	- Mithridates
	- Pyrrhus of Epirus
	- Syracuse
	- Zama
	
	Expansion 2: Ave Caesar
	-----------------------
	
	- Alesia
	- Britain
	- Caesar vs Pirates
	
	- Caesar vs Pompey
	
	Expansion 3: Pax Romana
	-----------------------
	
	- Actium
	- Coming of the Huns
	- Ctesiphon
	- Queen Zenobia
	- Year of the four Emperors
	
	Expansion 4: Enemies of Rome
	----------------------------
	
	- Crossing the Alps
	- Odenathus vs Persians
	- Spartacus
	- Third Greek War
