## Q310701: Midtown Madness: Game Quits Unexpectedly After Startup Movie

	Article: Q310701
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 29-JAN-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Midtown Madness, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you attempt to start Microsoft Midtown Madness, the game may quit
	unexpectedly after the startup movie finishes.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a third-party vehicle has been added to the Midtown
	Madness folder.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, use the following methods, as necessary, in the order
	presented.
	
	Remove Third-Party Vehicles
	---------------------------
	
	Remove all third-party vehicles from the Midtown Madness folder to another
	folder, and then try to start Midtown Madness. If the issue persists, use the
	following method.
	
	Uninstall and Reinstall
	-----------------------
	
	To uninstall and then reinstall Midtown Madness, follow these steps:
	
	1. In Windows Explorer, browse to the Midtown Madness folder.
	
	2. Right-click the Players folder, point to Send To, and then click My
	  Documents.
	
	3. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	4. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.
	
	NOTE: Because there are several versions of Windows, the following steps may be
	different on your computer. If they are, please consult your product
	documentation to complete these steps.
	
	5. On the Install/Uninstall tab, click Microsoft Midtown Madness, and then click
	  Add/Remove.
	
	6. When prompted to remove Midtown Madness, click Yes, and then click OK.
	
	7. Insert the Midtown Madness CD-ROM into your computer's CD-ROM or DVD-ROM
	  drive.
	
	8. Click Install, click Continue, click Accept, and then click Continue.
	
	9. In the Path box, change the suggested path for installation.
	
	10. Follow the prompts to complete the installation, and then click Reboot, or
	  click Exit if Reboot is not an option.
	
	11. In Windows Explorer, copy the Players folder from My Documents to the new
	  Midtown Madness folder. When prompted to replace the existing folder, click
	  "Yes to All".
