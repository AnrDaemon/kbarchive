## Q310400: Microsoft Outlook Version 2002 Step by Step Comments and Correct

{% raw %}

	Article: Q310400
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 12-OCT-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Microsoft Outlook Version 2002 Step by Step ISBN 0-7356-1298-6 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft Outlook Version 2002 Step by
	Step, ISBN 0-7356-1298-6.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- Page xiii: Two Files Missing From Step 5
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Page xiii: Two Files Missing From Step 5
	----------------------------------------
	
	Step 5 on page xiii is missing two filenames. Change:
	
	"NextShow, OrderStatus, NewSupplier, ReNextShow, Schedule, Kick-off, Status, and
	TeamMeet."
	
	To:
	
	"NextShow, OrderStatus, NewSupplier, ReNextShow, Schedule, Kick-off, Status,
	TeamMeet, TaskLayout, and TaskPayments."
	
	To follow the instructions on page 173, these files must be in the Inbox, so
	should be installed in Step 5.
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
