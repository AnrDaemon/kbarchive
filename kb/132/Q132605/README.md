## Q132605: Channels Reversed on Sound Galaxy Washington 16 Sound Card

	Article: Q132605
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 17-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows 95 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play MIDI files on an Aztech Sound Galaxy Washington 16 sound card, the
	left and right channels may be reversed.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This is a known problem with the Aztech Sound Galaxy Washington 16 sound card.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	For information about possible resolutions to this problem, please contact the
	vendor who provided the sound card before you contact Aztech.
