## Q132822: Some US Robotics Modems May Not Dial Long Numbers

	Article: Q132822
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbhw win95 msnetwork kbHardware
	Last Modified: 24-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows 95 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you use Phone Dialer to dial a long telephone number (greater than 35
	digits), all of the digits may not be dialed.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	You are using a US Robotics Sportster 28.8 modem that does not dial more than 35
	digits.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	For a short-term workaround, manually dial the telephone number. Then, contact
	US Robotics for a firmware upgrade for your modem.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The US Robotics Sportster is manufactured by US Robotics, a vendor independent
	of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this
	product's performance or reliability.
