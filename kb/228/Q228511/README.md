## Q228511: Baseball 2000: Screen Is Redrawn Several Times During Game Play

	Article: Q228511
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbui kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 17-APR-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Baseball 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Baseball 2000, the graphics on the screen may be redrawn
	several times during the game.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a Matrox Millennium G200 video adapter is installed
	in your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	You may be able to resolve this issue by selecting a lower display resolution in
	Baseball 2000. To do this, follow these steps:
	
	1. Start Baseball 2000.
	
	2. On the Home Plate screen, click Options.
	
	3. Click the Graphics tab.
	
	4. Under Resolutions Available, click a lower display resolution.
	
	5. Click OK.
