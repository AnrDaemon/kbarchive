## Q228526: Baseball 2000: Field Radar Is Not Displayed Correctly

	Article: Q228526
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbui kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 21-APR-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Baseball 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Baseball 2000, the field radar may not be displayed on
	the screen or may not be displayed correctly.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a video adapter based on the 3Dfx Voodoo or Voodoo2
	chipset is installed in your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for your
	video adapter from the following 3Dfx Web site:
	
	  http://www.3Dfx.com
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	If a video adapter based on the Voodoo2 chipset is installed in your computer,
	you can also resolve this issue by disabling Gouraud Shading. To do this, follow
	these steps:
	
	1. Start Baseball 2000.
	
	2. On the Home Plate screen, click Options.
	
	3. Click the Graphics tab.
	
	4. Click to set the Gouraud Shading option to Off, and then click OK.
