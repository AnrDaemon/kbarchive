## Q228447: Baseball 2000: Shredded or Torn Graphics in Game

	Article: Q228447
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 16-APR-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Baseball 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Baseball 2000, the graphics in the game may appear
	shredded or torn on the screen.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Creative Labs 3D Blaster Banshee video adapter is installed in your
	  computer.
	
	- You are using version 4.10.01.0108-6.232 of the Voodoo Banshee Reference
	  driver.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the
	Creative Labs 3D Blaster Banshee video adapter from the following Creative Labs
	Web site:
	
	  http://www.creaf.com
	
	To work around this issue, disable DirectX 6 support. To do this, follow these
	steps:
	
	1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	2. Double-click Display.
	
	3. On the Settings tab, click Advanced.
	
	4. On the Blaster Control tab, click Advanced.
	
	5. Click to clear the Enable DirectX 6 support check box.
