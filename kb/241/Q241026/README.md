## Q241026: NBA Inside Drive 2000: Text Appears Blocky or Distorted

	Article: Q241026
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbenv kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 01-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, the text on the screen may appear
	blocky or distorted.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if font smoothing is enabled on your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, follow the steps in the appropriate method for your
	version of Microsoft Windows.
	
	Microsoft Windows 98
	--------------------
	
	1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	2. Double-click Display.
	
	3. On the Effects tab, click to clear the "Smooth edges of screen fonts" check
	  box, and then click OK.
	
	Microsoft Windows 95 with Microsoft Plus! 95
	--------------------------------------------
	
	1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	2. Double-click Display.
	
	3. On the Plus! tab, click to clear the "Smooth edges of screen fonts" check
	  box, and then click OK.
