## Q241019: NBA Inside Drive 2000: Court Lines Disappear When Playing Game

	Article: Q241019
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbenv kbhw kbimu msgame kbHardware
	Last Modified: 25-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, the court lines may unexpectedly
	disappear.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- A video adapter based on the ATI Rage Pro chipset is installed in your
	  computer.
	
	- The Stadium Detail setting in NBA Inside Drive 2000 is set to Low.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, set the Stadium Detail setting to Medium:
	
	1. On the NBA Inside Drive 2000 startup screen, click Options.
	
	2. On the Performance tab, click Medium under Stadium Detail.
	
	3. Click OK.
	
	Additional query words: 1.00 nba2000 nba2k basketball missing
