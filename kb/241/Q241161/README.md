## Q241161: NBA Inside Drive 2000: Reflected Light Appears as White Patches

{% raw %}

	Article: Q241161
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbgraphic kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 10-SEP-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you start Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, reflected light may appear as
	white patches on the stadium floor.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a Creative Labs Graphics Blaster 3D video adapter is
	installed in your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, set the Stadium Detail setting in NBA Inside Drive 2000
	to Medium. To do this:
	
	1. On the NBA Inside Drive 2000 startup screen, click Options.
	
	2. Click the Performance tab.
	
	3. Under Stadium Detail, click Medium.
	
	4. Click OK.
