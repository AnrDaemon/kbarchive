## Q241175: NBA 2000: Shadow of Basketball Does Not Appear Properly

{% raw %}

	Article: Q241175
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbui kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 01-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, the shadow of the basketball may
	appear as a black circle inside a white box.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, disable shadows in NBA Inside Drive 2000:
	
	1. On the NBA Inside Drive 2000 startup screen, click Options.
	
	2. Click the Performance tab.
	
	3. Under Shadow Type, click None.
	
	4. Click OK.
	
	Additional query words: 1.00 msgame nba2000 nba2k basketball square
