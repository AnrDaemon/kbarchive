## Q186024: MTM2: Trucks Are Black and Black Boxes Surround Scenery Objects

	Article: Q186024
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame mtm2kbfaq
	Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Monster Truck Madness 2, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Monster Truck Madness 2, the trucks may be black and scenery
	objects may be surrounded by black boxes.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if 3D hardware acceleration is enabled and the video
	driver installed in the computer is out of date or cannot correctly map the
	game's shadow and texture display features.
	
	This issue has been reported with the following display adapters:
	
	- Matrox Millennium II
	- STB Nitro 3D
	- Diamond Stealth 2000
	
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, contact your hardware manufacturer to inquire if an
	updated video driver is available.
	
	For information about how to contact your hardware manufacturer, please see the
	following articles in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:
	
	  Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K
	
	  Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P
	
	  Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
	
	
	To determine the manufacturer and model of the display adapter installed in the
	computer, follow these steps:
	
	1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	2. Double-click System.
	
	3. On the Device Manager tab, click View Devices By Type.
	
	4. Double-click Display Adapters to expand the branch.
	
	5. Note the manufacturer and model of the display adapter listed in the Display
	  Adapters branch.
