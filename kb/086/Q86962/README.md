## Q86962: X400: How to Obtain CCITT X.400 &amp; X.25 Red Books

{% raw %}

	Article: Q86962
	Product(s): Microsoft Mail For PC Networks
	Version(s): MS-DOS:3.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Mail Gateway to X.400, version 3.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	All Consultative Committee for International Telephone and Telegraph (CCITT)
	X.400, X.200, and X.25 1984 recommendations can be obtained from the following
	source:
	
	  United Nations Bookstore
	  Room GA 32B
	  New York, NY 10017
	
	Some training programs also offer the sale of the Red Book recommendations.
