## Q242794: AoE II: List of Enhancement (Cheat) Codes

	Article: Q242794
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 06-AUG-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains a list of enhancement (cheat) codes for Age of Empires II:
	The Age of Kings.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The following table lists the cheat codes available in Age of Empires II, and
	their corresponding effects:
	
	  
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Cheat code              | Effect                                             | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Polo                    | Turn off the fog of war.                           | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Marco                   | Reveal the entire map.                             | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Aegis                   | Toggle instant buildings for all players.          | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Rock On                 | You receive 1000 stone.                            | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Lumberjack              | You receive 1000 wood.                             | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Robin Hood              | You receive 1000 Gold.                             | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Cheese Steak Jimmy's    | You receive 1000 Food.                             | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Natural Wonders         | You control the animals.                           | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Resign                  | You are defeated instantly.                        | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | WimpyWimpyWimpy         | You are destroyed instantly.                       | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | I Love The Monkey Head  | You receive a tall, fast-moving, useless villager. | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | How Do You Turn This On | You receive a Shelby Cobra with twin machine guns. | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Torpedox                | Opponent x is destroyed instantly.                 | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | To Smithereens          | You receive a Saboteur unit.                       | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | Black Death             | All opponents are destroyed instantly.             | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	  | I R Winner              | You win instantly.                                 | 
	  +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	
	To use a cheat code in a single player game, press ENTER, type the code in the
	Chat box, and then press ENTER.
	
	NOTE: These codes do not work in a multiplayer game unless the host clicks to
	select the Allow Cheats check box when he or she configures the game.
	
	NOTE: The How Do You Turn This On and I Love the Monkey Head cheat codes do not
	work in Campaigns.
