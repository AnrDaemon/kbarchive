## Q242874: Age of Empires II: Pointer Blinks on Windows 2000-Based Computer

	Article: Q242874
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0; :
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbhw kbimu msgame kbHardware
	Last Modified: 25-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II Expansion: The Conquerors 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings on a Microsoft Windows
	2000-based computer, the pointer may blink or flash.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:
	
	- Your video adapter is based on the 3Dfx Voodoo 3 chipset.
	
	- You need to install an updated video driver for your video adapter.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, contact your hardware manufacturer to inquire about how
	to obtain and install the latest version of the video driver for your video
	adapter.
	
	For information about how to contact your hardware manufacturer, click the
	appropriate article number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge
	Base:
	
	  Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K
	
	  Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P
	
	  Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
	
	To work around this issue, on the startup screen, click Options, and then select
	a lower Screen Size setting.

	================

	independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding
	these products' performance or reliability.
