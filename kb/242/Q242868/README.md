## Q242868: AoE II: Graphic Issues or Pointer Disappears

{% raw %}

	Article: Q242868
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv kbimu
	Last Modified: 29-JUN-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you run Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, you may experience any of the
	following behaviors:
	
	- The bottom border of the game screen may be distorted or tinted pink.
	
	- You may be unable to play the game at a screen resolution of 1024 x 768
	  pixels.
	
	- The pointer may disappear.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:
	
	- An Intergraph Intense3D 100 video adapter is installed in your computer.
	
	  -and-
	
	- The video driver for the Intense3D 100 video adapter is outdated.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this behavior, download and install the latest version of the video
	driver for your video adapter. To do so, visit the following Intergraph Web
	site:
	
	  http://www.intergraph.com
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	This issue does not occur with version 4.3.0.3133 or later of the Intense3D 100
	video driver.
