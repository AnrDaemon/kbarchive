## Q163384: NBA: Patches with Updated Rosters Available on Web

	Article: Q163384
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbfile
	Last Modified: 21-DEC-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NBA Full Court Press for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	NBA Full Court Press game patches are now available on the World Wide Web.
	
	You can download either one of the two following patches if you have the full
	United States (US) version of NBA Full Court Press installed on your computer:
	
	NOTE: Do not install either patch if you are using an International version of
	NBA Full Court Press.
	
	- The Exeratin.exe Game Patch (with 95-96 Rosters) includes the 1995-96 NBA
	  season player and team rating changes.
	- The Exerost.exe Game Patch (with 96-97 Rosters) includes the 1996-97 NBA
	  season player and team rating changes.
	
	Both patches also contain the following:
	
	- Animated nets on made shots.
	- Correct resetting of the 24-second clock after all defensive fouls and
	  illegal defenses.
	- Less frequently occurring lob passes.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The following files are available for download from the Microsoft Download
	Center:
	
	  Exeratin.exe
	  (http://download.microsoft.com/download/nbafullcourtpresswin95/Update/1/WIN98/EN-US/Exeratin.exe)
	
	  Exerost.exe
	  (http://download.microsoft.com/download/nbafullcourtpresswin95/Update/2/WIN98/EN-US/Exerost.exe)
