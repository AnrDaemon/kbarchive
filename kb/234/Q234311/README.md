## Q234311: PRB: Microsoft Word 2000 At A Glance Comments And Corrections

{% raw %}

	Article: Q234311
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 04-MAY-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Microsoft Word 2000 At a Glance ISBN 1-57231-940-2 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft Word 2000 At A Glance, ISBN
	1-57231-940-2.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- Page 53: Missing text in step 1
	
	- Page 114: Missing Text In Step 1, Second Procedure
	
	- Page 123: Missing Text In Step 2 Of Second Procedure
	
	- Page 159: Incorrect Step In Second Procedure
	
	- Page 192: Need Additional Information In Step 2
	
	- Page 300: Missing Information In Step 7
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Page 53: Missing text in step 1
	-------------------------------
	
	In the lower procedure's step 1, add the following text after "want to replace":
	"point to Synonyms,..."
	
	
	Page 114: Missing Text In Step 1, 2nd Procedure
	-----------------------------------------------
	
	In step 1 of the second procedure, Outline view may already be turned on.
	
	Change step 1 to read:
	"Start a new document, using the appropriate template, and switch to Outline view
	if it's not already turned on."
	
	
	Page 123: Missing Text In Step 2 Of 2nd Procedure
	-------------------------------------------------
	
	In the first bulleted entry of step 2 in the second procedure, replace the text
	"a number" with "the character's ANSI code".
	
	This item should read:
	"Hold down the Alt key and use the numeric keypad when keys are in the form
	Alt+0xxx, where x is the character's ANSI code."
	
	
	Page 159: Incorrect Step In Second Procedure
	--------------------------------------------
	
	The wording in step 2 of second procedure is incorrect. Change:
	
	"Click a cell boundary. To delete several cell boundaries, drag the Eraser
	pointer to include all cell boundaries. If you accidentally delete the wrong
	boundary, click the Undo button on the Standard toolbar."
	
	To:
	
	"Click a cell boundary to delete it. If you accidentally delete the wrong
	boundary, click the Undo button on the Standard toolbar."
	
	
	Page 192: Need Additional Information In Step 2
	-----------------------------------------------
	
	Change step 2 from:
	"Press Enter to insert a new paragraph."
	
	To:
	"Press Enter to insert a new paragraph, and then click in the new paragraph."
	
	
	Page 300: Missing Information In Step 7
	---------------------------------------
	
	Change step 7 from:
	"Click OK."
	
	To:
	"Click OK if you don't want to insert additional information, or leave the dialog
	box open to add custom information."
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
	
	Additional query words: 1-57231-940-2 EUBOOK EUAAG
