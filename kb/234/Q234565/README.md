## Q234565: PowerPoint 2000 At a Glance Comments and Corrections

{% raw %}

	Article: Q234565
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 09-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Microsoft PowerPoint 2000 At a Glance ISBN 1-57231-944-5 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft PowerPoint 2000 At a Glance, ISBN
	1-57231-944-5.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- Page x: Misspelling of "straightforward"
	
	- Page 3: Misspelling of "Section"
	
	- Page 6, 7, 9 and 181: Need to show new dialog box tab for Office 97 templates
	
	- Page 49: Dialog box tabs have changed
	
	- Page 120: Misspelling of "accommodate"
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Page x: Misspelling of "straightforward"
	----------------------------------------
	
	In second paragraph, 3rd line down, correct spelling of "straightforward" (it's
	now spelled staightforward).
	
	
	Page 3: Misspelling of "Section"
	--------------------------------
	
	In first line of 4th full paragraph in the left column, change "Sections" 10 to
	"Section" 10.
	
	
	Page 6, 7, 9 and 181: Need to show new dialog box tab for Office 97 templates
	-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	Screen shots should show the new dialog box tab, Office 97 Templates.
	
	
	Page 49: Dialog box tabs have changed
	-------------------------------------
	
	The Advanced tab has been eliminated.
	
	
	Page 120: Misspelling of "accommodate"
	--------------------------------------
	
	Change "accomodate" to "accommodate" in line 5 of the left text column.
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
