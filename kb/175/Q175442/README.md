## Q175442: The Internet Connection Wizard May Not Work When Installing MSN

	Article: Q175442
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbsetup kbtool
	Last Modified: 12-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you install MSN, The Microsoft Network, the installation may stop while the
	Internet Connection wizard is running.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the Internet Connection wizard is damaged.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this behavior, create a new MSN account. To do so, use the following
	steps:
	
	1. Click Start, and then click Run.
	
	2. In the Open box, type "signup" (without quotation marks), and then click OK.
	
	3. On the MSN Sign-In screen, click OK.
	
	4. In the Your Area Or City Code box, type your telephone area code. In the "The
	  first three digits of your phone number" box, type your telephone prefix, and
	  then click OK.
	
	5. Click Connect, and then follow the prompts on the screen to create your new
	  account. If you cannot connect with your new account, contact MSN Member
	  Support. To do so, see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge
	  Base:
	
	  Q142365 Member Support Phone Numbers for The Microsoft Network
