## Q175343: Age Of Empires: List of Enhancement (Cheat) Codes

{% raw %}

	Article: Q175343
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbusage aoe
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Age of Empires enhancement codes allow you to increase your stockpiles, enable
	your villagers to construct buildings quickly, and allow you to resign from the
	game. The following article lists the enhancement codes.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To use the enhancement (cheat) codes, press the ENTER key on your keyboard, and
	then type the code word in the Chat text box.
	
	Enhancement Code     Function
	----------------     --------
	
	Pepperoni Pizza      Increases your stockpile of food by 1000.
	
	Coinage              Increases your stockpile of gold by 1000.
	
	Woodstock            Increases your stockpile of wood by 1000.
	
	Quarry               Increases your stockpile of stone by 1000.
	
	Steroids             Turns on Quick Build.
	
	DieDieDie            Destroys all enemy buildings and units.
	
	Hari Kari            Destroys all of your buildings and units.
	
	Home Run             Lets you instantly win a scenario.
	
	Resign               Resigns you from the game.
	
	Zeus                 Makes your forces invisible to the enemy.
	<PARTNER>
	http://computerunderground.mycomputer.com/files/PC/1.html
	</PARTNER>
	
	No Fog               Removes the fog of war.
	
	Reveal Map           Reveals the whole map.
	
	Bigdaddy             Creates a car with a rocket launcher.
	
	Gaia                 Makes you the planet player (you control the animals).
	
	Kill<x> where x is   Kills the player with the starting position <x>.
	a number from 1-9
	
	ICBM                 Gives Ballistas a range of 100.
	
	Medusa               Turns killed Villagers into Black Riders.
	
	Black Rider          Turns killed Black Riders into a Catapults.
	
	Dark Rain            Disguises idle foot archers as trees.
	
	Hoyohoyo             Increases Priests speed to 6 and hit points to 600.
	
	Flying Dutchman      Turns Juggernaughts into Flying Dutchmen.
	
	Big Bertha           Gives Catapults a range of 16 and a damage area of 10.
	
	Jack Be Nimble       Changes the appearance of your catapult's missile to a
	                    villager, a cow, or a villager wearing a cape.
	
	E=MC2 Trooper        Creates a Nuke Trooper with a missile.
	
	Photon Man           Creates a Nuke Trooper with a laser.
