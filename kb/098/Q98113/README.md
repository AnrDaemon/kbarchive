## Q98113: Playing as Guest in Tri-Peaks Does Not Register High Score

	Article: Q98113
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-JUN-1999
	
	kbusage
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows Entertainment Pack, volume 3 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	If you play as "Guest" in the Tri-Peaks program, it doesn't register your high
	score in the high score table. "Guest" is the default player name, which is
	meant to be used as a temporary player and will not register scores in the high
	score table.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Workaround
	----------
	
	Use a name other than "Guest" if you want your high scores to register in the
	high score table.
	
	Additional query words: 1.00 TriPeaks name Tri Peaks Hi Score
