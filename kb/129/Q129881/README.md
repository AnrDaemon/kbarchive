## Q129881: Baseball '95: Houston Astros Invalid Opposing Team Reference

	Article: Q129881
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 11-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Complete Baseball Guide for Windows, 1995 edition 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The Houston Astros July 9, 1995 opponent is the San Diego Padres. The Baseball
	Guide schedule incorrectly abbreviates the team name as PAD. The correct
	abbreviation is SD.
	
	The abbreviation is correct on all other schedules.
