## Q134525: Placing Briefcase Inside Another Briefcase Causes Problems

	Article: Q134525
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 17-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows 95 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	If you place one Briefcase inside another, the following problems may occur:
	
	- The Briefcase that is inside the other Briefcase looks like a normal folder.
	
	- You cannot drag files into the Briefcase that is inside the other Briefcase.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	Windows 95 does not support placing a Briefcase inside another Briefcase.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Move the Briefcase that is inside the other Briefcase out of the other
	Briefcase. If the Briefcase that is inside the other Briefcase looks like a
	normal folder, first click it and then click Split From Original on the
	Briefcase menu.
