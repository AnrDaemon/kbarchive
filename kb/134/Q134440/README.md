## Q134440: Road Atlas 3.x ErrMsg: NBAMWV3 caused a GPF in AMW.EXE

	Article: Q134440
	Product(s): Microsoft Automap
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 25-AUG-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Automap Road Atlas for Windows, versions 3.0, 3.01, 3.02 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you click a small town after you have zoomed-in to a small area of the map,
	you may receive the error message:
	
	  NBAMWV3 caused a GPF in AMW.EXE.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Automap Road Atlas versions 3.0,
	3.01, and 3.02 for Windows.
	
	The problem has been corrected in Microsoft Automap Road Atlas version 3.04 for
	Windows.
