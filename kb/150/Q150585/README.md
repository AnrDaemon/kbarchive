## Q150585: Pregnancy and Child Care: Limits of the Back Feature

	Article: Q150585
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:95
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 01-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	The Back button in Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care does not return you to the
	Find By Symptom or Find By Word features.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Click the Find button to return to the Find By Symptom or Find By Word features.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The Back feature allows you to navigate through previously displayed screens of
	Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, but is not designed to return you to the
	Find By Symptom or Find By Word features.
