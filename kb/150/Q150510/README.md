## Q150510: Pregnancy and Child Care: Find Highlight is Inconsistent

{% raw %}

	Article: Q150510
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 16-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	When you use the Find By Word feature, the queried words are may not highlighted
	consistently in the retrieved article.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	When you enter multiple words, there is an implicit logical AND between them.
	Therefore, if the overview or article does not contain both word A AND word B,
	neither word is highlighted.
	
	
	Additional query words: preview multi media multimedia multi-media childcare mmtitles kbmm msnet
