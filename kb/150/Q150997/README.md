## Q150997: Opening Multiple Online Viewer Titles May Cause Hang

	Article: Q150997
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.2,1.3
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 13-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 1.2, 1.3 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	If you attempt to open more than one online viewer (MediaView) title
	simultaneously, your computer may stop responding (hang).
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To free your computer, press CTRL+ALT+DELETE, and then click End Task.
	
	To prevent the problem, open only one online viewer title at a time.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in The Microsoft Network versions
	1.2 and 1.3. We are researching this problem and will post new information here
	in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
