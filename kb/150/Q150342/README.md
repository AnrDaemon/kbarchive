	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	To get a list of the scene topics in Explorapedia:
	
	1. Click the scene you want to go to from the spaceship.
	
	2. After you are in the scene, click Tad.
	
	3. Click "See all of the topics that are in this scene."
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Listed below are the topics found in Explorapedia, the World of Nature, broken
	down by each screen you encounter outside of the spaceship. You can also click
