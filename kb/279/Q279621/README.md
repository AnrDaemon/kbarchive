## Q279621: MechWarrior 4 Err Msg: Nested Exception! Cause: EnterWindowMode

	Article: Q279621
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbenv kberrmsg kbimu
	Last Modified: 22-NOV-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft MechWarrior 4: Vengeance 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft MechWarrior 4: Vengeance, you may receive any of the
	following error messages:
	
	   - Nested Exception! Cause: 'EnterWindowMode'.
	
	   - STOP: FAILED (0x8876024e - DDERR_UNSUPPORTEDMODE).
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- MechWarrior 4 is installed on a Microsoft Windows 2000-based computer.
	
	- Your screensaver has started.
	
	  -or-
	
	  You pressed CTRL+ALT+DELETE to lock your workstation.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, turn off your screensaver before you start
	the game, and quit the game before you press CTRL+ALT+DELETE.
