## Q240988: Pandora's Box: Program Hangs When You Quit the Game

	Article: Q240988
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgamekbfaq
	Last Modified: 09-SEP-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Pandora's Box, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you quit Microsoft Pandora's Box, you may experience any of the following
	symptoms:
	
	- Your computer may stop responding (hang).
	
	- Images on the screen may become distorted.
	
	- You may be unable to restart Pandora's Box.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Hercules Stingray 128/3D video adapter is installed in your computer.
	
	- An outdated version of the Hercules Stingray video driver is installed on
	  your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest version of the Hercules
	Stingray 128/3D video driver from the following Hercules Web site:
	
	  http://www.hercules.com/support/drivers
