## Q193426: Virtual Globe: Statistics Are Not Listed in Alphabetical Order

	Article: Q193426
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbtool kbui kbimu
	Last Modified: 19-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta Virtual Globe 99, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The statistics that are available in the World Compare feature of Encarta
	Virtual Globe 99 are not listed in alphabetical order.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	This behavior is by design.
	
	The statistics that are available in the World Compare feature of Encarta Virtual
	Globe 99 are sorted by their internal article ID number instead of by
	alphabetical order.
