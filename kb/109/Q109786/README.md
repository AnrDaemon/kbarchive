## Q109786: Mac Art Gallery/Wine Guide: Sound May Break or Not Play

	Article: Q109786
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): MACINTOSH:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Art Gallery for the Macintosh, version 1.0 
	- Microsoft Wine Guide for the Macintosh, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	Sounds may play badly or not at all when you are playing animations in Art
	Gallery or Wine Guide. This problem can occur if some or all of the following
	conditions exist:
	
	- You are using a relatively slow Macintosh.
	
	- You do not have much free memory.
	
	- You are using a relatively slow CD-ROM drive.
	
	- The animation is complex or large.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To minimize this problem:
	
	1. Close Art Gallery.
	
	2. Free some memory by exiting any other applications or utilities that may be
	  running.
	
	3. Restart Art Gallery.
	
	4. Try running the Art Gallery animation again.
	
	For additional information, please see the following article in the Microsoft
	Knowledge Base:
	
	  Q99809 Sounds Are Choppy When Virtual Memory Is Turned On
