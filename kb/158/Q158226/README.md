## Q158226: Encarta 97: Personal Nutrition Interactivity Does Not Save

	Article: Q158226
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1997 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbhowto
	Last Modified: 24-MAY-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
	- Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Windows 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you enter data in the Personal Nutrition Interactivity in Encarta
	Encyclopedia, your data is not saved if you go to another article, or shift the
	main focus away from the Interactivity.
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	There is no way to save your personal data in Encarta Encyclopedia. However, you
	can copy your data into a word processor, spreadsheet, or any other text capable
	application (i.e., Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, or Microsoft Exchange).
	
	To copy your Personal Information to another program, do the following:
	
	1. On the Personal Nutrition drop-down menu (the triangle pointing down, located
	  in the upper left corner of the Personal Nutrition window), click Copy Data.
	
	2. Open your word processor, spreadsheet, or other program.
	
	3. On the Edit menu, click Paste to paste the copied data.
