## Q158656: Mac Encarta 97: Print Output Differs From Expected

	Article: Q158656
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1997 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 24-MAY-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you print from Encarta 97 for the Macintosh, the printout looks different
	than what you would expect.
	
	The following are some possible scenarios:
	
	- The right side of page is cut off.
	
	- Some or all of the words are printed in extremely light gray, but generally
	  the copyright is not affected.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Make sure you are using the correct printer driver for your printer. If
	necessary, use the corresponding section below to resolve the problem.
	
	Right Side of Page Cut Off
	--------------------------
	
	- Adjust the printer margins to enable all of the text to print.
	
	  -or-
	
	- Adjust printer settings so that the page prints in landscape mode.
	
	Words Print Extremely Light (or not at all)
	-------------------------------------------
	
	- Adjust printer settings to print in Black and White mode.
	
	  -or-
	
	- Copy the content and paste into SimpleText or another word processor capable
	  of accepting graphics and text. Print the items from this program.
