## Q158087: Mac Encarta 97: No Sound in Collages or MindMaze

	Article: Q158087
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1997 edition; MACINTOSH:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbfaq
	Last Modified: 24-MAY-2001
	
	1997
	MACINTOSH
	kbsound kbusage kbmm kbfaq
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
	- Microsoft Encarta 98 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play the MindMaze game in Encarta Encyclopedia, no background music
	plays.
	
	  - or -
	
	When you go to the Collage section of Encarta 97, you do not hear any theme music
	on a 68K Macintosh.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	This is by design. Encarta 97 is not designed to play background music in
	MindMaze on any Macintosh. The program also does not play theme music in the
	Collages on a non-Power Macintosh (680x0 processor).
	
	Only Macintoshes with a PowerPC chip play music in the Collages.
