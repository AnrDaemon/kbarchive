## Q274625: Combat Flight Simulator 2: Poor Video Performance

	Article: Q274625
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbenv kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2, you may experience poor video
	performance in the game. The poor performance may include the following
	symptoms:
	
	- The frame rates may be slow.
	
	- The screen may appear to stutter.
	
	- The program may appear to stop responding (hang).
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if all of the following conditions are true:
	
	- The nVidia 6.18 Detonator 3 reference video driver is installed on your
	  computer.
	
	- A Creative Annihilator II GeForce 2 video adapter is installed in your
	  computer.
	
	- The game is configured to play in Hardware mode.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver available
	from the manufacturer of your video adapter at the following Creative Labs Web
	site:
	
	  www.creativelabs.com
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	nVidia releases reference drivers for their chip sets. These reference drivers
	may not be appropriate for all video adapters that are based on those chip
	sets.
	
	For more information about the reference drivers available from nVidia, visit the
	following nVidia Web site:
	
	  www.nvidia.com
