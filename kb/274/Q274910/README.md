## Q274910: CFS2: Corsair Maximum Airspeed Is Slower Than Real Aircraft

	Article: Q274910
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbenv kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The maximum airspeed of the Corsair, model F4U-1A, in Combat Flight Simulator 2:
	WWII Pacific Theater may be slower than the speed that the Corsair was actually
	capable of flying.
	
	In Combat Flight Simulator 2, the Corsair can achieve a maximum airspeed of 395
	miles per hour (mph), but the maximum airspeed of the Corsair has been reported
	to be 417 mph.
