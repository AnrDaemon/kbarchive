## Q274677: Crimson Skies: Description of Enhancement (Cheat) Codes

	Article: Q274677
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 22-OCT-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Crimson Skies 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes the enhancement (cheat) codes in Microsoft Crimson Skies.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Crimson Skies includes two cheat codes. One cheat code allows you to select any
	mission within the game, and the other cheat code adds the clippings for all of
	the missions (except your own screen shots) to the scrapbook.
	
	To access all missions in Crimson Skies:
	
	1. On the Main Menu screen in Crimson Skies, click Campaign.
	
	2. Click your player name in the list of player names, or type a new player
	  name, and then click Continue.
	
	3. Click the microphone on the left side of the Cabin screen, and then type
	  "idaho" (without the quotation marks).
	
	4. In the box in the top right corner of the screen, click the mission that you
	  want to fly.
	
	To add the clippings for all of the missions to the scrapbook:
	
	1. On the Main Menu screen in Crimson Skies, click Campaign.
	
	2. Click your player name in the list of player names, or type a new player
	  name, and then click Continue.
	
	3. On the Cabin screen, click Previous Missions.
	
	4. Click the Rogue Pilots & Co. logo in the bottom left corner of the
	  scrapbook, and then type "ispy" (without the quotation marks).
	
	5. Click View All Missions.
