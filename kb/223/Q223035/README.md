## Q223035: Zone.com: How to Access Your Account Information

{% raw %}

	Article: Q223035
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui igz kbimu msgamekbfaq
	Last Modified: 30-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Zone.com 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes how to access your Zone.com account information.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To access your Zone.com account information, follow these steps:
	
	1. Click the following link to connect to Zone.com:
	
	  http://zone.msn.com
	
	2. Click Support.
	
	3. Click My Zone.
	
	Alternatively, you can go directly to the "My Zone" page at the following Web
	address:
	
	  https://zone.msn.com/services/MyZone.asp
	
	This page lists the following information:
	
	- MSN Nickname in use
	- Email address on file with the Zone
	- MSN Newsletter subscriptions
	- Zone Profiles and Ratings
	- Banning Status
	- Game Subscriptions and Expiration dates
	- Billing Services and Help
