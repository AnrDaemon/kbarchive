## Q178002: How to Report Unsolicited E-mail Messages to MSN Member Services

	Article: Q178002
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.5,2.51,2.52,2.6,5.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbmsn
	Last Modified: 22-SEP-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 2.5, 2.51, 2.52, 2.6, 5.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes how to report unsolicited or commercially-oriented e-mail
	messages to MSN, The Microsoft Network, Member Services.
	
	NOTE: Sending unsolicited or commercially-oriented e-mail messages to MSN members
	is a violation of the MSN Membership Agreement.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To report unsolicited or commercially-oriented e-mail messages to MSN Member
	Services, use the following steps:
	
	1. Go to the Member Services Help & Support site at:
	
	  http://memberservices.msn.com/us/default_help.asp
	
	2. Select E-mail Member Support from the table of contents on the left, and then
	  click Report a Problem with Another Member.
	
	3. Continue to follow the directions on the screen to submit your complaint.
