## Q302725: &quot;Must restart computer&quot; Message When Signing In To MSN Explorer

{% raw %}

	Article: Q302725
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When signing in to MSN Explorer, you may get a message telling you that you must
	restart your computer. You have the option to "Tell MSN" or "Don't Tell MSN".
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This is a known issue.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Shut down MSN Explorer, restart your computer, and then re-start MSN Explorer.
	Note: If you click "Tell MSN" and cannot shut down MSN Explorer as a result,
	then choose option "Don't Tell MSN".
