## Q302563: Multi-User Associated Accounts

	Article: Q302563
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 21-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Companion, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	You have an account that becomes locked. You then attempt to create a new
	account and want to associate previously associated accounts to the new account.
	The associated accounts fail to respond.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	You cannot "disassociate" accounts that were previously associated with a locked
	account.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Contact Customer Service to have the accounts disassociated.
	
	Additional query words: kbimu; MSN Companion
