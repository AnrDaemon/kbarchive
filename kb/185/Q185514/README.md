## Q185514: Age of Empires: List of Campaigns and Scenarios

	Article: Q185514
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbref aoe kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 26-MAY-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Microsoft Age of Empires includes scenarios for four different campaigns you can
	play in single-player mode. You can choose to play these scenarios in five
	increasing levels of difficulty, ranging from Easiest to Hardest.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The following list contains the campaigns and their associated scenarios
	included in Age of Empires.
	
	Ascent of Egypt Learning Campaign
	---------------------------------
	
	- Dawn of a New Age
	- Skirmish
	- Farming
	- Trade
	- Crusade
	- River Outpost
	- Naval Battle
	- A Wonder of the World
	- Siege In Canaan
	
	Glory of Greece
	---------------
	
	- Land Grab
	- Citadel
	- Ionian Expansion
	- Trojan War
	- I'll Be Back
	- Siege of Athens
	- Xenophon's March
	- Wonder
	
	Voices of Babylon
	-----------------
	
	- Holy Man
	- Tigris Valley
	- Lost
	- I Shall Return
	- The Great Hunt
	- The Caravan
	- Lord of the Euphrates
	- Nineveh
	
	Yamato Empire of the Rising Sun
	-------------------------------
	
	- The Assassins
	- Island Hopping
	- Capture
	- Mountain Temple
	- The Canyon of Death
	- Oppression
	- A Friend in Need
	- Kyushu Revolts
