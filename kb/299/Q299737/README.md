## Q299737: Train Simulator: Sound Disabled After Restoring from Standby

{% raw %}

	Article: Q299737
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Train Simulator on a portable computer (laptop), the
	sound may be disabled after you restore the game from Standby mode.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To work around this issue, quit and then restart the game.
