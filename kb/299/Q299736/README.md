## Q299736: Train Simulator: Vehicles Do Not Have Operating Headlamps

	Article: Q299736
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you operate any train in Microsoft Train Simulator, and you change the time
	setting from day to night, no headlamps are turned on as expected in any of the
	various vehicles on the side roads.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
	listed at the beginning of this article.
