## Q299739: Train Simulator: Compass Not Visible in Cab View

	Article: Q299739
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	The compass may not be visible in cab view on the Scotsman train.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To view the compass, press and hold SHIFT+1.
	
	Additional query words: msgame trainsim
