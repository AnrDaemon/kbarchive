## Q151011: Pregnancy: Text, &quot;Click here...&quot; Prints at Bottom of Articles

	Article: Q151011
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:95
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 01-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you print an entire article from Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, the
	following text message prints at the bottom of the article.
	
	  Click here for Extra information
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Pregnancy and Child
	Care, Preview on MSN. We are researching this problem and will post new
	information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	To print the article contents without the text message, copy the article into a
	word processor (such as Microsoft WordPad), edit the article to remove the
	unwanted text. Then, print the article from the word processor.
	
	To accomplish this process, do the following:
	
	1. On the toolbar, click the Options button on the toolbar, and then click Copy.
	
	2. Click the All Text button, then click OK.
	
	3. Open a word processor such as Microsoft WordPad (provided with Windows 95).
	
	4. On the Edit menu, click Paste to paste the article into the word processor.
	
	5. Edit the article to remove the unwanted text, and then print the article from
	  the word processor.
