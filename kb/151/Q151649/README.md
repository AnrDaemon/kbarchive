## Q151649: Bookshelf '96-'97: See Also Scroll Bars Missing

	Article: Q151649
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 16-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Bookshelf 1996-97 for Windows 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you click the See Also button after you open an article, the See Also list
	window appears, but the scroll bars are missing.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to scroll up or down the list. The HOME and
	END keys will take you to the top or bottom of the list, respectively.
