## Q151736: No Message Displayed If Mailbox Temporarily Unavailable

	Article: Q151736
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.2,1.3,2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 13-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 1.2, 1.3, 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you sign in to MSN, The Microsoft Network, you may receive a message
	stating that you have new mail. However, when you use Remote Mail to update
	headers or Deliver Now to deliver mail, headers may not be updated or mail may
	not be delivered.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	Your mailbox on MSN may be temporarily unavailable.
	
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Try again later to retrieve your mail.
