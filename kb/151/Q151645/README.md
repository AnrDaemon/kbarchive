## Q151645: Pregnancy and Child Care: Multiple Radio Buttons Selected

	Article: Q151645
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 16-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	More than one of the radio buttons in the Find By Symptom tab is selected.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This condition occurs when you press and hold the mouse button on one of the
	radio buttons then switch to another application without releasing the mouse
	button. For example, the condition occurs when you do the following:
	
	1. With your mouse, click the radio button next to "Newborn." to select it.
	
	2. Point your mouse on the radio button next to "Infant (1-12 months)."
	
	3. Press and hold the left mouse button.
	
	4. Without releasing the mouse button, switch to another program. To switch to
	  another program, press and hold down the ALT key while you press the TAB key
	  (ALT+TAB).
	
	5. Use the ALT+TAB key combination to return to Pregnancy and Child Care.
	
	"Newborn" and "Infant (1-12 months)" are selected.
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	Before switching to another application, press and release the mouse button on
	the radio button.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Pregnancy and Child
	Care, Preview on MSN. We are researching this problem and will post new
	information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To reset the radio buttons, exit, then restart the Pregnancy and Child Care.
