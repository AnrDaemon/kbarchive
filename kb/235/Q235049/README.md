## Q235049: Quick Course in Microsoft Publisher 2000 Comments and Correction

	Article: Q235049
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 07-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Quick Course in Microsoft Publisher 2000 ISBN 1-57231-990-9 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Quick Course in Microsoft Publisher 2000,
	ISBN 1-57231-990-9.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- LOC Page: Name And Address Of Online Press Have Changed
	
	- Pages 5,7: Graphics No Longer Match Dialog Box
	
	- Page 34: Graphics Are Incorrect
	
	- Page 74: Step 4 Incomplete
	
	- Page 87: Step 1 Is Incorrect
	
	- Page 96: Corrections To Steps 1 And 2 Of Procedure
	
	- Page 108: Incorrect Cross-Reference
	
	- Page 121: Customizing Forms Procedure Correction
	
	- Page 151: Adjusting The Page Setup Procedure Correction
	
	- Page 164: Creating A Web Site Procedure Correction
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	LOC Page: Name And Address Of Online Press Have Changed
	-------------------------------------------------------
	
	Online Press has merged with another company and now has a new name and address.
	Please replace the text in green on the LOC page with the following text:
	
	"A Quick Course Education/Training Edition for this title is published by Online
	Training Solutions, Inc. (OTSI). For information about supplementary workbooks,
	contact OTSI at 15422 Bel-Red Road, Redmond, WA 98052, USA. 1-800-854-3344.
	E-mail: quickcourse@otsiweb.com."
	
	
	Pages 5,7: Graphics No Longer Match Dialog Box
	----------------------------------------------
	
	A new button has been added to the Publisher Catalog dialog box since the first
	printing of the book.
	
	
	Page 34: Graphics Are Incorrect
	-------------------------------
	
	On page 34, due to last-minute changes in the software, the screen shots may not
	precisely match the dialog boxes that you will see.
	
	
	Page 74: Step 4 Incomplete
	--------------------------
	
	Please add the following sentence to the end of step 4:
	
	"Then click Update Display."
	
	
	Page 87: Step 1 Is Incorrect
	----------------------------
	
	Please change the last sentence in step 1 to read the following:
	
	"Then double-click the graphic placeholder (the pyramid) to display the Clip
	Gallery, click the All Categories button, and click the Navigation Controls
	category."
	
	
	Page 96: Corrections To Steps 1 And 2 Of Procedure
	--------------------------------------------------
	
	Page 96, Step 1, second sentence:
	
	Change:
	"Then double-click Waves Newsletter on the Publications By Wizard tab..."
	
	To:
	"Click Newsletter, and then double-click Waves Newsletter on the Publications By
	Wizard tab..."
	
	Page 192, Step 2:
	
	Replace Step 2 with the following text:
	"Answer the wizard's questions and respond to any messages, selecting the Tropics
	color scheme, a 2-column format, a customer address placeholder, and a
	double-sided layout for the publication. Then click Finish."
	
	
	Page 108: Incorrect Cross-Reference
	-----------------------------------
	
	Page 108, Step 4, first sentence:
	
	Change the cross-reference from:
	"(see page 80)"
	
	To:
	"(see page 84)"
	
	
	Page 121: Customizing Forms Procedure Correction
	------------------------------------------------
	
	Page 121, Step 2, fourth sentence:
	
	Change:
	"Then hold down the left mouse button, and drag until the pointer is below and to
	the right of the Signature frame."
	
	To:
	"Then hold down the left mouse button, and drag until the pointer is below and to
	the right of the Exp Date frame."
	
	
	Page 151: Adjusting The Page Setup Procedure Correction
	-------------------------------------------------------
	
	Page 151, Step 2:
	
	Append the following text to the end of Step 2:
	"Resize the title frame to match the main text frame."
	
	
	Page 164: Creating A Web Site Procedure Correction
	--------------------------------------------------
	
	Page 164, Step 3:
	
	Change:
	"Click the cactus graphic in the first row, and then click the Play Clip
	button."
	
	To:
	"Click the cactus graphic, and then click the Play Clip button."
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
