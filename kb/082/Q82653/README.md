## Q82653: Da Vinci eMAIL may GP Fault When Attaching a Shared Worksheet

{% raw %}

	Article: Q82653
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
	Version(s): WINDOWS:3.1,3.11
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 25-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows versions 3.1, 3.11 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Da Vinci eMAIL may cause a general protection (GP) fault in USER.EXE at
	0019:0978 when you try to attach an Excel spreadsheet file (.XLS) that is
	already held open by Excel.
	
	Note: Under Windows 3.0, you will receive an Unrecoverable Application Error
	(UAE).
	
	WORKAROUND
	==========
	
	To work around this problem you can close the spreadsheet and try to attach it
	again, or contact Da Vinci Systems Corporation for an update.
	
	The product included here is manufactured by a vendor independent of Microsoft;
	we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's performance
	or reliability.
