## Q21356: How to Refuel and Rearm in the World War I Ace Game

	Article: Q21356
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	In the World War I Ace game and Reality mode, you must land at the friendly air
	base and proceed to the figure that looks like a large "F". You must then
	position the plane between the two forks of the "F". Your plane will then be
	refueled and rearmed.
