## Q325977: Train Simulator 1.0: Mouse Pointer Is Erratic or Stops Moving

	Article: Q325977
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 06-AUG-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Train Simulator, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you try to access train controls in Microsoft Train Simulator, your mouse
	pointer may become sluggish, unresponsive, or flicker on your screen.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This issue may occur if one or more of the following conditions are true:
	
	- Your video adapter drivers are outdated.
	
	- Your sound card drivers are outdated.
	
	- You are not using the latest version of DirectX.
	
	- You have not installed the most recent drivers for your VIA Technologies
	  chipset motherboard.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, use the following method.
	
	NOTE: Because there are several versions of Microsoft Windows, the following
	steps may be different on your computer. If they are, see your product
	documentation to complete these steps.
	
	Update Your Video Adapter Driver and Your Sound Card Driver:
	
	To identify the manufacturer and the model of your video adapter and your sound
	card, follow these steps:
	
	1. Click Start, point to Settings, click Control Panel, and then double-click
	  System.
	
	2. On a Microsoft Windows 95, Microsoft Windows 98, or Microsoft Windows
	  Millennium Edition (Me)-based system, click the Device Manager tab.
	
	  On a Microsoft Windows 2000 or Microsoft Windows XP-based system, click the
	  Hardware tab, and then click Device Manager.
	
	3. Expand Display Adapters, note the manufacturer and the model of your video
	  adapter, and then click OK.
	
	4. Expand "Sound video, and game controllers", note the manufacturer and model
	  of your sound card, and then click OK.
	
	5. Close all open windows.
	
	For information about how to download and install the latest drivers for your
	hardware, contact the manufacturer.
	
	For information about how to contact your sound card manufacturer or your video
	adapter manufacturer, click the appropriate article number in the following list
	to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:
	
	  Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K
	
	  Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P
	
	  Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
	
	Download and Install the Latest Version of DirectX:
	
	For information about how to download and install the latest version of DirectX,
	visit the following Microsoft Web site:
	
	  http://www.microsoft.com/windows/directx/downloads/default.asp
	
	Install the Most Recent 4in1 Drivers for Your VIA Technologies Chipset Motherboard:
	
	NOTE: This step applies only if an AMD processor and an applicable VIA
	Technologies chipset motherboard are installed in your computer.
	
	To obtain and install 4in1 drivers, visit the following VIA Technologies Web
	site:
	
	  http://www.viatech.com
	
	IMPORTANT: Contact your hardware manufacturer before you install the latest VIA
	Technologies 4in1 driver on your computer or make any modifications to the basic
	input/output system settings on your computer.
