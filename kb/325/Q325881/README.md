## Q325881: Asheron's Call: How to Purchase a House in Dark Majesty

	Article: Q325881
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 26-JUL-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Asheron's Call: Dark Majesty, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Asheron's Call Dark Majesty contains a house feature. The house is a place where
	you can gather with other characters, their friends, and allegiances. When you
	purchase a house, you can decorate it with furniture, hang treasured items on
	hooks to personalize your house, and use it to keep your belongings safe. Houses
	come in three sizes: Cottages, Villas, and Mansions.
	
	This article describes how to purchase a house in Asheron's Call Dark Majesty.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Ownership Restrictions:
	
	Before you can buy a house, you must meet the following qualifications:
	
	- You must have purchased, installed, and redeemed the coupon for Asheron's
	  Call Dark Majesty.
	
	- You must be at level 20 or higher to buy a small house. You must be at higher
	  levels to purchase correspondingly larger dwellings.
	
	- You can only own one house per active account per world.
	
	To Purchase a House:
	
	To purchase a house, follow these steps:
	
	1. Double-click the House Guardian that is adjacent to the house.
	
	2. When the Purchase window appears, note the purchase price (that is, the
	  number of pyreals, items, and Writs of Refuge), and then click the Buy House
	  tab.
	
	3. Drag pyreals, trade notes, items, or Writs of Refuge from your inventory into
	  the Purchase window, and then click Buy House.
	
	  You are given a house deed as proof of ownership.
	
	Housing Ownership Information:
	
	For additional information about housing in Asheron's Call Dark Majesty, see page
	32 in your owner's manual.
