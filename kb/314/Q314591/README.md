## Q314591: Streets and Trips 2002: Hangs At Various Times

	Article: Q314591
	Product(s): Microsoft Automap
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 06-AUG-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Streets & Trips 2002, version 1.0, used with:
	   - the operating system: Microsoft Windows Millennium Edition 
	   - the operating system: Microsoft Windows 98 Second Edition 
	   - the operating system: Microsoft Windows 98 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you use the Find feature in Microsoft Streets and Trips 2002 to search for
	an address, the returned results may be blank or the program may stop responding
	(hang).
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if Microsoft Internet Explorer is not properly updated
	on your system.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, restart your computer with a clean boot, and then
	reinstall Internet Explorer. To do this, follow these steps:
	
	1. Perform a clean boot:
	
	  a. Click Start, and then click Run.
	
	  b. In the Open box, type "msconfig" (without the quotation marks).
	
	  c. In the System Configuration Utility dialog box, click "Selective startup".
	
	  d. Click to clear each check box under "Selective startup", and then click
	     OK.
	
	  e. When prompted, restart your computer.
	
	2. Reinstall Internet Explorer 5.5:
	
	  a. Insert your Streets and Trips 2002 CD-ROM into your computer's CD-ROM or
	     DVD-ROM drive.
	
	  b. Click Start, and then click Run.
	
	  c. In the Open box, type the following
	
	  <D>:\OSP\1033\IE5\EN\ie5setup.exe
	
	     (where <D> is the letter of your computer's CD-ROM or DVD-ROM drive)
	     and then click OK.
	
	  d. Follow the instructions to complete the installation.
	
	If the following steps do not resolve the issue, then remove and reinstall the
	program.
	
	1. Close all running programs.
	
	2. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	3. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.
	
	4. Select Microsoft Streets & Trips 2002, and then click Remove.
	
	5. Follow the on-screen instructions.
	
	Once the uninstall is complete, close any open windows, and then put the Streets
	& Trips setup CD in the CD-ROM drive. Setup should automatically run and you
	can follow the wizard to install the program.
