## Q314527: Microsoft Office XP Step By Step Comments And Corrections

{% raw %}

	Article: Q314527
	Product(s): Microsoft Press
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
	Last Modified: 12-DEC-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSPRESS Microsoft Office XP Step by Step ISBN 0-7356-1294-3 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
	relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft Office XP Step By Step, ISBN
	0-7356-1294-3.
	
	The following topics are covered:
	
	- Pages 11 and 15: CreatingDoc Should Be Chap01
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Pages 11 and 15: CreatingDoc Should Be Chap01
	---------------------------------------------
	
	On page 11, in step 9, and on page 15, in step 21,
	
	Change:
	"CreatingDoc"
	
	To:
	"Chap01"
	
	
	Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
	comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
	printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
	already contain most or all of the above corrections.
