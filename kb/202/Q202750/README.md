## Q202750: CART: Steering Wheel Pedals Do Not Function

	Article: Q202750
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbenv kbhw kbimu kbHardware
	Last Modified: 25-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft CART Precision Racing, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you use a steering wheel and pedals to play Microsoft CART Precision
	Racing, the pedals may not function.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if any of the Customize Controller Button Assignments
	are configured to use the Acceleration or Brake actions.
	
	NOTE: One or both of the pedals may not function.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, do not configure the Customize Controller
	Button Assignments to use the Acceleration or Brake actions.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To configure button assignments for your steering wheel and pedals:
	
	1. On the main screen, click Options, and then Controls.
	
	2. Under Primary Driving Control, click Race Car Controller.
	
	3. Click Customize Controller.
	
	4. Click Defaults, and then click Yes.
	
	5. Click OK, and then click OK again.
