## Q57569: FltSim: Use Tandy Video Drivers for Panasonic Computers

	Article: Q57569
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	All Panasonic Business Partner computers are manufactured by Tandy.
	
	The Panasonic Business Partner models FX-1650 and FX-1750 are internally
	identical to models in the Tandy 1000 series.
	
	To get the best video results from Flight Simulator Versions 3.00 and 4.00,
	choose the Tandy 16 color as your video display.
