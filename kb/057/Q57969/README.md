## Q57969: Flight Simulator 4.00: World War I Scenery Problems

	Article: Q57969
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	In the World War I mode, enemy planes, factories, and targets may randomly
	appear and disappear in Flight Simulator Version 4.00.
	
	Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
	becomes available.
