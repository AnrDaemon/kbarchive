## Q280436: MechWarrior 4: SHIFT+Button Does Not Work Correctly

	Article: Q280436
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 4.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbenv kbhw kbimu kbHardware
	Last Modified: 25-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft MechWarrior 4: Vengeance, version 4.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you press SHIFT plus a button on a Gravis game controller, MechWarrior 4
	responds as though you pressed SHIFT+1.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the Gravis Controller Software is installed on your
	computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, remove the Gravis Controller Software:
	
	1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.
	
	2. Double-click Game Controllers.
	
	3. Click the Gravis controller, and then click Remove.
	
	4. Click Add.
	
	5. Click to select a joystick that has similar characteristics as your game
	  controller from the list of devices, and then click OK.
	
	For example, click "3 axis 2 button" if you use a joystick that has three axis
	and two buttons.
