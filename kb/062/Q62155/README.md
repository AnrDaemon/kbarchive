## Q62155: Flight Simulator 4.00: Delay When Moving Landing Gear

	Article: Q62155
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Flight Simulator version 4.00 may have as much as a 45 second delay when raising
	or lowering the landing gear with the "G" key. This problem occurs on 8088-based
	machines with a monochrome display and adapter, and is due to the slower speed
	of the 8088 processor.
