	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The following is a summary of the performance specifications for the Cessna
	Skylane RG II in Flight Simulator version 4.00:
	
	  Feature                          Specification
	  -------                          -------------
	
	  Length                           28 feet
	  Height                           9 feet 3 inches
	  Wing span                        36 feet
	  Wing area                        174 square feet
	  Maximum takeoff weight           3100 pounds
	  Empty weight                     1752 pounds
	  Maximum useful load              1360 pounds
	  Maximum landing weight           3100 pounds
	  Wing loading                     17.8 pounds per square foot
	  Power loading                    13.2 pounds per horsepower
	  Maximum usable fuel              88 gallons (526 pounds)
	  Maximum rate of climb
	     at sea level                  1050 feet per minute
	  Maximum rate of climb
	     at 8000 feet                  455 feet per minute
	  Service ceiling                  14,900 feet
	  Maximum speed                    146 knots
	  Cruise, 65 percent power
	     at 8000 feet                  133 knots
	  Endurance at 65 percent power    7.5 hours
	  Stall speed clean                54 knots
	  Stall speed flaps down           49 knots
	  Turbulent air penetration speed  110 knots
	  Landing gear                     Retractable tricycle,
	                                   steerable nose-wheel
