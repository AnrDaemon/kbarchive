## Q143119: PractiCard 14.4 PCMCIA Modem Is Not Initialized

	Article: Q143119
	Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
	Version(s): 95
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): win95
	Last Modified: 17-DEC-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Windows 95 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you insert a Practical Peripherals PractiCard 14,400 bps modem, revision A,
	in a PCMCIA slot in your computer, the modem may not be initialized.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Contact Practical Peripherals for information about upgrading your modem to
	revision B.
