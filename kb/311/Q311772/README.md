## Q311772: Encarta Online Deluxe: Description of Encarta Online Deluxe

	Article: Q311772
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu
	Last Modified: 23-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Encarta Online Deluxe Encyclopedia 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes Encarta Online Deluxe Encyclopedia and how to obtain a
	subscription to it.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Encarta Online Deluxe Encyclopedia
	----------------------------------
	
	Encarta Online Deluxe Encyclopedia is a premium Web-based multimedia encyclopedia
	that includes 42,000 articles, an atlas, a dictionary, Web links, and much more.
	The general Encarta Online Web site contains only a portion of the reference
	content that is available in Encarta Online Deluxe. To gain access to Encarta
	Online Deluxe, you must first sign up and activate your subscription, and then
	sign in by using a .NET Passport account.
	
	To sign in to and use Online Deluxe, click Deluxe Sign-In on the Encarta Online
	home page at the following Web site:
	
	  http://encarta.msn.com (http://encarta.msn.com)
	
	The Encarta Online Web site (at the preceding link) is available to all Web
	users. You are not required to sign in. The encyclopedia, dictionary, atlas,
	language lessons, and other services are available to all, with the exception of
	articles that are marked with a star. These articles are available only to
	Encarta Online Deluxe subscribers.
	
	How to Obtain a Subscription to Encarta Online Deluxe
	-----------------------------------------------------
	
	You are eligible for a free subscription to Encarta Online Deluxe if any of the
	following conditions are true:
	
	- You own a CD-ROM or DVD-ROM copy of Encarta Encyclopedia Deluxe 2002 or
	  Encarta Reference Library 2002.
	
	  If you own a North American edition of Encarta Encyclopedia Deluxe 2002 or
	  Encarta Reference Library 2002, you can activate your one-year free access to
	  Encarta Online Deluxe when you join Club Encarta. To do this, click Join Club
	  Encarta on the Encarta 2002 Help menu.
	
	  Make sure that you select "Free one-year access to Encarta Online Deluxe" on
	  the Club Encarta benefits form. When you sign up for Encarta Online Deluxe,
	  you are prompted to enter your Passport account information or to create a
	  Passport account if you don't already have one. Please remember the Passport
	  information that you provide. It is required each time that you sign in to
	  Encarta Online Deluxe.
	
	For additional information about how to join Club Encarta, click the article
	number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:
	
	  Q306747 ENC2002: How to Obtain Free Membership to Encarta Online Deluxe
	  Encyclopedia
	
	NOTE: If you do not own a current copy of Encarta Encyclopedia Deluxe or Encarta
	Reference Library, browse to the following Microsoft Web site for more
	information about how to obtain the latest version:
	
	  http://encarta.msn.com/shop/default.asp
	  (http://encarta.msn.com/shop/default.asp)
	
	-or-
	
	- Your organization has a site license.
	
	  -or-
	
	- You recently purchased a new Compaq or Hewlett-Packard (HP) computer, and
	  your computer manufacturer included a free one-year access to Encarta Online
	  Deluxe.
	
	  If a one-year subscription to Encarta Online Deluxe is included with your new
	  computer, an Encarta Online Deluxe icon is displayed on your desktop.
	  Double-click this icon to sign up for your free access.
