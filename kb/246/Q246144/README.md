## Q246144: FS2000: Computer Stops Responding the First Time You Play Game

{% raw %}

	Article: Q246144
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbsetup kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 
	- Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	The first time you play Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000, your computer may stop
	responding (hang).
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Diamond Viper 550 video adapter is installed in your computer.
	
	- You do not restart your computer after you install Flight Simulator 2000.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, restart your computer.
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, restart your computer after you install
	Flight Simulator 2000.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	At the conclusion of Flight Simulator 2000 Setup, you are not prompted to
	restart your computer.
