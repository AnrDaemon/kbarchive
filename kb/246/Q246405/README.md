## Q246405: How to Close the MSN Quick View Icon

	Article: Q246405
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.5,2.6,5.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 19-NOV-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 2.5, 2.6, 5.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes how to close the MSN Quick View icon.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To close the MSN Quick View icon click the MSN Quick View icon, point to MSN
	Options, and then click Close. The next time you connect to MSN Internet Access,
	the Quick View icon will be restored.
