## Q139765: Julia Child: Animated Buttons Become Inactive

	Article: Q139765
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbbuglist
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Julia Child: Home Cooking with Master Chefs for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	In Microsoft Julia Child, if you click an animated button and hold it down while
	the sound associated with the button plays, and then move the cursor away from
	the animated button and release the mouse button, the animated button fails to
	perform its designated function.
	
	Note that the sound associated with the animated button plays, even though the
	animated button fails to perform as expected.
	
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Single click on the To Chef or To Recipe button, don't hold the mouse button
	down, to activate the jump to the desired topic.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Julia Child: Home
	Cooking with Master Chefs for Windows, version 1.0. We are researching this
	problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it
	becomes available.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	In the Chefs, Recipes, and Lessons categories, the animated buttons include: To
	Chef, To Recipe, To Lesson, Shopping List, Print, and Notes.
