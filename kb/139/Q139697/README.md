	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Fury 3 for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Fury3 enhancement codes allow you to warp to different planets, boost your
	ship's strength, or provide you with full weapons. The following article lists
	those codes.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To use the codes, type the code name from within the Fury3 game. You do not need
	to press ENTER after typing in the codes.
	
	Enhancement Code     Function
	----------------     --------
	TRYMEON              Turns invulnerability on/off; you cannot die
	TUFENUF              Restores your shields to 100%
	JUMPNIT              Allows you to jump to the next stage in the game
	STPNSHP              Toggles hovering on/off by pressing and holding down
	                    CTRL
	SSMOKIN              Gives you 1000 units of Afterburner fuel
	URDUSTD              Augments the Afterburner so that you have three times
	                    the thrust at its maximum.
	PACKIN1              Plasma Assault Cannon (PAC)
	PACKIN2              Ion Burst Cannon (ION)
	PACKIN3              Rapid-targeting laser (RTL)
	PACKIN4              20 Manual Aimed Missiles (MAM)
	PACKIN5              20 Seek-and-destroy [Guided Missiles] (SAD)
	PACKIN6              20 Shock-WAVE Torpedo [Super Missile] (SWT)
	PACKIN7              Discrete Annihilation Missile [Smart Bomb] (DAM)
	LIVITUP              120 Units of Afterburner Fuel (FLY)
	ICUNOMO              Invisibility for 30 seconds
	IMTUFFF              Invincibility of 30 seconds
	GIVITUP              Gives you lots of everything
	WORMIT1              Go to planet 1
	WORMIT2              Go to planet 2
	WORMIT3              Go to planet 3
	WORMIT4              Go to planet 4
	WORMIT5              Go to planet 5
	WORMIT6              Go to planet 6
	WORMIT7              Go to planet 7
	WORMIT8              Go to planet 8
