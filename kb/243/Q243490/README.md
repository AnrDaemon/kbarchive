## Q243490: Asheron's Call: Other Players Cannot See Monster on Your Screen

	Article: Q243490
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbimu msgame kbgraphxlinkcriticalkbfaq
	Last Modified: 16-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Asheron's Call, version 1.0 
	- Microsoft Asheron's Call: Dark Majesty, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Asheron's Call, you may see a monster on your screen,
	but other players you are playing with do not see the monster.
	
	In addition, you cannot examine or attack this monster.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior occurs if the server is not updating your computer with current
	game data.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, leave and then reenter the Asheron's Call game world:
	
	1. On the Asheron's Call game screen, click Options ([GRAPHIC:the green triangle
	  with two check boxes]) in the lower right corner of the screen.
	
	2. Click Leave World, and then click Yes.
	
	3. Click your character's name, and then click Enter Game.
