	Word 97 Hangs
	-------------
	
	When you use a Trident 9660/968X Linear Accelerated for PCI v. 1.0a video driver,
	Microsoft Word 97 will suddenly freeze when working in a document. The
	resolution to this is to change the video driver to VGA or to obtain an updated
	video driver from Trident.
