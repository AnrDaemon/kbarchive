## Q183984: How to Obtain Information About Your MSN Classic Account

{% raw %}

	Article: Q183984
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.2,1.3
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbtool kbmsn
	Last Modified: 10-OCT-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 1.2, 1.3 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes how you can easily obtain information, such as your
	current balance or a detailed record of charges, for your MSN, The Microsoft
	Network, account.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To obtain information about your MSN account, follow these steps:
	
	1. Click Start, point to Find, and then click Files Or Folders.
	
	2. In the Named box, type "onlstmt.exe" (without quotation marks).
	
	3. Click Find Now.
	
	4. In the list of found files, double-click the Onlstmt.exe file.
	
	5. Click Connect.
	
	Additional query words: msnet msnetwork microsoft-net m.s.n.
