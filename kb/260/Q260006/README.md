## Q260006: StarLancer: Poor Video Performance or Game Quits Unexpectedly

	Article: Q260006
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft StarLancer, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft StarLancer, you may experience one or more of the
	following symptoms:
	
	- You may experience poor video performance in the game.
	
	- The game may quit unexpectedly during the jump sequence at the beginning of
	  Mission 1.
	
	- Textures in the game may appear jagged or blocky.
	
	- When you attempt to change the brightness level for your video adapter, the
	  change may not be applied in the game.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a Creative Labs GraphicsBlaster Savage4 video adapter
	is installed in your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest version of the video
	driver for your video adapter from the following Creative Labs Web site:
	
	  http://support.creativelabs.com
	
	If the issue continues to occur, download and install the latest version of the
	S3 Savage4 reference driver from the following S3 Web site:
	
	  http://www.s3.com
