## Q260047: FS2000: Interior View Is Distorted in Hairy Carrier Adventure

	Article: Q260047
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbenv kbgraphic kbui kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition 
	- Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you fly in the Hairy Carrier Adventure in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000
	or Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition, the interior views may
	be distorted.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, resize the interior view window:
	
	1. Start the Hairy Carrier Adventure in Flight Simulator 2000.
	
	2. Make sure that the Num Lock light on your keyboard is lit.
	
	  If the Num Lock light is not lit, press the NUM LOCK key to turn on the Num
	  Lock setting on the numeric keypad.
	
	3. Press and hold down the 2 key on the numeric keypad.
	
	  Note that the interior view is only displayed on the top half of the screen.
	
	4. While you continue to hold down the 2 key on the numeric keypad, drag the
	  bottom edge of the interior view in order to resize the window to use the
	  full screen.
	
	5. Release the 2 key on your numeric keypad.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products that are
	listed at the beginning of this article.
