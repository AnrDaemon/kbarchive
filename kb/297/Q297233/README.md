## Q297233: Single MSN Internet Access Account On Two Computers--Connectivit

	Article: Q297233
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.0,1.05,1.2,1.3,2.0,2.5,2.5 (OEM Release),2.51,2.52,2.6,5.0,5.1,5.2,5.3,6.0,6.1
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 1.0, 1.05, 1.2, 1.3, 2.0, 2.5, 2.5 (OEM Release), 2.51, 2.52, 2.6, 5.0, 5.1, 5.2, 5.3, 6.0, 6.1 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	You can't access your MSN Internet Access account or you receive the following
	error message:
	
	  "Either your username or password is incorrect or your account could not be
	  accessed."
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This error occurs because you have two computers and you have connected with MSN
	Internet Access on both computers simultaneously.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	If you have MSN set up on two computers and you are currently connected to MSN
	Internet Access on both computers, you will need to disconnect the second
	connection.
	
	Note: The MSN Subscriber Agreement (2.2) specifies that simultaneous connections
	to MSN are prohibited.
	
	To view the information about simultaneous connections in the MSN Internet Access
	Subscriber Agreement
	
	1. Go to: http://supportservices.msn.com.
	
	2. Under Get Support, click Rights and Responsibilities, and then click
	  Subscriber Agreement.

	================
