## Q171703: Kids Plus!: Resetting Protect It! and Internet Ratings Passwords

	Article: Q171703
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): 1.00
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbusage
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Plus! for Kids, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article provides steps to remove passwords for the Protect It! Owner Key
	and the Internet Ratings Supervisor in Microsoft Plus! For Kids. These steps are
	provided for administrative users who have forgotten one or both of these
	passwords.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To remove passwords for Protect It! or the Internet Ratings Supervisor from your
	computer, do the following:
	
	1. Put your Microsoft Plus! For Kids compact disc in the CD caddy (if needed),
	  and then insert it into the CD-ROM drive.
	
	2. Click Start, and then click Run.
	
	3. In the Open box type the following line, and then click OK:
	
	  " rundll32.exe d:\protect!\pireset.dll,reset 1 " (without the quotation
	  marks)
	
	  NOTE: If your CD-ROM drive letter is not d, substitute the letter of your
	  CD-ROM drive. IMPORTANT: Don't type any spaces around the comma.
	
	4. Click the Protect It! or the Internet Ratings Supervisor check box to select
	  the password you want to reset.
	
	5. Click OK.
	
	The password for Protect It! or the Internet Ratings Supervisor will be removed,
	depending on which check box you selected. You will be asked to create a new
	password the next time you do one of the following:
	
	- Run Protect It!
	
	- Display the Content Advisor
	
	- Enable/disable Internet Ratings
	
	Removing or resetting these passwords does not remove or disable Protect It!
	restrictions on user profiles, nor does it disable Protect It! options or
	Internet Ratings for All Family Members.
