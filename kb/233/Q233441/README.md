## Q233441: MSBP: Heading Text Is Cut Off When You Paste Article into Word

{% raw %}

	Article: Q233441
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbinterop kbimu
	Last Modified: 23-JUN-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Business Planner 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you copy an entire article from Microsoft Business Planner and paste the
	text into Microsoft Word, you may experience one or both of the following
	symptoms:
	
	- The text of the heading may not be displayed.
	
	- The text of the heading may be truncated.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To work around this issue, paste the text into Microsoft WordPad.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Business Planner.
