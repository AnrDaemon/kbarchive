## Q176298: Age Of Empires: Location of Product Identification Number (PID)

	Article: Q176298
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui aoe kbimu msgamekbfaq
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes how to locate the Product Identification Number (PID) for
	Age of Empires.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To locate the Age of Empires PID, follow these steps:
	
	1. Start Age of Empires.
	
	2. On the Age of Empires startup screen, click Single Player.
	
	3. Click Random Map.
	
	4. Click Start Game.
	
	5. On the game navigation bar, click Menu, and then click About.
	
	6. Note the PID on the Product ID line, and then click OK.
