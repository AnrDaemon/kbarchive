## Q277936: Transferring Outlook Express Contact List or AOL Address Book to

	Article: Q277936
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.x,6.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Explorer, version 1.x 
	- The Microsoft Network version 6.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The transfer of an Outlook Express Contact List or AOL Address Book to MSN
	Explorer Address Book may need to be manually initiated by the user.
	
	MSN Explorer Version 1.0
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Under normal circumstances, after installing MSN Explorer, the first time a user
	with either an Outlook Express Contact List or an AOL Address Book accesses MSN
	Explorer Mail they are given the opportunity to transfer the content of these
	lists into an MSN Explorer Address Book. The content will remain in Outlook
	Express Contact List or AOL Address Book, but be copied to the new address
	book.
	
	If the system fails to offer this transfer or if the user chooses to decline this
	transfer by clicking Cancel, it can be initiated later.
	
	To transfer your Outlook Express Contact List or AOL Address Book to your MSN
	Explorer Address Book
	
	1. At the top of the MSN Explorer browser click Help and Settings.
	
	2. In the left column under the Member Center, click E-mail, and then click
	  Import Address Book.
	
	3. On the new page click Import Address Book and follow the instructions.
