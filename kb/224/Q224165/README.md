## Q224165: MSB Animals: License and Certificate Printouts Are Not Centered

{% raw %}

	Article: Q224165
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbprint kbimu
	Last Modified: 07-SEP-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the World of Animals for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you print a Driver's License or a Certificate in Magic School Bus Explores
	the World of Animals, the printout may not be centered or may be too wide.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if you are using any of the following printers:
	
	- Brother M-1924L
	- Canon Bubble-Jet BJC-4550
	- Canon Bubble-Jet BJC-610
	- Epson Stylus ESC/P
	- HP DeskJet 660C
	- IBM 4019 Laser
	- IBM Personal Printer II 2381
	- Xerox Docuprint 4517PS
