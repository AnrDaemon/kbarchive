## Q224254: S&amp;T 2000: &quot;Construction Out of Date&quot; Message Remains on Screen

	Article: Q224254
	Product(s): Microsoft Automap
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui kbimu
	Last Modified: 27-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you click the following message
	
	  Road construction information is out of date. Click this line to update.
	
	on the Directions tab in Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000, the message
	may remain on the screen after the download is complete.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if you have not loaded a route when you download the
	construction information file.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, do one of the following:
	
	- Plan and calculate a new route.
	
	- Open a saved route.
	
	- Quit and then restart the program.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Expedia Streets and
	Trips 2000.
