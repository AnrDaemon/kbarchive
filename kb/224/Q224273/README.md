## Q224273: Streets &amp; Trips 2000: Cannot Hide Park And Ride Icons on the Map

	Article: Q224273
	Product(s): Microsoft Automap
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui kbimu
	Last Modified: 27-JUL-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	In Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000, you cannot prevent Park And Ride
	icons from being displayed on the map.
	
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Expedia Streets and
	Trips 2000.
