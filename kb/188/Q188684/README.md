## Q188684: XADM: Units of Information Store Delivery Time PerfMon Counters

	Article: Q188684
	Product(s): Microsoft Exchange
	Version(s): WINDOWS:4.0,5.0,5.5
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 15-MAY-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Exchange Server, versions 4.0, 5.0, 5.5 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The units for the Average Delivery Time and Average Local Delivery Time counters
	of the MSExchangeIS Private and MSExchangeIS Public objects in the Performance
	Monitor are tens of milliseconds.
	
	Hence a counter of 100 corresponds to 1 second.
