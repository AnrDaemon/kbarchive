## Q275663: CFS2: Sounds Are Not Played in Training Missions

{% raw %}

	Article: Q275663
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbsound kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you fly a Training Mission in Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2, none of
	the sounds may be played.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the Combat Flight Simulator Disc 2 CD-ROM is not
	inserted in your CD-ROM drive, and either of the following conditions is true:
	
	- You installed Combat Flight Simulator 2 with the Typical installation option.
	
	- You installed Combat Flight Simulator 2 with the Custom installation option,
	  and then clicked to clear the Training Mission Media check box.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, end the Training Mission, insert the Combat Flight
	Simulator 2 Disc 2 CD-ROM into your CD-ROM drive, and then restart the Training
	Mission.
	
	To do this:
	
	1. Press CTRL+U to end the Training Mission.
	
	2. Insert the Combat Flight Simulator 2 Disc 2 CD-ROM into your CD-ROM drive.
	
	3. Restart the Training Mission.
