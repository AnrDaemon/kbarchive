## Q255944: Allegiance: Censored Words Are Displayed When You Chat

	Article: Q255944
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kbtool kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	When you play Microsoft Allegiance, censored words may be displayed when you
	chat, even if you enable the Censor Display feature.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	This behavior can occur because the Censor Display feature filters words based
	on their exact spelling. Censored words that are either misspelled or spelled
	phonetically are not filtered
	
	Additional query words: 1.00 msgame igz msngz dirty swear
