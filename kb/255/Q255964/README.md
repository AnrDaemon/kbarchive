## Q255964: Allegiance: Incorrectly Prompted to Click the Launch Button

	Article: Q255964
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdocerr kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	When you play Training Mission 3 in Microsoft Allegiance, you are prompted to
	click the Launch button when you are ready.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Because the Launch button is not active in this training mission, clicking the
	Launch button has no effect. When the training mission advances to the next
	module, your ship is launched automatically.
	
	Additional query words: 1.00 msgame igz msngz ineffective nothing happens
