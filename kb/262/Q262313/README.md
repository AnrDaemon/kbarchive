## Q262313: StarLancer: Officer States That Enriquez Is on the Warpath

{% raw %}

	Article: Q262313
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft StarLancer, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	At the end of mission eight, the Yamato bridge officer states that Enriquez is
	on the warpath, even though during the mission the Reliant was destroyed with
	Flight Commander Enriquez on board.
	
	This statement does not affect the outcome of the game, whether or not you
	completed the mission successfully.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft StarLancer, version
	1.0.
