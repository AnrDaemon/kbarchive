## Q68743: ASD: Scenery Does Not Change Color at Night

	Article: Q68743
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Scenery designed with Flight Simulator Aircraft & Scenery Designer will not
	change color at night. Therefore, scenery such as a blue river will be highly
	visible at night.
	
	Darker or muted colors will give a more realistic appearance at night.
