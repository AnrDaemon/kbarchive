## Q140768: MSB: Game in Progress Is Not Saved

	Article: Q140768
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): MACINTOSH:1.0; WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbtlckbfaq
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	1.00 1.00a | 1.00 1.00a
	WINDOWS    | MACINTOSH
	kbmm kbusage kbprb kbfaq
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the Human Body for the Macintosh, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the Human Body for Windows, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores Inside the Earth for Windows, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the Ocean for Windows, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the Rainforest for Windows, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the Solar System for Windows, version 1.0 
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores in the Age of Dinosaurs for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	If you exit from one of the programs listed above by using a shortcut or hot
	key, your games and your location in the program are not saved.
	
	The Macintosh hot key is:
	
	  Keyboard Command key+Q
	
	On Macintosh computers, the problem also occurs if you click Exit on the Game
	menu.
	
	The Windows exit hot key is:
	
	  ALT+F4
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Each of the following methods allow you to exit from the program and give you
	the opportunity to save your game:
	
	MSB Human Body
	--------------
	
	- In the classroom, click the Quit bell, and then click Quit.
	
	  -or-
	
	- In the back of the bus, click Exit on the backdoor, and then click Quit.
	
	  -or-
	
	- When you are in the driver's seat of the bus, click Exit, and then click
	  Quit.
	
	Each of these methods automatically saves your current position in the program.
	
	MSB Solar
	---------
	
	- In the classroom, click the Quit bell, and then click Yes when you receive
	  the message, "Are you sure you want to QUIT?"
	
	  -or-
	
	- In the front of the bus, click the Quit bell, and then click Yes when you
	  receive the message, "Are you sure you want to QUIT?"
	
	There is no way to save a game in Magic School Bus Explores the Solar System.
	
	MSB Ocean
	---------
	
	- In the front of the bus, click Quit, and then click Save Game, and click on a
	  box to save the game into. Once the game has been saved, click Quit in the
	  front of the bus, and then click Quit.
	
	  -or-
	
	- In the front of the bus, click Save, and then click on a box to save the game
	  into. Once the game has been saved, click Quit in the front of the bus, and
	  then click Quit.
	
	MSB Earth
	---------
	
	- In the classroom, click the Quit bell, then click Save, and then click Quit
	  when you receive the message, "Your game has been saved."
	
	  -or-
	
	- In the front of the bus, click Quit, then click Save, and then click Quit
	  when you receive the message, "Your game has been saved."
	
	  -or-
	
	- In the back of the bus, click Quit, then click Save, and then click Quit when
	  you receive the message, "Your game has been saved."
	
	MSB Dinos
	---------
	
	- In the classroom, click the Quit/Save sign, and then click Save Game. When
	  you return to the classroom, click the Quit/Save sign, and then click Quit.
	- In the front of the bus, click Quit/Save, and then click Save Game. When you
	  return to the front of the bus, click Quit/Save, and then click Quit.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	You can only save one game each time you run MSB Human. You can access this
	saved game by clicking the Resume Previous Game token as soon as you enter the
	Classroom. If you do not select the token, it automatically disappears after
	about 15 seconds.
	
	If you do not click the token before it disappears, you can restore the token by
	quitting and then restarting MSB Human.
