## Q140085: Golf 2.0: Accessing Tips Included on CD-ROM Version

	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Golf for Windows, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Microsoft Golf includes golf tips for the Torrey Pines and Firestone courses.
	Each hole includes a single tip.
	
	To access the tip, do one of the following:
	
	- Click the tip button in the swing control window.
	
	  -or-
	
	- Click the Action menu, and then click Tip.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The Tip button and Action menu Tip option are unavailable with all golf courses
	except those included on the Golf compact disc.
	
	The tips may be presented by a male or female pro. The gender of the person
	giving the tip is random and not dependent on whether the golfer is male or
	female.
