## Q239872: NBA/NFL 2000: Can Only Play Game in Software Mode

	Article: Q239872
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgamekbfaq
	Last Modified: 03-SEP-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
	- Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000 or Microsoft NFL Fever 2000, you
	may experience the following symptoms:
	
	- You are only able to play the game in software mode.
	
	- The video adapter installed in your computer may not appear in the list of
	  available video drivers in the Display options window.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if at least two of the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Creative Labs Graphics Blaster Exxtreme video adapter is installed in your
	  computer.
	
	- A video adapter based on the Riva TNT2 chipset is installed in your computer.
	
	- The video driver installed on your computer is outdated.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for your
	video adapter from the appropriate Web site:
	
	Creative Labs Graphics Blaster Exxtreme:
	
	  http://support.soundblaster.com/files/download.asp
	
	Riva TNT2-Based Video Adapter:
	
	  http://www.nvidia.com/3Dgraphics/resource_center.html
