## Q43237: Mac Wkst: Difference Between Closing and Signing Out

{% raw %}

	Article: Q43237
	Product(s): Microsoft Mail For Appletalk Networks
	Version(s): WINDOWS:3.0,3.1
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 09-NOV-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks, versions 3.0, 3.1 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	To close your Microsoft Mail mailbox, make sure the Summary window is active,
	then choose Close Mailbox from the Mail menu. The workstation remains connected
	to the Mail system and you are notified when new mail is received (if you have
	selected that option in Preferences).
	
	To sign out of Microsoft Mail, make sure the Summary window is active, then
	choose Close & Sign Out from the Mail menu. The workstation is disconnected
	from the Mail system and you are not notified when new mail is received by the
	Microsoft Mail server.
	
	
	By closing your mailbox and signing out of Mail, you close all open Mail windows.
	However, your messages remain in your mailbox on the server.
