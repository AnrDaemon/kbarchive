## Q273414: Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings Update 2.0a Available

{% raw %}

	Article: Q273414
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): aoe kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Microsoft has released a new update to Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of
	Kings. This update includes several new enhancements and bug fixes in addition
	to the bug fixes that are included in the Age of Empires II AI Update patch.
	
	To download the Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings Update 2.0a, visit the
	following Microsoft Web site:
	
	  http://www.microsoft.com/games/age2/downloads.htm
	
	NOTE: The Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings Update 2.0a is approximately 2.6
	megabytes (MB) in size.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	For additional information about the enhancements and bug fixes are included in
	the Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings Update 2.0a, click the article
	number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:
	
	  Q273397 AoE II: Enhancements and Fixes Included in the Age of Empires 2.0a
	  Update
