## Q273346: How to add, change, and delete contact records in MSN Companion

	Article: Q273346
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Companion, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Step by step instructions on how to add, change, and delete contact records in
	MSN Companion address book.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To add a contact
	
	1. Click mail on the toolbar, click open address book, and then click add a new
	  person.
	
	2. Type the name and e-mail address in the corresponding input boxes, select
	  whether or not to add the contact to instant messaging*, and then click add.
	
	To change contact information
	
	1. Click mail on the toolbar, click open address book, and then click change for
	  the contact you want to change.
	
	2. Type the changed name or e-mail address in the corresponding input boxes,
	  select whether or not to add the contact to instant messaging*, and then
	  click save.
	
	To delete a contact click mail on the toolbar, click open address book, and then
	click delete for the contact you want to delete.
	
	* If you select set this person up for instant messaging and click save, you are
	given a choice of whether or not to send and invitation to instant messaging and
	an opportunity to preview the invitation. Click send invitation to invite your
	contact to join MSN Messenger Service.
