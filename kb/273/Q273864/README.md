## Q273864: Asheron's Call: How to Securely Trade with Another Player

{% raw %}

	Article: Q273864
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu msgame kbgraphxlinkcritical
	Last Modified: 06-AUG-2002
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Asheron's Call, version 1.0 
	- Microsoft Asheron's Call: Dark Majesty, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Microsoft Asheron's Call has added a feature that allows two players to securely
	trade with one another. This article describes how to use this new feature.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To securely trade with another player in Asheron's Call:
	
	+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
	| On the Asheron's Call game screen, click Social
	        Systems (the green triangle with the outline of a head). Click the Trade tab. In the View window, click the name of the player that
	        you want to trade with, and then click Trade securely
	        with player name. Place the item that you want to trade into your trade
	        slot. Wait for the other player to place the item that they
	        want to trade into their trade slot. When both players click Trade, the items
	        are traded. If you do not want the item that is offered for trade,
	        click Exit trade with player name
	        before you click Trade. | Trade tab | 
	+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
