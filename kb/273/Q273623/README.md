## Q273623: Midtown Madness 2: Sky and Checkpoint Textures Are Distorted

{% raw %}

	Article: Q273623
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbenv kbgraphic kbimu
	Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Midtown Madness 2, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Midtown Madness 2, the textures in and around the sky,
	the checkpoints, and the heads-up display (HUD) may be distorted.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:
	
	- An ATI Radeon video adapter is installed in the computer.
	
	- You use the T&L feature of the ATI Radeon video adapter in the game.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, turn off the T&L feature in the game:
	
	1. Start Midtown Madness 2.
	
	2. On the startup screen, click Options.
	
	3. Click Graphics.
	
	4. In the Renderer box, click Hardware.
	
	5. Click Done.
	
	6. Click Back.
	
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	For additional information about how to troubleshoot video-related issues in
	Midtown Madness 2, click the article number below to view the article in the
	Microsoft Knowledge Base:
