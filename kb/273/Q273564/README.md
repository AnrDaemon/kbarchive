## Q273564: How to change call waiting settings in MSN Companion

	Article: Q273564
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): 1.0,2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- MSN Companion, versions 1.0, 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Step-by-step instructions for changing call waiting settings.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	If your phone has Call Waiting, you can choose whether to have the call
	disconnect your Web session.
	
	To turn off Call Waiting so your calls will not interrupt your online sessions
	
	1. Click settings on the start page and then click call waiting.
	
	2. Select the don't allow incoming calls to interrupt me while I'm online
	  checkbox, select *70 (used mostly for touch tone phones) or select other code
	  and type your cancel-call-waiting code, and then click done.
	
	To turn on Call Waiting so your calls will interrupt your online sessions
	
	1. Click settings on the start page and then click call waiting.
	
	2. Clear the don't allow incoming calls to interrupt me while I'm online
	  checkbox, click done, and then disconnect MSN companion from the Internet.
	
	Note: If you do not have Call Waiting from the phone company and select call
	waiting settings on MSN Companion, you will not be able to connect to the
	Internet. After MSN Companion fails to connect, you will be given a dialup
	troubleshooting page. Click I heard an operator's message and then click I do
	not have Call Waiting.
