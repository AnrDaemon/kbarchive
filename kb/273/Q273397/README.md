## Q273397: AoE II: Enhancements and Fixes in Age of Empires 2.0a Update

	Article: Q273397
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 09-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article describes the enhancements and fixes that are included in the Age
	of Empires II: The Age of Kings Update 2.0a for Microsoft Age of Empires II: The
	Age of Kings.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The enhancements and fixes included with the Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings
	Update 2.0a are:
	
	- Single-Player Performance Improvements - The performance of single-player
	  campaign games has been improved, especially on computers that contain slower
	  processors.
	
	- Limited Pauses - The total number of pauses available to each player in a
	  multiplayer game is limited. The total number of pauses remaining is
	  displayed each time someone pauses the game.
	
	- Display Name of Out of Sync Player - In a multiplayer game with three or more
	  players, if a player's computer becomes out of sync, that player's name is
	  displayed in an out of sync message on all the other player's computers.
	
	- Stability Improvements - A number of hanging and multiplayer out of sync
	  problems are fixed, which results in improved game stability.
	
	- Computer Player Does Not Resign - The computer player no longer resigns after
	  a few minutes of play. (Previously available in the AI Update.)
	
	- Fish Trap - You cannot build a Fish Trap on top of an existing fish resource.
	
	- Fishing Ship - You cannot use a Fishing Ship to construct buildings other
	  than a Fish Trap.
	
	- Flare and Waypoint - The game does not stop responding (hang) when you set a
	  flare or waypoint location at the very Southern-most point on the map.
	
	- Cooperative Farming - When you take over a farm foundation that was built by
	  an ally, you cannot extract more than the standard amount of food from that
	  farm.
	
	- Farming on Forest - You cannot build a Farm or Wall on top of a partially cut
	  section of forest.
	
	- Unit Ignoring Orders - Units do not ignore orders under some circumstances.
	
	- Multiplayer Restore from Zone - The ability to save and restore a
	  disconnected or out-of-sync multiplayer game on Zone.com has been improved.
	
	- Foundation Outline - Building foundations now have outlines, so you can click
	  them when they are behind trees or other objects.
	
	- Trebuchet Firing - Trebuchets do not miss targets that are at a different
	  elevation.
	
	- Single-Player Campaign After Regicide - A single-player game does not
	  automatically end immediately after you play a multiplayer game of Regicide.
	
	- Computer Player - The behavior of the computer player in multiplayer games
	  has been improved.
	
	- Scenario Editor - A number of Scenario Editor problems have been fixed.
