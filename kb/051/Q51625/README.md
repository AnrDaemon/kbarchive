## Q51625: Flight Simulator Version 4.00 Hangs on AT&amp;T 6300

	Article: Q51625
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	Using Flight Simulator Version 4.00 on an AT&T 6300 with a ROM earlier than
	Version 1.43 may result in the program hanging when it is started.
	
	To find the ROM version in your computer, turn it off and then back on after
	waiting about 30 seconds. On the initial screen, the ROM version is displayed
	beneath the words "Resident Diagnostics."
	
	To upgrade the ROM on your computer, contact AT&T National Parts at (800)
	222-7278.
