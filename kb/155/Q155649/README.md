## Q155649: Monster Truck: Playing Music from a Music CD While Racing

	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Monster Truck Madness, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article contains information from the Monster Truck Madness Readme.txt
	file.
	
	If you chose either the Compact or Typical option in Setup when
	installing Monster Truck Madness, you need to have the Monster Truck
	Madness compact inserted in your CD-ROM drive before you start the
	game. If you chose the Complete option in Setup when installing the
	game, you have the option of playing music from your favorite music
	CD while playing the game. There are two ways to listen to your
	music CD.
	
	First Method:
	
	Insert your favorite music CD into your CD-ROM drive.
	
	Start Monster Truck Madness.
	
	On the Options menu, click Sound.
	
	Click the Play Music check box and the Play CD Music Tracks check
	  box.
	
	Click OK. Your music will play when you start a race.
	
	 TIP: To skip to a previous or to the next track, press F11 and F12.
	
	Second Method:
	
	Exit Monster Truck Madness.
	
	Insert your favorite music CD into your CD-ROM drive.
	
	Start the Windows 95 CD Player: Click Start, point to Programs,
	  point to Accessories, point to Multimedia, and then click CD
	  Player.
	
	Start Monster Truck Madness.
	
	On the Options menu, click Sound.
	
	Clear the Play Music check box.
	
	Use the Windows 95 CD Player application to control your music.
