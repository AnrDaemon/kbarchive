## Q264605: StarLancer: Cannot Fire Rear-Facing Weapons

	Article: Q264605
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft StarLancer, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	When you fly a ship in Microsoft StarLancer that is equipped with a rear-facing
	weapon, the weapon may move automatically, but you cannot fire the weapon.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	This behavior is by design.
