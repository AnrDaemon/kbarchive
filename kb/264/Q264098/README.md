## Q264098: Baseball 2001: Game Quits If You Simulate Entire Playoff Series

{% raw %}

	Article: Q264098
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 03-JUN-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Baseball 2001 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you attempt to simulate an entire playoff series from the Playoffs screen
	in Microsoft Baseball 2001, the game may quit immediately after the World Series
	champion is determined.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:
	
	- A Number Nine Visual Technology SR9 video adapter is installed in your
	  computer.
	
	- You attempt to simulate the entire playoff series from the Playoff Bracket
	  screen.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To work around this issue, simulate the entire playoff series one game at a time
	from the Schedule screen.
	
	To simulate the playoffs from the Schedule screen:
	
	1. Start Microsoft Baseball 2001.
	
	2. On the Home Plate screen, click New Playoffs.
	
	3. Click Sports Pages.
	
	4. On the Schedules tab, click a scheduled game on the calendar.
	
	5. Click Play.
	
	6. In the Heads Up! dialog box, click Simulate.
	
	The final score for the scheduled game that you simulated is displayed on the
	Schedules tab.
	
	Repeat these steps to simulate the entire playoff series one game at a time to
	determine the World Series champion.
