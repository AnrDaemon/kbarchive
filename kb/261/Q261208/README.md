## Q261208: StarLancer: Explanation of Missions Described on the Product Box

	Article: Q261208
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft StarLancer, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	This article provides an explanation of the following statement on the back of
	the Microsoft StarLancer product box:
	
	  Over 25 gripping missions ranging from an assault on Coalition space stations
	  built into an asteroid field to escorting ripper ships to steal valuable fuel
	  cells.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Over 25 missions are included in StarLancer, but you cannot play all of the
	missions in Single Player mode.
	
	The following table provides a breakdown of the number of missions that you can
	play in each mode of play that is available in StarLancer.
	
	+------------------------------------------------+
	| Number of missions | Mode of play              | 
	+------------------------------------------------+
	| 24                 | Single Player             | 
	+------------------------------------------------+
	| 3                  | Training                  | 
	+------------------------------------------------+
	| 6                  | Deathmatch (multi-player) | 
	+------------------------------------------------+
	
	NOTE: A total of 33 missions are included in StarLancer.
