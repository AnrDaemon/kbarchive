## Q248308: Fighter Ace II: Bombs Do Not Explode When You Drop Them

	Article: Q248308
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui igz kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 09-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you drop a bomb in Microsoft Fighter Ace, the bomb may not explode.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if you have not reached a sufficient altitude to allow
	the bomb to arm itself.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, increase your altitude before you release
	the bomb.
