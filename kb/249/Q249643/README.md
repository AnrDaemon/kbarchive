## Q249643: AoE II: Disband and Wheel Commands Are Not Available

	Article: Q249643
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbui aoe kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The Disband and Wheel commands are not available in Microsoft Age of Empires II:
	The Age of Kings, even though the Age of Kings manual discusses these commands
	on page 43.
	
	Additional query words: 2.00 msgame aoe2 aok
