## Q118336: Mac Encarta 94: Flag Flashes Momentarily When Being Copied

	Article: Q118336
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1994 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbbuglist
	Last Modified: 26-MAY-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Encarta 1994 The Complete Multimedia Encyclopedia 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you choose the copy button below the picture of a flag, the flag flashes
	momentarily. This occurs because Encarta is selecting, copying, and deselecting
	the flag. However, this behavior does not occur when copying any other kind of
	image.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Encarta 1994 for the Macintosh.
	We are researching this problem and will post new information here as it becomes
	available.
