## Q250855: MSB Bugs: How to Obtain the Product Identification Number

	Article: Q250855
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): WINDOWS|MACINTOS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbpolicy mskids kbimu
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores Bugs, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	If you contact Microsoft Product Support Services for technical support of
	Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores Bugs, you must provide the
	Product Identification Number (PID) for your product to the Customer Service
	representative.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	To obtain the PID for your product, use the appropriate method for your
	operating system.
	
	Microsoft Windows
	-----------------
	
	To obtain the PID when you run Magic School Bus series: Explores Bugs on a
	Windows-based computer, press ALT+SHIFT+A while you are running the program.
	
	Macintosh OS
	------------
	
	To obtain the PID when you run Magic School Bus series: Explores Bugs on a
	Macintosh computer, press COMMAND+SHIFT+A while you are running the program.
