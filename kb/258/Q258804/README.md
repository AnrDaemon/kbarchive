## Q258804: Streets 98: Error Message on a Blue Screen When You Run Program

	Article: Q258804
	Product(s): Microsoft Automap
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbdisplay kberrmsg kbimu
	Last Modified: 11-APR-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Expedia Streets 98 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you run Microsoft Expedia Streets 98, you may receive an error message on a
	blue screen.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if the following conditions are true:
	
	- You run Expedia Steets 98 on a Microsoft Windows NT-based computer.
	
	- An outdated video driver is installed on your computer.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To resolve this issue, contact the manufacturer of your video adapter to inquire
	about how to obtain and install the latest version of the video driver for your
	video adapter.
	
	For information about how to contact the manufacturer of your video adapter,
	click the appropriate article number in the following list to view the article
	in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:
	
	  Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K
	
	  Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P
	
	  Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z
