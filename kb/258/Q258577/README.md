## Q258577: MSB Solar: Sounds and Speech Are Cut Off Abruptly

	Article: Q258577
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbsound mskids kbimu
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Scholastic's Magic School Bus series: Explores the Solar System for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you run Microsoft Scholastic's Magic School Bus: Explores the Solar System,
	some sound effects and sentences spoken by the characters in the program may be
	cut off abruptly.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a Creative Labs SoundBlaster Live! sound card is
	installed in your computer.

	================

	companies that are independent of Microsoft. Microsoft makes no warranty,
	implied or otherwise, regarding the performance or reliability of these
	products.
