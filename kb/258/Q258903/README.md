## Q258903: Allegiance: Weapon Sounds Stutter After Ship Reloads Ammunition

	Article: Q258903
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbsound kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	After your ship reloads ammunition in Microsoft Allegiance, sound in the game
	may stutter when you fire your weapons.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if you hold down the fire button while your ship is
	reloading ammunition.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To prevent this issue from occurring, do not hold down the fire button while
	your ship is reloading ammunition.
	
	STATUS
	======
	
	Microsoft has confirmed that this is a problem in Microsoft Allegiance, version
	1.0.
