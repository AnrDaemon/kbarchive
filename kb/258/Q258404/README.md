## Q258404: Baseball 2001: Colors on the Screen Appear Blotchy or Distorted

	Article: Q258404
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
	Last Modified: 17-JUN-2000
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Baseball 2001 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	When you play Microsoft Baseball 2001, colors on the screen may appear blotchy
	or distorted.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	This behavior can occur if a video adapter based on the Chromatic Research Mpact
	2 3DVD chip set is installed in your computer.
	
	The Mpact 2 3DVD chip set does not support alpha blending.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	To work around this issue, disable 3D hardware acceleration in Baseball 2001:
	
	1. Start Baseball 2001.
	
	2. On the Home Plate screen, click Options.
	
	3. Click the Graphics tab.
	
	4. Under Mode, click Software.
	
	5. Click OK.
