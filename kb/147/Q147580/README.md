## Q147580: Member ID Appears Twice in Chat Member List

	Article: Q147580
	Product(s): The Microsoft Network
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.2,1.3,2.0,2.5
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): kbmsn
	Last Modified: 16-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- The Microsoft Network versions 1.2, 1.3, 2.0, 2.5 
	- the operating system: Microsoft Windows 95 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SYMPTOMS
	========
	
	Your member ID may appear twice in a chat member list. Your member ID may also
	remain in the member list after you close a chat room.
	
	CAUSE
	=====
	
	You may still be logged into the chat room, or the chat room may have been
	entered twice.
	
	RESOLUTION
	==========
	
	Close one instance of the chat room, or restart the computer and re-enter the
	chat room.
