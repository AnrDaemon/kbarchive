## Q138313: World of Flight: Symbols for Videos, Peelaways, Animations

	Article: Q138313
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft World of Flight for Windows, version 1.0 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The following is a list of symbols used for videos, animations, peelaway
	screens, and interactivities.
	
	- A blue piece of film symbolizes a Video clip.
	
	- A orange piece of film symbolizes an Animation.
	
	- A wrench or a pair of scissors symbolizes a Peelaway screen. The wrench
	  indicates a metal skin and scissors indicates fabric skin.
	
	- An orange chess piece (knight) symbolizes Interactivities.
	
	- A red plane symbolizes a topic.
