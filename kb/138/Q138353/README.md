## Q138353: Complete Basketball 95-96: No Daily Update Feature Available

	Article: Q138353
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1995-1996 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 13-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Complete NBA Basketball for Windows 1995-1996 edition 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	The Complete NBA Basketball Daily feature is not available in the 1995- 1996
	edition.
	
	MORE INFORMATION
	================
	
	The Basketball Daily is not included in the 1996 edition and the informative
	information about the Daily is not printed on the box.
	
	Complete Basketball, 1994-1995 did include the Daily feature. The back of the
	product box stated:
	
	  Basketball Daily is available in the United States and Canada only for $1.25
	  per call in the United States and $2.25 per call in Canada. VISA or
	  MasterCard required for Basketball Daily billing.
	
	Basketball Daily service concluded shortly after the 1994-1995 season and is no
	longer offered.
