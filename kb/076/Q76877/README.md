## Q76877: Blue Dots in Hammond Atlas Map of Australia

	Article: Q76877
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1991 edition
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 04-DEC-1999
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows 1991 edition 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	On the map of Australia in the Hammond Atlas, some cities are blue. These are
	the capitals of the subdivisions of the nation and include the offshore island
	state of Tasmania; the five mainland states of Queensland, New South Wales,
	Victoria, South Australia, and Western Australia; the Northern Territory; and
	the Australian Capital Territory, containing Canberra, the federal capital.
	
	The blue dots are not in the documented legend.
