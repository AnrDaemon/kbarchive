## Q76852: Cities Excluded from Encyclopedia Map of United States

{% raw %}

	Article: Q76852
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
	Version(s): 1991 edition; WINDOWS:
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 13-JUN-2001
	
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	The information in this article applies to:
	
	- Microsoft Bookshelf for Windows 1991 edition 
	- the operating system: Microsoft Windows 98 
	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	
	The pop-up map of the United States under the List of Maps in
	the Concise Columbia Encyclopedia has no city labels. There are
	red dots to indicate city locations, but no city labels.
	
	These city dots are only present in Bookshelf 1991 for Windows.
	
	Additional query words: USA cities wbk 1991 city dots
