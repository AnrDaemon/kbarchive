## Q50305: Plane Still Flies When Out of Fuel in World War I Ace

	Article: Q50305
	Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
	Version(s): 
	Operating System(s): 
	Keyword(s): 
	Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001
	
	SUMMARY
	=======
	
	When you run out of fuel in the World War I Ace mode, your plane continues to
	fly normally.
	
	Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
	becomes available.
