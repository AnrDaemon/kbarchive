Q132418: Dogs: Documented Content Errors

Article: Q132418
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 11-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Dogs for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains a list of the known content errors in Microsoft Dogs for
Windows, version 1.0.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Article Title                 Change/Addition
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Bearded Collie                Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Bulldog                       Change Country of Origin: Great Britain
                             Change Weight: 40-55 pounds

Bull Terrier                  Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Collie                        Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Cocker Spaniel:Owner's Tips   Change Last Sentence to: They may
                             respond by snapping or biting.

English Springer Spaniel      Change Country of Origin: Great Britain
                             Change Weight: 49-55 pounds

English Toy Spaniel           Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Field Spaniel                 Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Golden Retriever              Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Irish Setter                  Change Color: Chestnut Red

Jack Russell Terrier          Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Japanese Chin                 Change Height: to 9 inches

Labrador Retriever            Change Country of Origin: Great Britain
                             Change Height: 21.5 inches to 24.5 inches

Lhasa Apso                    Change Colors: All dog colors

Manchester Terrier            Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Old English Sheepdog          Change Weight: 66 pounds or more

Papillon                      Change Weight: up to 10 pounds

Pembroke Welsh Corgi          Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Pomeranian                    Change Height: to 11 inches

Pug                           Change Colors: Apricot, silver, or fawn
                             with black mask

Scottish Terrier              Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Shetland Sheepdog             Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Smooth Fox Terrier            Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Vizsla                        Add Other Names: Magyar Vizsla

Welsh Terrier                 Change Country of Origin: Great Britain

Yorkshire Terrier             Change Country of Origin: Great Britain
