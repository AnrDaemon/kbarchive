Q145801: Pregnancy and Child Care: Problems Connecting to WWW Sites

Article: Q145801
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): WINDOWS:95
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 01-JUN-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care, Preview on MSN 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care contains links to various World Wide Web
(WWW) sites. Sometimes, you are not able to connect to those sites.

RESOLUTION
==========

World Wide Web (WWW) sites that are listed in Microsoft Pregnancy and Child Care
are not managed or monitored by Microsoft. These sites may not be accessible for
one or more of the following reasons:

- The WWW address may not be accessible at all times. Try accessing the WWW
  link at a later time.

- The owner of the World Wide Web link may have moved or given up the WWW
  address. Use an Internet browser to check the address. Pregnancy and Child
  Care cannot search for the WWW address if the site has been moved.
