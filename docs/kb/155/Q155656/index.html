Q155656: Monster Truck Madness: Some Trucks Crash More Often than Other

Article: Q155656
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbfaq
Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001

1.0
WINDOWS
kbusage kbhowto kbreadme kbfaq

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Monster Truck Madness, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

To keep the competition exciting, the performance characteristics of the trucks
are similar, but with subtle differences. You will find that some trucks handle
better than others in some situations.

Here's a hint: Truck geometry makes a difference.
