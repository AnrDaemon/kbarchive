Q90354: Cinemania: Filters Display Four Stars Instead of Five

Article: Q90354
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1992 editions
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 05-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania 1992 editions 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

In the Movie Listings section of Cinemania, you are allowed to use Index Filters
to narrow the focus of your search. Within the Index Filters there is a Star
Rating category which is based on a 1 to 4 scale. The 1 to 4 rating is based on
"Leonard Maltin's Movie and Video Guide 1992" which contains capsule reviews of
over 19,000 movies.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Motion pictures in Cinemania which have a 5 star rating are in the Detailed
Review and are listings from "The Motion Picture Guide." Cinemania contains only
750 reviews from "The Motion Picture Guide" and are therefore not included in
filtering. Cinemania contains content from three sources; "Leonard Maltin's
Movie and Video Guide 1992," "The Motion Picture Guide," and "The Encyclopedia
of Film."

Additional query words: index filter stars
