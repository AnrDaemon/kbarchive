Q272545: Contacting Member Support for MSN Companion

Article: Q272545
Product(s): The Microsoft Network
Version(s): 1.0,2.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- MSN Companion, versions 1.0, 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

How to contacting Member Support for Web Companion

MORE INFORMATION
================

To contact MSN Companion support

1. On the Start page, click help to open the help center.

2. In the member services section click customer support and then click the link
  that best matches your concern:

   - e-mail to customer service

   - e-mail to technical support

   - e-mail to provide general feedback

   - phone support

      - technical support: (888) 386-6497

      - customer service: (888) 765-2411

   - report a problem with another member
