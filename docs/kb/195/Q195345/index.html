Q195345: My Personal Tutor: No Sound After You Sign In

Article: Q195345
Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbsound mskids kbimu
Last Modified: 30-NOV-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft My Personal Tutor: Sam's Hide and Seek Adventure, version 1.0 
- Microsoft My Personal Tutor: Sky's Space Station Voyage, version 1.0 
- Microsoft My Personal Tutor: Turru's Daring Sea Quest, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

After you sign in to one of the My Personal Tutor 1st & 2nd grade programs
listed earlier in this article, you may no longer hear any sound in the program.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

- A Crystal PnP sound card is installed in your computer.

- You are using an outdated sound driver for the Crystal PnP sound card.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest sound driver for the
Crystal PnP sound card from the following Cirrus Logic Web site:

  http://www.crystal.com
